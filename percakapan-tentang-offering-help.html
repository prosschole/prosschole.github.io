20. Tolong buat teks dialog offering help tentang covid 19 minimal 6 percakapan!!!


Jawaban:

In making a dialogue, especially with the topic of today's most common affairs, the covid-19. One surely has to include the impact of such issue to the characters in the dialogue. Moreover, the discussion about the issue itself should be included as well.

Penjelasan:

Here's the example of the dialogue on the phone:

Morten: Dude, how are you doing?

Hassem: Well, not so good, mate. This covid-19 really ruins the business.

M: Oh, so sorry to hear that. Have you closed your store for the past few weeks?

H: Well, not yet, I do still have some customers coming here.

M: Oh, I see, dude. I hope it's over soon.

H: We all hope so. But if things are still the same, I might have to close the store for a while.

M: Hmm, understandable, how about your workers?

H: I'll have to send them home temporarily. That's the hardest part.

M: I know. I see they've been with you for a long time.

H: True that, anyway, what about you, mate? does this outbreak affect your work too?

M: Definitely

H: But I see you still go to work, not work from home.

M: Yeah, I still go to the office twice a week, but there won't be any bonuses.

H: Oh, sorry about that.

M: I can manage it, but what's more worrying is my side projects.

H: Oh, which one?

M: My favorite one, the football.

H: what about it?

M: Well, what am I supposed to write when all matches are delayed? Oh, how I hate this plague so much!! It affects so many people.

H: Let's just pray may this catastrophe soon be gone. I can't imagine how our unfortunate fellows deal with it.

M: Yeah, you're right.
