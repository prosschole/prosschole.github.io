16. Dialog bahasa inggris baby shower


Baby shower is a little party ahead of the birth of the baby. These parties are so trend among young couples millennial. We recommend that when the baby shower be held?


Baby Shower is a small party with friends made familiar, friends and close relatives to the eve of the birth of the baby. Actually we have the baby shower event version of Indonesia, namely those events four monthly or monthly nujuh.


The purpose of the second event is relatively the same, namely to celebrate and pray for a pregnancy of the mother as well as welcome the arrival of the baby. Only difference is, the monthly nujuh four or more accentuated on the values of religious and ritual tradition. Meanwhile, the baby shower is the tour much more relaxed, inviting only those closest, which aims at “watering” the prospective mother and her baby with gifts from the invitation; could be a friend, family, friends, or neighbors.


So, the baby shower is a form of attention from those closest to the baby who will be born. Usually this little feast prepared by the companions as a form of support and a sense of pity for pregnant women. However, now the baby shower happens more often held or prepared by the candidate and the mother (father) of his own. Think of it as motivation before Labor Party. Therefore, it is usually the party ahead of the birth of this baby is done a few weeks approaching the day of birth.

26. apa yang menyebabkan jika mata tetap terbuka melihat ke arah shower dan shower di hidupkan?​


jika mata tetap terbuka melihat ke arah shower dan shower dihidupkan, maka mata akan menerima air dalam jumlah besar sehingga mata bisa terlalu basah

selain itu, air dalam shower bukan air minum sehingga di dalamnya bisa terdapat kotoran yang tidak bisa dilihat langsung sehingga bisa menyebabkan mata menjadi iritasi

biasanya kita menggunakan air dalam tanah untuk digunakan dalam kegiatan sehari - hari (tidak termasuk minum). karena berasal dari dalam tanah, wajar air masih terdapat kotoran, ditambah lagi dengan adanya pencemaran tanah oleh sampah anorganik seperti plastik dan lainnya

untuk membersihkan air tanah tersebut, digunakanlah penyaring air. tetapi tidak dapat menghilangkan kotoran secara sempurna sehingga hanya bersih saja, tidak dengan kandungannya sehingga jika terkena mata bisa menyebabkan iritasi
