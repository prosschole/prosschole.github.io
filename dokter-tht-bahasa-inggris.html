22. Deskripsi kan profesi dokter hewan dalam bahasa inggris dalam bahasa inggris


Descriptive Text

Descriptive text adalah teks yang bertujuan untuk mendeskripsikan sesuatu (manusia, hewan, tumbuhan dan benda) secara khusus atau spesifik.

Generic Structure dari Descriptive text adalah:

1. Identification: Berisi tentang penjelasan singkat dari apa yang akan di deskripsikan.

2. Description: Mendeskripsikan benda, hean, tumbuhan dan manusia secara spesifik atau khusus.

Language features dari Descriptive text adalah:

1. Menggunakan simple present tense

2. Menggunakan kata yang spesifik atau tidak terlalu umum

3. Menggunakan adjective untuk mendeskripsikan sesuatu

Pembahasan

My father's job

My name is Randa. I have one brother and one sister. My parents is Mr. Anton and Mrs. Yuliana. My father's job is a doctor. He works in a big Hospital in Semarang city. My father also serves patients in our house. It usually opens at 6 am to 9 am and 4 pm to 9 pm. Many patients come to my house to get treatment from my father. My father examines each patient carefully. I usually help my father as a receptionist every evening. I wish I can be a doctor too one day. I like to help people.

Semoga membantu. Terimakasih :)

25. macam macam dokter dalam bahasa inggris


Kategori soal: Bhs Inggris
Kelas: SMA
Pembahasan:

There are approximately more than 20 types of doctor in English. They are as follows:
1. Audiologist - a doctor who specializes in ear problem. (dokter telinga)
2. Allergist- a doctor who deals with patients with allergic issue. (dokter alergi)
3. Anesthesiologist -a doctor who handles the illness caused by anesthetic medicines. (dokter anestesi)
4. Cardiologist -a doctor who works on heart problem.(dokter penyakit dalam  terutama jantung)
5. Dentist -a doctor who specializes in problem with teeth. (dokter gigi)
6. Dermatologist - a doctor who deals with skin issues / diseases. (dokter kulit)
7. Endocrinologist - a doctor who focuses on glands, endocrine system. (dokter hormon)
8. Epidemiologist - a doctor who works on epidemic diseases and its prevention for contagion. (dokter ahli epidemiologi)
9. Gynecologist - a doctor who deals with female reproductive system and prenatal condition of mothers (ginekolog/ dokter kebidanan)
10.Immunologist - a doctor who specializes in the immune system. (dokter ahli immunologi)
11. Infectious Medicine Specialist - a doctor who focuses on treatment for infectious diseases (dokter spesialis penyakit menular)
12. Medical Geneticist - a doctor who specializes in diseases caused by genetics. (dokter spesialis penyakit turunan)
13. Microbiologist - a doctor who studies on viruses and bacteria in diseases.
(dokter ahli mikrobiologi)
14. Neurologist - a doctor who deals with brain issue (dokter spesialis otak)
15. Neurosurgeon - a doctor who operates the patients with brain problem (dokter bedah otak)
16. Oncologist - a doctor who focuses on the treatment for cancer and patients at-risk. (onkolog)
17. Orthopedic surgeon - a doctor who deals with operation on the skeletal system. (dokter bedah tulang)
18. ENT Specialist - a doctor who specializes on Ear, Nose and Throat problem (dokter THT)
19. Pediatrician - a doctor who focuses on kids diseases and general health problems (dokter anak)
20. Physiologist - a doctor who works on the states of human body. (dokter ahli faal)
21. Plastic surgeon - a doctor who deals with the plastic surgery. (dokter bedah plastik)
22. Psychiatrist - a doctor who specializes on human psychology (psikolog)
23. Radiologist - a doctor who focuses on the use of x-rays for medical treatment. (radiolog)
24. Urologist - a doctor who is an expertise in urology system (dokter kelamin)

Dokter spesialis THT adalah salah satu spesialisasi pada bidang kedokteran yang berfokus dan memiliki keahlian untuk mengobati masalah yang terjadi pada area telinga, hidung, dan tenggorokan. Tidak hanya itu, dokter spesialis THT juga memeriksa dan mengobati gangguan yang ada pada sekitar leher serta kepala.

Pengobatan Rumahan untuk Meredakan Sakit Tenggorokan

1. Istirahat yang cukup dengan tidur setidaknya delapan jam sehari.

Selain tidur cukup, kamu juga perlu mengistirahatkan suara.

2. Banyak minum cairan.

3. Pilih makanan dan minuman yang menenangkan, seperti sup kaldu, teh bebas kafein atau air madu hangat.
