In writing a descriptive text about an idol, which is basically describing about idol relying on your senses, though perhaps not all of them, you need to include several things in it such as:
- physical appearance of the idol (height, weight, face, hair etc)
- description of her/his scent of perfume on her/him or the odour if you imagine that you were close to her/him
- description of her/his voice while speaking, humming or the natural movement
- additional information about her/him like interest, education, family, place of origin and many more.
