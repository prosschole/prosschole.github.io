3. what is the purpose, generic structure, language featurers of discussion text.......


General structure of Discussion Text

– Issue : stating the issue which is to discussed
– Argument pro : presenting the point in in supporting the presented issue
– Argument cons : presenting other points which disagree to the supporting point
– Conclusion / recommendation : stating the writer’ recommendation of the discourse

Purpose of Discussion text

– To present two points of view about issue or problem.
– To present arguments from differing points of view about issue or problem..



Language feature of Discussion text

– Introducing category or generic participant
– Using thinking verb; feel, hope, believe, etc
– Using contrastive conjunction; however, on the other hand, but, in other side, although, etc
– Using modalities; must, should, could, may, etc
– Using adverbial of manner; deliberately, hopefully, etc
– Simple present

5. write generic structure (text structure) of news item text!


Generic structures of News Item text :

Main Event / Newsworthy EventBackground Event / ElaborationSource

Pembahasan

News item is a kind of text that has a purpose to inform readers / listeners about events of the day which are considered newsworthy or important.

Generic structures :

A. Main event / newsworthy event

=> It is the main information. It explains the summary of an important event happens.

B. Background event / elaboration

=> It explains the the background of an event in details. Who are involved, where does it happen and how does it happen.

C. Source

=> It consists of the source of the news in the form of interviewees, the witnesses' comment, the expert's opinion, and the statement of involved subjects in an event.

8. mention generic structure explanation text​


WHAT IS THE EXPLANATION TEXT'S GENERIC STRUCTURE?

________________________

Jawaban:

Generic Structure of the explanation text :

1. General statement

2. Explanation

Penjelasan:

EXPLANATION TEXT

Explanation text is a text which explains the process ( chronological ) of natural ( phenomenon ) or non natural beyond the human's life. Explanation text is related to How and Why the phenomenon happens.

So, Explanation text menjelaskan proses ( runtutan kejadian ) dari faktor-faktor fenomena alam atau yang bukan termasuk kejadian alam yang terlingkup dalam kehidupan manusia. Explanation text lebih cenderung berkaitan dengan bagaimana dan mengapa fenomena tersebut muncul.

GENERIC STRUCTURE

1. General statement

It is located at the beginning paragraph, it tells the phenomenon that has been issued.

Pada umumnya General statement memaparkan latar belakang dari kejadian -kejadian ( WHY ) yang dipermasalahkan secara global baik berupa fenomena ataupun bukan fenomena alam. Paragraf ini diletakkan di bagian pertama sebagai pembukaan paragraf.

2. Explanation

it tells the sequence process of how the phenomenon occurs.

Paragraf selanjutnya menjelaskan runtutan proses-proses ( HOW ) kejadian  tersebut timbul.

SOCIAL FUNCTION/ COMMUNICATIVE PURPOSE

To explain how or why the thing happens.

Untuk menjelaskan bagaimana atau mengapa sesuatu hal terjadi

LANGUAGE FEATURES ( UNSUR KEBAHASAAN )

1. Penggunaan  sequence connection  ( penghubung berurutan )

( first, second, then, ..etc )

2. Penggunaan  passive voice  ( lebih memfokusan tindakan aksi daripada pelaku-pelaku yang terlibat )

( ...is usually caused by.....)

3. Penggunaan  simple present tense ( struktur kalimat menggunakan simple present karena erumuskan kejadian berdasarkan fakta kebenaran secara umum terjadi )

( the water carries along objects .......... )

4. Penggunaan action verbs ( kata kerja verbal ( tindakan aksi )

( makes, live , appear , overflows..........)

5. Penggunaan Noun phrase ( berisi sekumpulan kata-kata yang  memodifikasi satu makna kata benda nya  )

( bad habits , ground surface , lower place, the natural flooding, and etc ......)

6. Penggunaan technical terms ( merujuk pada istilah-istilah ilmiah / teknis  )

( evaporation , condensation .......... )

13. generic structure dari procedure text


Procedure text is a text that explains or helps us how to make or use something. Its communicative purpose is to describe how something is made through a sequence of actions or steps.

Pembahasan

Generic structure of procedure text is

The goal (title)The ingredients, stating the materials needed to make somethingThe method, stating the steps to make the something

The goals of the recipe is stated as the title.

The ingredients section states the materials.

That the methode section states the steps.

That the structure to state the ingredients consist of the name of materials and amount of materials needed.

That the structure to state steps consist of process to make something.

Penjelasan:

There are now more than 33.7 million confirmed cases and over one million deaths.

The Asia Pacific Region has comparatively low COVID-19 figures today, partly because countries have built up the capacities and mechanisms for emergency response over the years guided by the Asia Pacific Strategy for Emerging Diseases and Public Health Emergencies, or APSED.

However, as the pandemic in the Asia Pacific Region is entering a “new phase” of the outbreak, governments and individuals need to take further actions to mitigate risk and minimize transmission while maintaining social and economic activities.

While several countries have managed to contain outbreaks early on, many are now tackling new surges. For some, these new clusters have been larger and more complex than their initial outbreaks.

Preventing COVID-19 transmission is contingent on public compliance and private sector cooperation to suppress human to human transmission. Countries of the Western Pacific have adopted new behaviors to prevent infection, such as mask-wearing, physical distancing, tele-working and hand hygiene as part of daily life. Now the challenge is to make these new behaviors part of our everyday habits. One key lesson learned from this pandemic is that clear, caring, inclusive and regular communication from authorities contributes to public trust in the government's response, which leads to improved understanding of individual responsibility and, subsequently, a greater willingness to adopt infection prevention practices as part of “the new normal”. Embedding these practices as part of our “new normal” can be a stepping stone to a “new future”, with benefits for other health issues, far beyond the response to COVID-19.

They looked and saw a crying baby who looked just like a little finger. “This child must be a gift from God. Thanks to God!” “We will call this child ‘Issumboshi’,” they said. They raised Issumboshi with much care, but Issumboshi never grew bigger. “Hey, Issumboshi, do you want to be eaten by a frog?” Issumboshi was always being bullied by the children of the village and often went home feeling unhappy. Grandmother would make some big rice balls and encourage him. “Eat a lot, and grow up quickly,” Grandmother said. become a respectable person. Then I will come back.” Grandfather and Grandmother were worried about him, but Issumboshi’s mind would not be changed. At once they began to prepare for his trip. Issumboshi sheathed a needle sword in a straw case, put on a cup for a sedge hat, and started out with a chopstick staff, in high spirits. “I’m going now,” Issumboshi said.
“Is he safe? With such a small body?” Grandfather and Grandmother asked as they saw him off.
Issumboshi went on the trip with a big wish in a small body.

At last Issumboshi reached the capital city and anchored under the bridge. Then he climbed up to the railing and viewed the town. “There is a fine palace over there. 24. Apa Generic Structure Dari Narrative Text


Orientation, complication, and resolution
narrative = teks yg berisi cerita atau dongeng . terdapat konflik didalamnya dan disertai penyelesaiannya .

generic structure = orientation - complication - resolution

orientation = pengenalan tokoh, tempat, dan waktu cerita
*complication = konflik yang terjadi dalam cerita
resolution = pemecahan konflik

*di beberapa teks biasanya terdapat lebih dari 1 complication, jadi dituliskan complication1 - resolution1, lalu complication2 - resolution2

generic structure = orientation - events - reorientation

events = kejadian kejadian yg terjadi
reorientation = pengenalan kembali

function = to entertain the reader



procedure = teks yg berisi langkah langkah untuk melakukan sesuatu . seperti cara memasak mi, memakai handphone, dll

generic structure = goal(tujuan) - materials(bahan2) - steps(cara2)

function = to inform the readers how to .. (untuk memberitahu para pembaca bagaimana cara* ...)*tergantung mau bikin teks ttg apa

25. apa Generic Structurenya review Text?


1. ORIENTATION

Orientation adalah sebuah pengenalan. Sebelum masuk ke dalam ulasan yang lebih mendalam, penulis melakukan sedikit cuap-cuap mengenai apa yang ingin diulas. Hal itu bisa dilakukan dengan cara menyebutkan nama produk, hal-hal yang akan dibahas, atau bahkan sedikit membandingkan dengan produk yang lainnya.

2. INTERPRETATION

Disinilah sebuah review detail terjadi. Penulis akan mengulas secara penuh mengenai bagian-bagian produk mulai dari cover hingga spesifikasi secara keseluruhan. Sebagai contoh, penulis sedang melakukan review terhadap Tab, maka dijelaskan mulai dari bahan pembuata cover tab, apa filosofi desain dari tab tersebut, seberapa besar kapasitas baterainya, bagaimana kualitas layarnya, apa saja aplikasi terbaru yang ditawarkan, serta berapa pixel dan seberapa baik jepretan kamera yang dihasilkan.

3. EVALUATION

Disini, akan dibahas mengenai evaluasi atau penilaian mengenai tab tersebut dari sudut pandang penulis. Sebagai contoh, penulis memberikan kritikan bahwa tab yang sedang diulas belum mampu diandalkan dalam pemakaian waktu jangka panjang karena kapasitas baterainya yang terlalu rendah. Kemudian, ada pula kritikan mengenai kualitas casing yang hanya terbuat dari plastik kualitas rendah sehingga sangat mudah rusak.

29. apa itu generic structure dari descriptive text


Generic structure:

*Identification
Identifies phenomenon to be described

*Description
Describe parts, qualities or characteristicsThe Generic Structure of Descriptive Text
Setiap mempelajari genres atau jenis teks, pasti tidak boleh melewatkan istilah generic structure. Istilah ini dipakai untuk menunjukkan bagaiaman penulis teks itu menyusun tulisannya sehingga pesan dan maksudnya lebih mudah tersampaikan kepada pembacanya. Berikut adalah generic structure teks descriptive bahasa Inggris.
1. Identification: Menidentifikasi phenomena atau benda atau object tertent yang mau dideskripsikan. Biasanya ini letaknya diawal paragraf.
2. Description : Menjelasakan bagaimana rupa dan ciri-ciri hal yang sedang dideskripsikan tersebut. Secara umum apa yang menjadi patokan penggambaran teks jenis ini adalah bagian-bagian objek (parts), kuwalits objek (qualities), atau cicir ciri da kebiasaanya (characteristics)
