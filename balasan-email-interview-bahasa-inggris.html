Surat Konfirmasi Pemesanan Hotel Bahasa Inggris adalah surat yang dikirimkan kepada calon pelanggan hotel atau restoran mengenai rencana booking pelanggan tersebut. Konfirmasi ini ditujukan untuk memastikan tentang spesifikasi atau detail pesanan sehingga tidak mengalami kesalahan karena adanya ketidaksepahaman. Selain itu, hal terpenting bagi pihak hotel pada khususnya, surat konfirmasi pemesanan atau booking hotel ini dimaksudkan untuk mengetahui perihal kepastian jadi atau tidaknya calon penginap datang menginap di hotel tersebut terutama jika model pembayarannya dilakukan secara cash.

I have some questions that will help me understand the significance and impact of the discoveries of my eminent person, Michael Faraday.

1. First off can you tell me about your field of expertise and how you were trained to do what you do.

2. Can you tell me a bit about what you do at your workplace that uses your specialized training.

3. Michael Faraday is famous for many history changing inventions but his first and most well known is discovering electromagnetic rotation or what we call the electric motor.  Can you describe to me how this was a significant discovery of that time.

4. Ten years after the motor discoveries, Michael Faraday discovered electromagnetic induction.  This discovery may sound not as simple or obvious as an electric motor so could you explain to me what this is and how we use it today.

Selly: Nice to meet you sir.

(Senang bertemu dengan anda pak.)

Agung: Are you prepared for the prospective employee meet-up?

(Apakah kamu siap untuk wawancara kerja?)

(Ya, saya siap pak.)

(Tidak pak. Saya adalah lulusan baru.)

Agung: Do you have any PC capacity? Could you talk different dialects?
