A : Hi! I just saw you entered my class, it seems like we're classmate. But it's my first time seeing your face. Are you perhaps a new student? Anyway, nice to meet you, my name is A. How about yours?

B : Oh, hey A. So it's your class too? I just got transfered here from my previous school, and yeah, I'm a new student here. Pleased to meet you, my name is B.

A : I hope we can get along as fellow classmates and friends! Oh, are you familiar with the school already? Do you want me to escort you sightseeing the entire school?

B : No, it's alright. I've memorized some places, and I'm sure that I will memorize the rest of it after a few weeks here.

A : Okay, so what can I help you with? Hmm.. I know lots of people here, you want me to introduce some of them?

B : Well, that helps me a lot. I'm quite a shy person, you see. If you had not spoke to me first then I will not befriend you like now.

A : I'm glad I greeted you first! You're a fun person to associate with, it's disappointing to have not befriended you.

B : Thanks for the compliment. You're quite an interesting person yourself, it's nice to befriend you.

C : Hey A, it seems like you've met the transferred student already.

B : I'm afraid this is our first meeting.

C : Well, I have heard of you from our homeroom teacher, but it is our first meeting. Let me introduce myself, my name is C. It's a pleasure to meet you.

B : The pleasure is all mine. My name is B, and I was just transferred from my previous school because of my parents' jobs. I hope this is the last time I got transferred though.

A : I hope so, too! I'm sure you're tired of going to different places with different kinds of people.

B : Now that you mention it, it is tiring.

A : Yeah, then let us continue the chat at recces!

Clara: Yes...

Fauzi: Want a drink? (Mau minuman?)

Clara: Ow, thank you. I was looking for this before. (Ow, makasih. Tadinya aku lagi nyari minum juga.)

Fauzi: I know. It's a huge party, anyway. (Aku tau. Ngomong-ngomong pesta ini besar juga.)

Clara: You're right. (Kamu benar.)

Fauzi: So, let's start with my name. I am Fauzi. (Jadi, mari mulai dengan namaku. Aku Fauzi.)

Clara: I'm Clara. Nice to meet you. (Aku Clara. Senang bertemu denganmu.)

Fauzi: It's beautiful to meet you. Not gonna lie. (Sebuah keindahan berjumpa denganmu. Beneran.)

Clara: Don't do that... (Jangan gitu...)

Fauzi: Hmm, What brings you here? (Hmm, apa yang membawa kamu kesini?)

Clara: Well, I have a friend. This is her friend's party. (Nah, aku ada teman. Ini pesta temannya.)

Fauzi: Impressive. I just snuck in here. (Mantap. Aku cuma nyelinap kesini.)

Clara: Wait, are you kidding? (Tunggu, kamu bercanda?)

Fauzi: No. I live around here. (Tidak. Aku tinggal sekitar sini.)

Clara: That's made sense. (Itu baru masuk akal.)
