Travel to Bajul Mati Beach during School Holidays

Already since a week ago my parents planned to go to the beach, considering that my sister and I were on a school holiday, as well as a gift because the day after yesterday my sister celebrated her birthday.

The clock still showed at 4:06 a.m., but my family and I were getting ready to go to Bajul Mati beach. We left this morning because the distance from the house to the beach was around 3 hours. To block our stomach, my mother has prepared my favorite egg sandwich.

Our journey took a car. In the car there is me, my sister, father and mother. My sister and I were prohibited from carrying cellphones by mothers and fathers to enjoy the trip and the holiday itself. To spend my time playing "guess names based on letters" with my sister who was only 2 years away from me.

The first step in this game is to determine the theme first, this time the theme we chose was animals. Next, we ask for help from the mother to mention the front letter of the animal name that we will guess. If the mother says the letter "M" then me and my sister compete quickly and compete a lot in mentioning the names of animals that have the prefix "M", such as monkeys, peacocks, pigeons etc.

After our animal themes began to change themes to fruit names, comic character names, country names, etc. Not infrequently the father suddenly joined in answering even though he was not the official participant of the game. I never thought if this simple game turned out to be so fun and entertaining.

It didn't feel like two hours had passed. We began to enter the hilly area. My sister and I began to pay close attention to the scenery. We were fascinated by the beautiful view of the rice fields below. Unfortunately I could only enjoy the scenery for a while, because after that I was dizzy and nauseous due to the winding road, up and down. Mother gave me eucalyptus oil. The aroma can really reduce the nausea that I experience.

Finally, our family arrived at Bajul Mati Beach. This beach is located in Malang Regency, East Java. We immediately headed to a small gazebo in several coastal locations. While enjoying the sea breeze, we eat together first. Our menu at that time was fried chicken and sausage. Indeed my mother's food tastes very good.

I can't stop being amazed at the beauty of this beach. Bajul Mati Beach is not too famous, so there are not many visitors who come. But the beach is still very clean which makes our family comfortable.

My sister and I couldn't wait to play, so we immediately ran towards the beach. We did not heed the words of the mother who told us to use sunblock first. It feels fresh when sea water hits our feet.

On the beach I found a long wooden branch. Finally I took and dragged it along the way. After walking for a while we arrived at the estuary, which is a meeting place for river water and sea water. We stopped and looked back, seen a long line in the sand from the wooden branches that I dragged from it. Somehow my sister and I felt the line was very cool. Next we decided to swim first in the estuary. Because the current is calm unlike in the sea.

We swam for about 15 minutes, before our parents called, told us to go to the waterfront. We all play water. Father invited us to sit cross-legged with his back to the sea. When the waves came we were dragged to the beach. It feels very exciting, because we don't see when the waves come. While the mother just played water on the beach and photographed us through her camera.

29. jelaskan perbedaan penggunaan dalam bahasa inggrisverb-ingverb-eddll :) soalnya dalam soal bahasa inggris kelemahan saya di pertanyaan semacam itu


verb ing itu adalah digunakan untuk kata kerja yang sedang kamu lagi saat ini atau sampai ini
verb ed itu digunakan untuk kata kerja yang sudah terjadi kalau verb + ing digunakan untuk kejadian saat ini atau sekarang
sedangkan verb +ed untuk kejadian yang sudah berlangsung (lampau)
