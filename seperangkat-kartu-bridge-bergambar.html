PELUANG KEJADIAN MAJEMUK

n(S) = 52 kartu

kartu bergambar orang ada 3 kartu (king, queen, Jack), masing masing ada 4 (sekop, hati, keriting, intan)

n(A) = 3 Ã— 4 = 12

peluang terambil kartu bergambar orang :

P(A) = n(A)/n(S)

P(A) = 12/52

P(A) = 3/13

peluang terambil kartu bukan bergambar orang :

P'(A) = 1 -P(A)

P'(A) = 1 -3/13

P'(A) = 13/13 -3/13

P'(A) = 10/13
