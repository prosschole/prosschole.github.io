1. please write some sentences showing obligation, prohibition , advice obligation - - - prohibition - - - advice / suggestion - - -


Jawaban:

Suggestion/Recommendation

The social function of Suggestion is to express what are should we/they/I do.

For examples :

• You should change your attitude.

• You should love your body.

• We should ask Mrs. Ariyani to open the seminar.

Keyword in suggestion is should

Respond :

• Okay

• Alright

• Of course

• Certainly

• Sure

Structure

1. S + should + V1 + (O) + (adv)

  You should study hard.

Example dialog :

Miss Sofi : "You'd better put your items into a backpack and handbag. You should love your body."

Edo : "You could be right. Thanks for your suggestion, Miss.

Miss Sofi : "My pleasure"

Obligation

The sosial function of obligation is to express what are must we/they/I do.

For examples :

• We must wear a uniform every day.

• You have to study hard.

• You has to do five prayers on time.

Keywords in obligation is must and has/have to

Structure

1. S + must + V1 + (O) + (adv)

  We must wear a uniform every day.

2. S + have to + V1 + (O) + (adv)

  You have to do five prayers on time.

Example paragraph :

 We must wear a uniform every day. From Monday to Thursday we must wear the batik shirt. The girls must wear a black skirt, and boys must wear a pair of black pants. On Friday we must wear the Scout uniform. We must wear proper shoes.

Prohibition

The social function of prohibition is to express what are must not we/they/I do.

For examples :

• We must not be late to school.

• We must not litter.

• You don't have to play truant the class.

• You don't has to skip the lesson.

• You don't break the rules

Keywords in prohibition is must not, don't have/don't has, and don't

Respond :

• Yes

• Ok

• Certainly

Structure

1. S + must not/musn't + V1 + ( O ) + (adv)

  We must not be late to school.

2. S + don't have/has to + V1 + ( O ) + (adv)

  You don't have to play truant the lesson.

3. S + don't + V1 +  (O) + (adv)

  You don't break the rules.

Example dialog :

Beni : "Udin, let's go out. Don't be lazy. Get up and have some exercise outside"

Udin : "Okay. Wait. Let me put on my shoes."

10. Buat kesimpulan tentang sugestion,obligation dan prohibition


Jawaban:

Suggestion : saran

kalimat yang berisi ekspresi yang memberi sugesti atau menyarankan orang lain untuk melakukan suatu hal.

example :

ShouldOught toHow about.. ?What about.. ?You had better..I suggest..Let's.. (e.g. let's go now)

2. Obligation : keharusan

tindakan atau ekspresi dalam bahasa Inggris yang menyatakan suatu hal yang harus dilakukan (keharusan/kewajiban).

example :

Has toHave toMustShould

3. Prohibition : larangan

tindakan atau ekspresi untuk memperingati atau melarang orang lain untuk melakukan suatu hal tertentu.

example :

Do not / don't..Please don't..Don't be..No.. (e.g. no smoking)Watch out!

KESIMPULANNYA :

Suggestion yaitu saran , Obligation yaitu suatu keharusan dan Prohibition adalah sebuah larangan

23. Prohibition and obligation catatan​


Jawaban:

Pengertian Obligation

Kewajiban adalah tindakan seseorang diperlukan untuk mengambil, baik legal maupun moral.

Expression of Obligation:

Present/ Future Form

Singular

- I’m supposed to….

- She’s supposed to….

- He’s supposed to….

- It’s supposed to….

Plural

- We’re supposed to…

- You’re supposed to…

- They’re supposed to…

Past Form

Singular

- I was supposed to….

- She was supposed to….

- He was supposed to….

- It was supposed to….

Plural

- We were supposed to….

- You were supposed to….

- They were supposed to...

Pengertian Prohibition

Prohibition adalah sebuah kalimat larangan atau peringatan bagi orang lain untuk tidak melakukan sesuatu.

Verbal Prohibition :

- Don't + Verb 1

- Don't touch me! (jangan sentuh aku!)

- Don't cry! (jangan menangis!)

- Don't come late! (jangan datang terlambat!)

- Don't step in the grass! (jangan menginjak rumput!)

Nominal Prohibition

- Don't be late! (jangan terlambat!)

- Don't be sad! (jangan bersedih!)

- Don't be lazy! (jangan malas!)

- Don't be a stupid person (jangan jadi orang yang bodoh!)

- Don't be so serious (jangan jadi terlalu serius!)

Prohibition 'No + Verb-Ing'

- No smoking! (dilarang merokok)

- No fishing (dilarang memancing)

- No littering (dilarang membuang sampah)

- No feeding (jangan memberi makan)

- No tipping (jangan memberikan uang tip)

Polite prohibition

- Please don't leave me alone (tolong jangan tinggalkan aku sendirian)

- Please don't go (tolong jangan pergi)

- Please stay with me (tolong tinggalah bersamaku)

- Please don't cry (tolong jangan menangis)

Berguna untuk menyatakan suatu yang harus dan penting untuk dilakukan. Biasanya obligation dijabarkan dengan must or have to.

2. Prohibition

Merupakan larangan untuk melakukan sesuatu, biasanya dijabarkan dengan mustn't, haven't to, don't.

3. Recomendation

Adalahh pernyataan yang memuat saran. Biasanya dijabarkan dalam bentuk Should, ought to, suggest (you/ he/ she)
