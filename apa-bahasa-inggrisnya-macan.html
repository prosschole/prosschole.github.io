9. buatlah kalimat deskriptif tentang macan dengan bahasa Indonesia dan Inggris_pelajaran bhs Inggris


Tiger

Tiger is considered to be the largest cat in the world. The scientific name of tiger is Panthera tigris. They are carnivorous mammals. Nowadays, tigers are in the list of endangered animals created by the International Union for Conservation of Nature (IUCN). It is recorded that the global population of tiger in the wild is about 3,062 to 3,948 individuals (the number has dropped around 100,000 compared to the beginning of the 20th century).

Tigers have four leg and a very long tail. They have some retractable sharp claws in their paws. They also have a muscular body. There are a mane-like fur around their neck and jaws. The most typical part of their body is their skin. They have a very unique dark vertical stripes pattern on their skin which make them easy to recognize even when they were still a baby. Most tigers have orange fur, but there are also golden and white tigers with golden and white fur. Most tigers can live up to 20 years or more. A full grown tiger can run at 49 to 65 km/h. Adult male tigers can reach 310 kg in weight while the adult female tigers can reach 170 kg.

Harimau

Harimau dianggap sebagai kucing terbesar di dunia. Nama ilmiah dari harimau adalah Panthera tigris. Mereka adalah mamalia pemakan daging. Saat ini, harimau ada dalam daftar hewan yang terancam punah yang dibuat oleh International Union for Conservation of Nature (IUCN). Dicatat bahwa populasi harimau di seluruh dunia adalah sekitar 3.062 hingga 3.948 ekor (angka itu telah menurun sekitar 100.000 dibandingkan dengan pada awal abad ke 20).

Harimau memiliki empat kaki dan sebuah ekor yang sangat panjang. Mereka memiliki beberapa kuku tajam yang bisa dikeluar masukkan di tapak kaki nya. Mereka juga memiliki tubuh yang berotot. Terdapat bulu bulu yang terlihat seperti tengkuk di sekitar leher dan rahang nya. Bagian yang paling khas dari tubuh mereka adalah kulitnya. Mereka memiliki pola garis vertikal hitam yang sangat unik di kulitnya yang membuat mereka mudah dikenali bahkan saat mereka masih bayi. Sebagian besar harimau memiliki bulu berwarna jingga tua, namun ada juga harimau emas dan putih dengan bulu emas dan putih. Sebagian besar harimau bisa hidup hingga usia 20 tahun atau lebih. Seekor harimau yang telah tumbuh sempurna dapat berlari pada kecepatan 49 hingga 65 km/jam. Harimau jantan dewasa bisa mencapai berat badan 310 kg sementara harimau betina dewasa bisa mencapai berat badan 170 kg.

Leopards are graceful and powerful big cats closely related to lions, tigers, and jaguars. They live in sub-Saharan Africa, northeast Africa, Central Asia, India, and China.

However, many of their populations are endangered, especially outside of Africa.

Most leopards are light colored with distinctive dark spots that are called rosettes, because they resemble the shape of a rose.

Black leopards, which appear to be almost solid in color because their spots are hard to distinguish, are commonly called black panthers.

Female leopards can give birth at any time of the year. They usually have two grayish cubs with barely visible spots.black in colour and is otherwise identical to the feline species to which it belongs. The only real exception to this is the Florida Panther found in the south east region of the USA, that is believed to be a subspecies of Cougar and is quite rarely dark brown in colour and tends to have more of a speckled appearance.

Unlike Leopards and Jaguars, the Panther has no spots on its long body or tail, but instead has a shiny coat of dark fur. Panthers have small heads with strong jaws and emerald green eyes, and tend to have hind legs that are both larger and slightly longer than those at the front. Being a member of the Big Cat family, the Panther is not only one of the largest felines in the world but it is also able to roar which is something that felines outside of this group are not able to do.

Male panthers are larger than female panthers. They weigh from 100 to 160 pounds; female panthers weigh from 70 to 100 pounds. Panthers vary in height at the shoulder from 24 to 28 inches and measure from 6 to 7.2 feet from nose to tip of the tail.

The leopard (Latin: Panthera pardus) is one of four big cats. This animal is also known as a tiger limb because of its ability to climb. At first, people thought that the leopard was a hybrid of singadan and tiger, so the name "leopard" appeared among early European researchers. Javan leopard (P. p. melas) is an identity fauna of West Java and includes endangered animals in Indonesia.

Large leopard, with a body length of one to two meters. This species generally has a yellowish brown fur with black spots. Black spots on the head are smaller. Female leopard is similar, and smaller than males.

Leopard distribution area is on the continent of Asia and Africa. The species was considered to have many species (more than 30 subspecies) found in all habitats, ranging from tropical forests, deserts, savanas, mountains and residential areas, but is now reduced to only nine after molecular testing.

Leopards are solitary animals, avoiding each other. This species is more active at night. Due to the high child mortality rate, females typically have one to two children, who live with their mothers to young tigers aged between one and a half to two years.

The Leopard is an opportunistic hunter, who uses every opportunity to get his prey. They eat almost any prey of any size. Its main prey consists of various lactating animals, rodents, fish, birds, monkeys and other animals found around its habitat.

In general, the Leopard avoids humans. But the less healthy tiger, hungry or injured so it can not hunt regular prey, can prey on humans. There was an incident about a male Leopard in Rudrapraya, having more than 125 souls, and a female Leopard called the "Panar Leopard" preyed over 400 in the early 20th century in India.

Some subspecies of the Leopard like the Beetle Tigers of Indonesiaterancam are extinct, but in general the Leopard is evaluated as Low Risk in the IUCN Red List.

29. deskripsi hewan macan dalam bahasa inggris beserta artinya​


Jawaban:

The leopard is one of four big cats.

Large leopard, with a body length between one to two meters. This species generally has brownish yellow fur with black spots. Black spots on the head are smaller. Female leopards are similar, and are smaller in size than males.

Leopards are loners, who avoid each other. This species is more active at night. Because of high child mortality, women usually have one to two children, who live with their mothers until young tigers are between one and a half to two years old.

Leopards are opportunistic hunters, who use every opportunity to get their prey. They eat almost all prey of various sizes. The main prey consists of various mammals, rodents, fish, birds, monkeys, and other animals located around their habitat.

Artinya :

Macan tutul adalah salah satu dari empat kucing besar.  

Macan tutul besar, dengan panjang tubuh antara satu hingga dua meter. Spesies ini umumnya memiliki bulu kuning kecoklatan dengan bintik-bintik hitam. Bintik hitam di kepala lebih kecil. Macan tutul betina mirip, dan ukurannya lebih kecil daripada jantan.

Macan tutul adalah penyendiri, yang saling menghindari. Spesies ini lebih aktif di malam hari. Karena tingginya angka kematian anak, wanita biasanya memiliki satu hingga dua anak, yang tinggal bersama ibu mereka sampai harimau muda berusia antara satu setengah hingga dua tahun.

Macan tutul adalah pemburu oportunistik, yang menggunakan setiap kesempatan untuk mendapatkan mangsa mereka. Mereka memakan hampir semua mangsa dari berbagai ukuran. Mangsa utama terdiri dari berbagai mamalia, hewan pengerat, ikan, burung, monyet, dan hewan lainnya yang terletak di sekitar habitatnya.
