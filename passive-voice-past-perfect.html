2. Soal dan jawaban passive voice past perfect


Kalimat aktif (active) adalah penulisan kalimat yang menempatkan pelaku kegiatan sebagai subjek kalimat. Secara umum ditulis dengan pola dasar berikut:

Subject + (Aux) + Verb + Object

Sebaliknya, kalimat pasif (passive) adalah penulisan kalimat yang menempatkan korban/penerima kegiatan sebagai subjek kalimat. Berdasarkan pola kalimat aktif di atas, maka rumusan kalimat pasif adalah sebagai berikut:

Object + (Aux) + to be + Past Participle (Verb3) + by Subject

Keterangan:

Aux = auxiliary verb (kata kerja bantu, bisa terdiri dari 1 atau lebih kata)to be = salah satu dari kata-kata berikut: is, am, are, was, were, been, being, be, to be

Rumusan di atas mengacu pada rumusan umum yang meliputi berbagai tenses dalam bahasa Inggris. Jadi, penggunaan to be harus disesuaikan dengan kata kerja bantu (auxiliary verb) yang digunakan juga.

Selain itu, untuk tenses dalam bentuk sederhana (simple), penulisan kalimat aktif seringkali tidak menggunakan kata kerja bantu (aux) sehingga bagian itu bisa diabaikan/dihilangkan pada kalimat pasif (passive voice) juga.


Pembahasan

Kalimat past perfect tense mengikuti rumusan berikut:

Subject + had + Verb3 + object

Kata "had" disini adalah kata kerja bantu (aux) untuk past perfect tense, sehingga penulisan kalimat pasifnya menjadi:

Object + had + been + Verb3 + (by subject)

Contoh soal:

1. Tuliskan kalimat pasif dari "John had completed the task before you arrived."

Jawaban: The task had been completed (by John) before you arrived.

Penjelasan: Dalam kalimat ini, penyebutan John sebagai pelaku kegiatan bisa ditambahkan dalam kalimat jika dianggap penting menurut situasinya.

6. Buatlah masing masing satu kalimat active voice dari1. present perfect continuous tense 2. past perfect continuous tense 3. future perfect tense 4. past future perfect tense 5. future perfect continuous tense 6. past future perfect continuous tense.lalu ubah kalimat ke passive voice ​


Jawaban:

1) Active and Passive Voice "Present Perfect Continuous Tense".

Rumus Active:

Subject + To be + Been + Verb(ing) + O/C.

Rumus Passive:

Subject + To be + Been + Being + V3.

Contoh kalimat:

Active Voice: Angelina has been practicing her English ability.

Passive Voice: Practicing English has been being ability by Angelina.

2) Active and Passive Voice "Past Perfect Continuous Tense".

Rumus Active:

Subject + To be + Been + Verb(ing) + O/C.

Rumus Passive:

Subject + To be + Been + Being + V3.

Contoh kalimat:

Active Voice: Sam was tired because he had been jogging.

Passive Voice: Jogging had been being tired by Sam.

3) Active and Passive Voice "Future Perfect Tense".

Rumus Active:

Subject + Will + Have+V3.

Rumus Passive:

Subject + Will + Have + Been + V3 + by...

Contoh kalimat:

Active Voice: She will have won the first prize.

Passive Voice: The first prize will have been won by her.

4) Active and Passive Voice "Past Future Perfect Tense".

Rumus Active:

Subject + Would/Should/Could/May/Might/ Must + Have + V3/Past Participile.

Rumus Passive:

Subject + Would + Have + Been + V3.

Contoh kalimat:

Active Voice:

I would have been eating dinner with my friends today. Angelina would have been gone to the salon after lunch. I would have been studying about martial arts.

Passive Voice:

Dinner would have been eaten today by my friends and meSalon would have been gone after lunch by Angelina.Martial arts would have been studied about by me.

5) Active and Passive Voice "Future Perfect Continuous Tense".

Rumus Active:

Subject + Will + To be + Been + Verb(ing) + O/C.

Subject + To be + Going to + To be + Been + Verb(ing) + O/C.

Rumus Passive:

Subject + Will + To be + Been + Being + V3.

Subject + To be + Going to + To be + Been + Being + V3.

Contoh kalimat:

Active Voice:

My friend will have been graduating from University of Indonesia by the end of this month.We will have been singing a few song at the party.I will have been listening a music tonight.Emily have been feeding the cat.

Passive Voice:

University of Indonesia will have been being graduated by the end of this month by my friend.A few song will have been being sung at the party by us. A music will have been being listened by me.The cat will have been being fed by Emily.

6) Active and Passive Voice "Past Future Perfect Continuous Tense".

Rumus Active:

Subject + Would + Have + Been + V1-ing/Present Participle.

Rumus Passive:

Subject + Would + Have + Been + Being + V3.

Contoh kalimat:

Active Voice:

I would have been driving the car.Angelina and her family would have been living at their new house if its construction had finished.We would have been leaving on vacation in Bali island.They would have been playing martial arts.I would have been sleeping to finish my homework.We would have been going to the cinemas tonight.She would have been eating health food because it is good for health.We would have been going to the museum.I would have been learning Japanese language.We would have been learning Thai language.We would have been going to martial arts.

Passive Voice:

The car would have been being driven by me.Their new house would have been being left if its construction had finished by Angelina and her family.Bali island would have been being left by us.Martial arts would have been being played by them.My homework would have been being slept by me.Health food would have been being eaten because it is good for health by her.The museum would have been being gone by us.Japanese language would have been being learnt by me.Thai language would have been being learnt by us.Martial arts would have been being gone by us.

Perlu diketahui bahwa terdapat 5 Non Convertible Tenses, artinya terdapat 5 bentuk kalimat yang tidak bisa dirubah ke dalam bentuk Passive Voice:

Present Perfect Continuous TensePast Perfect Continuous TenseFuture Perfect Continuous TenseFuture Continuous TenseFuture Perfect Tense

Kalimat aktif subjeknya aktif melakukan aksi dalam kalimat.

Kalimat pasif subjeknya pasif dikenai aksi dalam kalimat.

Active Voice:

1. Simple Present Tense:

(+) Subject + Verb-1 S/ES + Object

2. Present Continuous Tense:

(+) Subject + Tobe (is/am/are) + Verb-ing + Object

3. Present Perfect Tense:

(+) Subject + Have/Has + Verb-3 + Object

4. Simple Past Tense:

(+) Subject + Verb-2 + Object

5. Past Continuous Tense:

(+) Subject + Tobe (was/were) + Verb-ing + Object

6. Past Perfect Tense:

(+) Subject + Had + Verb-3 + Object

7. Simple Future Tense:

(+) Subject + Will + Verb-1 + Object

8. Future Continuous Tense:

(+) Subject + Will + Be + Verb-ing + Object

9. Future Perfect Tense:

(+) Subject + Will + Have + Verb-3 + Object

Passive Voice:

1. Passive Voice [Simple Present Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (is/am/are) + Verb3

2. Passive Voice [Present Continuous Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (is/am/are) + being + Verb3

3. Passive Voice [Present Perfect Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + have/has + been + Verb3

4. Passive Voice [Simple Past Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (was – were) + Verb 3

5. Passive Voice [Past Continuous Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (was – were) + Being + Verb3

6. Passive Voice [Past Perfect Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + had + been + Verb3

7. Passive Voice [Simple Future Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + will + be + Verb3

21. please find explanation about perfect, past, future, present perfect (passive voice)


Answer:


Here's the explanation of perfect, past, and future perfect tense including the passive form of each tense.


Present perfect tense

Present perfect tense is a tense which is used to indicate an action which has completely done at an unspecific time before now.

Sentence structure:

(+) S + has / have + V3 + O

(-) S + has / have + not + V3 + O

(?) has / have + S + V3 + O + ?

Example:

I have had breakfast


Past perfect tense

Past perfect tense is a tense which is used to indicate an action which had completely done before another action in the past.

Sentence structure:

(+) S + had + V3 + O

(-) S + had + not + V3 + O

(?) Had + S + V3 + O + ?

Example:

She left the class after she had submitted the assignment.


Future perfect tense

Future perfect tense is a tense which is used to indicate an action which will have completely done in the future.

Sentence structure:

(+) S + will + have + V3 + O

(-) S + will + not + have + V3 + O

(?) Will + S + have + V3 + O + ?

Example:

I will have cooked dinner by the time you arrive home.


-PASSIVE VOICE-

Passive voice is a kind of sentence whose subject is the receiver of the verb’s action.

The general sentence structure of passive voice is:

O + to be + V3 + by + S

(Actually, the structure will be written as S + to be + V3 + by + O or S + modal aux + to be + V3 + by + O but I write it in this version so you can see the difference between active and passive voice)


Here's the structure of present, past, and future perfect tense in passive form.

Present perfect tense

(+) O + has / have + been + V3 + by + S

(-) O + has / have + not + been + V3 + by + S

(?) has / have + O + been + V3 + by + S + ?

Example:

Active: I have bought a new pair of shoes

Passive: A new pair of shoes has been bought by me.


Past perfect tense

(+) O + had + been + V3 + by + S

(-) O + had + not + been + V3 + by + S

(?) Had + O + been + V3 + by + S + ?

Example:

Active: I had finished the assignment

Passive: The assignment had been finished by me


Future perfect tense

(+) O + will + have + been + V3 + by + S

(-) O + will + not + have + been + V3 + by + S

(?) will + O + have + been + V3 + by + S + ?

Example:

Active: She will have eaten breakfast at 7 am tomorrow

Passive: Breakfast will have been eaten by me at 7 am tomorrow

Kalimat passive (passive voice)

Passive voice merupakan suatu bentuk kalimat dimana subjek kalimat menerima aksi, bukan melakukan aksi. Tidak seperti active voice yang fokus terhadap pihak yang melakukan aksi (doer of action), bentuk ini lebih berfokus kepada pihak atau objek yang menerima hasil dari suatu aksi tersebut (receiver of action).

Kalimat aktif yang bisa dirubah menjadi kalimat pasif berbentuk kalimat aktif yang mengandung transitive verbs (kata kerja yang memerlukan objek langsung) seperti make, bring, buy, write dan sebagainya. Sebaliknya, kalimat pasif yang mengandung intransitive verbs (kata kerja yang tidak memerlukan objek langsung) seperti cry, swim, go, arrive, dan die tidak bisa diubah menjadi kalimat pasif. Tetapi, apabila intransitive verbs tersebut diikuti oleh preposition, ada kemungkinan kalimat tersebut bisa diubah menjadi kalimat pasif tetapi kalimat pasif yang terbentuk akan sangat kaku.

Rumus Kalimat Pasif ( Passive Voice)

Subject(object dari kalimat aktif) + to be ( disesuaikan dengan bentuk tensesnya + Verb 3 (past participle)

TENSES                                              RUMUS PASSIVE VOICE

Simple present                                subject + is/am/are + Verb 3

Present continuous                     subject + is/am/are + being + Verb 3

Present perfect                              subject + have/has been + Verb 3

Simple past                                     subject + was/were + Verb 3

Past continuous                         subject + was/were + being + Verb 3

Past perfect                                     subject + had been + Verb 3

Simple future                                   subject + will be + Verb 3

Be going to                              subject + is/am/are + going to + be + Verb 3

Future perfect                                subject + will have been + Verb 3

Modal                                              Subject + Modal + be + Verb 3

Note :  Penggunaan kalimat pasif dengan perfect continuous tenses biasanya jarang dilakukan karena akan menghasilkan struktur kalimat yang terlalu rumit dan sulit dipahami. Oleh karena itu, hal ini sebaiknya dihindari meskipun secara tata bahasa benar (grammatically correct).
