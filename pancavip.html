2. Buatlah 3 kalimat yang di dalamnya ada kata pancavip


Jawaban:

Contoh kalimat menggunakan Parts of Speech (PANCAVIP):

Pronoun (Kata Ganti): Hera very friendly, so many people like her. (Hera sangat ramah, sehingga banyak orang menyukainya).

Adjective (Kata SIfat): The hungry children ate burgers. (Anak-anak yang lapar memakan burger).

Noun (Kata Benda): I just finished reading a book. (Saya baru saja selesai membaca buku).

Conjunction (Kata Sambung): Frank doesn’t like wasting her money whereas he has a lot of money. (Frank tidak menghabiskan uangnya padahal dia memiliki banyak uang).

Adverb (Kata Keterangan): Robby entered the classroom quietly because he was late. (Robby masuk kelas diam-diam karena dia terlambat).

Verb (Kata Kerja): We study English twice a week. (Kami belajar bahasa Inggris dua kali dalam seminggu).

Interjection (Kata Seru): Insya Allah! I’ll be there. (Insya Allah, saya akan ada di sana).

Preposition (Kata Depan): My niece went to the park in the afternoon. (Keponakan saya pergi ke taman pada sore hari).

Penjelasan:1. Noun (Kata Benda)

Noun digunakan untuk nama orang, benda, hewan, tempat, dan ide/konsep.

Jenis-jenis noun:

Countable (dapat dihitung)

- Book, Elephant, Train, Money

Uncountable / Mass (tidak dapat dihitung)

- Love, Sand, Happiness, Pain

Common (Sesuatu yang umum)

- Country, City, Month, Year

Proper (Sesuatu yang spesifik)

- Malang, Surabaya, January, July

Concrete (Sesuatu yang berwujud nyata)

- Bag, Cake, Building, Car

Abstract (Menyatakan ide, konsep, keadaan, dan hal abstrak lainnya)

- Friendship, Time, Imagination

Collective (Merujuk sekelompok orang, hewan, atau benda)

- Deer, Family, Government

2. Pronoun (Kata Ganti)

Pronoun adalah kata yang digunakan untuk menggantikan noun.

Jenis-jenis pronoun:

Personal

- I, We, He, She

Demonstrative

- This, That, These, Those

Interrogative

- Who, Which, What, Whom, Whose

Indefinite

- Nothing, Someone, Anywhere, Everybody

Possessive

- Mine, Yours, Hers, His

Reciprocal

- Each other, One another

Relative

- Who, Which, Whom, Whose

Reflexive

- Myself, Yourself, Ourselves, Themselves

Intensive

- Himself, Herself, Ourselves, Themselves

3. Verb (Kata Kerja)

Verb adalah kata kerja yang digunakan untuk menunjukkan tindakan/keadaan.

Jenis-jenis verb:

Action Verb (Transitive) - Menunjukkan tindakan atau kepemilikan, dan dilanjutkan dengan direct object.

- Have, Give, Ride

Action Verb (Intransitive) - Menunjukkan tindakan, tidak diikuti dengan direct object.

- Arrive, Rise, Wait

Linking Verb - Penghubung subjek dan deskripsinya.

- Be, Taste, Remain

4. Adjective (Kata Sifat)

Adjective adalah kata sifat yang digunakan untuk memberi keterangan pada noun atau pronoun.

Contoh:

- Pretty, Sad, Faithful, Happiness

5. Adverb (Kata Keterangan)

Sebagai kata keterangan, fungsi adverb adalah untuk memberikan keterangan tambahan pada verb, adjective, atau adverb itu sendiri.

Jenis-jenis adverb :

Adverb of Manner

- Well, Softly, Quickly

Adverb of Degree

- Just, Almost, So

Adverb of Frequency

- Usually, Always, Rarely

Adverb of Place

- In, Above, Below

Adverb of Time

- Annually, Daily, Yesterday

6. Preposition (Kata Depan)

Fungsi preposition adalah untuk menunjukkan hubungan antara noun dan kata-kata lainnya dalam sebuah kalimat.

Contoh Preposition :

- In, On, At, Through, With, By, Of

7. Conjunction (Kata Hubung)

Conjunction digunakan untuk menghubungkan dua kata, frasa, klausa hingga kalimat.

Jenis-jenis conjuction:

Coordinating Conjuction - Menghubungan 2 bentuk gramatikal yang sama, misalnya kata dengan kata.

- For, And, Nor, But, Or, Yet, So

Subordinating Conjuction - Menghubungkan induk kalimat dan anak kalimat.

- After, Since, Although

Correlative Conjuction - Kata hubung yang berpasangan.

- Not only....but also, Either...or

8. Interjection (Kata Seru)

Jenis kata yang satu ini biasanya digunakan untuk mengungkapkan emosi.

Contoh Interjection :

- Oops, Whoa, Oh no!, Oh Yeah!

The word "Grasshopper" is in bold. The Grasshopper is in the second sentence of the story. Here is the story with the word in bold:

"It was summertime and the Ants were hard at work, storing food for the winter. The Grasshopper saw them toiling away and asked why they were doing it. "Don't you know?" said one of the Ants. "The winter is coming and we need to store food so that we can survive." The Grasshopper laughed and said, "I don't need to worry about the winter. I can sing and dance all day and I'll be just fine." But when the cold weather came, the Grasshopper was hungry and cold and had to beg the Ants for food. They gave him some, but said, "You should have worked with us in the summer and then you would have had enough to eat." Moral massages: This story teaches us that we should work hard and not be lazy because if we are lazy, we will not have anything to eat when winter comes.

Explanation:

In the first sentence, the story introduces the setting and the Ants, who are hard at work storing food for the winter. In the second sentence, the Grasshopper is introduced as the character who sees the Ants working and asks them why they are doing it. This is why the word "Grasshopper" is in the second sentence of the story.
