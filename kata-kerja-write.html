Jawaban:

Simple Past

I bought a new book yesterdayShe wrote a letter last weekMy mom made a cake this afternoonHe read a magazine five minutes agoMy sister drew her favorite character last night

Present Continuous

I'm buying a new book nowShe is writing a letter at the momentMy mom is making a cake right nowHe is still reading a magazineMy sister is drawing her favorite character now

Penjelasan:

Simple Past

Digunakan untuk menunjukkan suatu aksi yang sudah selesai di masa lampau. Biasanya ditandai dengan kata kerja bentuk kedua dan kata sinyal lampau seperti yesterday, last year, ago, dll. Polanya adalah:

S + V2

Contoh:

⤷ I bought a new book yesterday

Aku membeli buku baru kemarinBuy (v1) ➔ bought (v2) ➔ bought (v3)

Present Continuous

Digunakan untuk menunjukkan suatu aksi yang sedang berlangsung. Ditandai dengan kata kerja berakhiran -ing dan kata sinyal right now, now, at the moment, still, dll. Polanya adalah:

S + is/am/are + V-ing

Contoh:

⤷ I + am + buying a new book now

Aku sedang membeli buku sekarangBuy +  -ing ➔ buying

Note:

Gunakan am untuk subjek IGunakan is untuk subjek he, she, itGunakan are untuk subjek you, we, they

Past Continuous Tense

Past Continuous Tense adalah bentuk kalimat yang menyatakan kegiatan/kejadian yang berlangsung di masa lampau (Past).

Formula:

(+) Sub + was/were + V1-ing

(-) Sub + was/were + not + V1-ing

(?) Was/Were + Sub + V1-ing + ?

Question:

buat kalimat past continuous dari kata kerja join,write,dan sew

1.

Join

(+) I was joining choir this morning.

(-) I was not joining choir this morning.

(?) Was I joining choir this morning?

2.

Write

(+) She was writing a letter yesterday.

(-) She was not writing a letter yesterday.

(?) Was she writing a letter yesterday?

3.

Sew

(+) Adam's mother was sewing his clothes last night.

(-) Adam's mother was not sewing his clothes last night.

(?) Was Adam's mother sewing his clothes last night?

27. Kalimat simple present tense dri kata kerja write


Penjelasan:

Simple Present Tense adalah bentuk tense yang digunakan untuk menjelaskan sesuatu yang terjadi saat ini (present) pada suatu spesifik waktu. Karena terjadi pada waktu sekarang, maka pasti menggunakan kata kerja pertama (verb 1) dalam susunan kalimatnya.

Rumus Simple Present Tense

<=> (+) S + to be + Verb1 + O/C

<=> (-) S + do/does + not + Verb1 + O/C

<=> (?) Do/does + S + Verb1 + O/C?

Yes, S + do/does

No, S + do/does + not

Contoh kalimat:

(+) Rani is Write in the paper

(-) Rani doesn't write in the paper

(?) Doed Rani write in the paper?

Yes, She does

No, She doesn't
