7. turunan dari y=ln(3x-25)^½


y = ln (3x - 25)^1/2

Misal u = (3x - 25)^1/2
du/dx = 1/2 (3x - 25)^-1/2 . 3
du/dx = 3/2 . 1/(3x - 25)
du/dx = 3 / 2√(3x - 25)

y = ln u => dy/du = 1/u = 1/(3x - 25)^1/2 = 1/√(3x - 25)

dy/dx = dy/du . du/dx
dy/dx = 1/√(3x - 25) . 3/(2√(3x - 25))
= 3/2(3x - 25)
= 3/(6x - 50)

17. tentukan turunan kedua di ln (x+4)


Supaat Mengajar Turunan Fungsi Logaritma Natural dengan Chain Rules

[tex] \begin{aligned}\tfrac{d}{dx}\ln(x+4)&=\frac{1}{x+4}\cdot\tfrac{d}{dx}(x+4)\\&=\frac{1}{x+4}\cdot1\\&=\frac{1}{x+4}\\ \tfrac{d}{dx}\left(\tfrac{d}{dx}\ln(x+4)\right)&=\tfrac{d}{dx}\frac{1}{x+4}\\ \tfrac{d^2}{dx^2}\ln(x+4)&=\tfrac{d}{dx}(x+4)^{-1}\\&=-1\cdot(x+4)^{-2}\cdot\tfrac{d}{dx}(x+4)\\&=-\frac{1}{(x+4)^2}\cdot1\\&=-\frac{1}{(x+4)^2}\end{aligned}[/tex]

21. pembuktian turunan dari ln x = 1/x


Gunakan definisi diferensial.

[tex]$\begin{align}\lim_{h \to 0}\frac{\ln|x+h|-\ln|x|}{h}&=\lim_{h \to 0}\frac{\ln|\frac{x+h}{x}|}{h} \\ &=\lim_{h \to 0}\ln|\frac{x+h}{x}|^{\frac{1}{h}} \\ &=\lim_{h \to 0}\ln|\frac{x+h}{x}|^{\frac{x}{h}\times\frac{1}{x}} \\ &=\lim_{h \to 0}(\ln|\frac{x+h}{x}|^{\frac{x}{h}})^{\frac{1}{x}} \\ &=\lim_{h \to 0}\frac{1}{x}\ln|\frac{x+h}{x}|^{\frac{x}{h}} \\ &=\lim_{h \to 0}\frac{1}{x}\ln|1+\frac{h}{x}|^{\frac{x}{h}}\end{align}[/tex]

jika nilai h dimasukan mendekati 0, maka h/x nilainya akan sangat kecil sekali, sehingga bisa kita anggap menjadi 1/n

sedangkan x/h nilainya akan sangat besar sekali, hingga nilainya mendekati n, sehingga :

[tex]$\begin{align}\lim_{h \to 0}\frac{\ln|x+h|-\ln|x|}{h}&=\lim_{h \to 0}\frac{1}{x}\ln|1+\frac{1}{n}|^{n}\end{align}[/tex]

Dengan deret maclaurin, kita tau bahwa :

[tex]$\begin{align}\lim_{n \to \infty}(1+\frac{1}{n})^n&=e\end{align}[/tex]

maka,

[tex]$\begin{align}\lim_{h \to 0}\frac{1}{x}\ln|1+\frac{1}{n}|^n&=\lim_{h \to 0}\frac{1}{x}\ln e \\ &=\lim_{h \to 0}\frac{1}{x} \\ &=\frac{1}{x} \\ &Q.E.D.\end{align}[/tex]

27. turunan dari y=ln(√x) dan y=ln(x^2-4)​


Jawab:

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

gunakan aturan rantai

[tex]y=ln\sqrt{x}\\\\misal~\\\\u=\sqrt{x}~\to~\frac{du}{dx}=\frac{1}{2\sqrt{x}}\\\\y=lnu~\to~\frac{dy}{du}=\frac{1}{u}\\\\\\maka\\\\\frac{dy}{dx}=\frac{dy}{du}.\frac{du}{dx}\\\\\frac{dy}{dx}=\frac{1}{u}.\frac{1}{2\sqrt{x}}\\\\\frac{dy}{dx}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{x}}.\frac{1}{2\sqrt{x}}\\\\\frac{dy}{dx}=\frac{1}{2x}[/tex]

[tex]y=ln(x^2-4)\\\\misal~\\\\u=x^2-4~\to~\frac{du}{dx}=2x\\\\y=lnu~\to~\frac{dy}{du}=\frac{1}{u}\\\\\\maka\\\\\frac{dy}{dx}=\frac{dy}{du}.\frac{du}{dx}\\\\\frac{dy}{dx}=\frac{1}{u}.2x\\\\\frac{dy}{dx}=\frac{1}{x^2-4}.2x\\\\\frac{dy}{dx}=\frac{2x}{x^2-4}[/tex]

Jawab:

[tex]\displaystyle y'=\frac1{2x}\\\\y'=\frac{2x}{x^2-4}[/tex]

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

[tex]\displaystyle \text{a.}\\y=\ln\left(\sqrt x\right)\\y=\ln x^\frac12\\y=\frac12\ln x\\y'=\frac12\cdot\frac1x\\\boxed{\boxed{y'=\frac1{2x}}}[/tex]

[tex]\displaystyle \text{b.}\\y=\ln\left(x^2-4\right)\\y=\ln\left[(x-2)(x+2)\right]\\y=\ln(x-2)+\ln(x+2)\\y'=\frac1{x-2}+\frac1{x+2}\\y'=\frac{x+2+x-2}{x^2-4}\\\boxed{\boxed{y'=\frac{2x}{x^2-4}}}[/tex]
