14. apa bahasa inggris hari pahlawan?


Bahasa Inggris dari Hari Pahlawan adalah HEROES' DAY.

Pembahasan

Apostrophe adalah tanda baca yang digunakan dalam kalimat bahasa Inggris, untuk contraction (penyingkatan), possessive form (kepemilikan), dan plural (menjamakkan).

Pada penulisan HEROES' DAY, apostrophe ditulis setelah kata benda karena jamak.

Nama hari nasional lainnya dalam bahasa Inggris:

Hari kemerdekaan: independence dayHari kartini: kartini dayHari pendidikan: education daySumpah pemuda: youth pledge dayHari buruh; Labour dayHari batik: Batik dayHari TNI: armed forces day

November 10, 1945 there was a battle in Surabaya which was a major battle between the Indonesian army and British troops.

This battle was the first war between Indonesian troops and foreign troops after the Proclamation of Indonesian Independence and one of the biggest and toughest battles in the history of the Indonesian National Revolution which became a national symbol for Indonesia's resistance to colonialism.

After the ceasefire between the Indonesian and British soldiers was signed on October 29, 1945, the situation gradually subsided. Even so, there were still clashesThese clashes culminated with the killing of Brigadier General Mallaby (Leader of the British Army for East Java) on October 30, 1945.

The death of General Mallaby caused the British to be angry with the Indonesian side and resulted in a decision to replace Mallaby namely Major General Eric Carden Robert Mansergh issued an Ultimatum of 10 November 1945 which asked the Indonesian side to surrender weapons and stop resistance to the AFNEI army and the NICA administration and threats to storm the city of Surabaya from land, sea and air if Indonesians disobey British orders.
