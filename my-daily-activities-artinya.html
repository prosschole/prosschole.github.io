2. my daily activities menggunakan ordinal number dan artinya​


Jawaban:

1. i eat = aku makan

2.i take a bath = aku mandi

3.i play = aku bermain

4.i study = aku belajar

Jawaban:

First, I usually wake up in the morning at 5 a.m. and then make my bed up.

Second, I take a bath at about 5.30 a.m. It usually takes 8-10 minutes.

Third, at 5.40 I wear my clothes like uniform, trousers, etc.

Fourth, I sit in the dining room to have breakfast. The meal is prepared by my mom.

Fifth, I finish my breakfast at about 6 a.m. Then I get my shoes in the shoe rack. After wearing the shoes I go to school by motorcycle.

Sixth, I arrive at school about 6.30 because my school is quite distant from my home.

Seventh, My class starts from 07.00 a.m. till 01.30 p.m. The break times are 09.00 a.m. till 09.30 a.m. and 11.30 p.m. till 12.30 a.m.

Eighth, I back to home about at 01.30 a.m. The trip takes 30 minutes to home.

Ninth, Arrive at home at 02.00 a.m. And I straight out put my uniform off and then I take a nap for a while. I don't have lunch at home because I had lunch already in the school at break time. Take a nap usually takes 1-2 hours.

Tenth, I do the second bath at 4 p.m. and commit my homework or playing game. Both of it takes 2 hours.

Eleventh, I have dinner after that with my family.

Twelfth, Henceforth I continue do my homework or playing game. I usually watching tv or listening to music on my phone as my homework is done by.

Thirdteenth, I go to bed about at 9 p.m. I sometimes wash my legs or brush my teeth if i don't forget to do it.

Penjelasan:

Ordinal number artinya kata ganti angka yang biasanya disebut "pertama, kedua, dst" jika dalam Bahasa Indonesia.

Terjemahan Daily Activity di Atas

Pertama, aku biasanya bangun pukul 5 di pagi Hari kemudian menata tempat tidurku.

Kedua, aku mandi sekitar pukul setengan 6. Biasanya memerlukan waktu sekitar 8 sampai 10 menit untuk mandi.

Ketiga, pukul 5.40 aku memaka pakaian-pakaianku seperti seragam, celana panjang, dll.

Keempat, aku duduk di ruang makan untuk sarapan. Makanannya disiapkan oleh ibuku.

Kelima, aku selesai makan sekitar pukul 6. Lalu aku mengambil sepatuku yang ada di rak sepatu. Setelah memaka sepatu aku berangkat ke sekolah dengan menggunakan sepeda motor.

Keenam, aku sampai di sekolah sekitar pukul 6.30 karena sekolahku cukup jauh dari rumah.

Ketujuh, kelasku mulai dari pukul 7 sampai 1.30. Waktu istirahat mulai pukul 9 sampai 9.30 dan 11.30 sampai 12.30.

Kedelapan, aku pulang sekitar pukul 1.30. Perjalanan memerlukan waktu 30 menit ke rumah..

Kesembilan, tiba di rumah pukul 2 am. Dan aku langsung melepas seragamku kemudian tidur siang sebentar. Aku tidak makan siang di rumah karena aku telah makan di sekolah saat waktu istirahat. Tidur siangku memerlukan waktu biasanya sekitar 1-2 jam.

Kesepuluh, aku melakukan mandi yang kedua pukul 4 pm. Dan mengerjakan Pr atau bermain game. Keduanya memerlukan waktu 2 jam.

Kesebelas, Aku makan malam setelah itu dengan keluargaku.

Kedua belas, Selanjutnya aku melanjutkan mengerjakan tugas rumah/pr atau bermain game. Aku biasanya menonton televisi atau mendengarkan musik setelah tugas rumahku telah diselesaikan.

Ketiga belas, aku tidur sekitar pukul 9. Aku kadang-kadang mencuci kakiku atau menggosok gigi ketika aku tidak lupa melakukannya.

First of all, I do my bed until it is very tidy. After that, I sweep the floor. I love to see my room neat and clean. When I still feel sleepy, I walk around my house. Also, I do some physical movements to stretch my body.

Then, I prepare some books based on schedule. I do not want to leave any book at home. I wash my face, brush my teeth, then have a shower. Finally, I eat breakfast after wearing my school uniform. Before going to school I hug my parents. I enjoy learning time at school. I like to play with my friends too.

6. "My daily activities during the holiday were reading novels and comics" atau "My daily activities during the holiday were read novels and comics"


"My daily activities during the holiday were reading novels and comics" atau "My daily activities during the holiday were read novels and comics"

Yg benar yg mana?

Jelaslah yg benar yg "My daily activities during the holiday were read novels and comics" karena itu dikerjakan setiap hari/selama liburan. Klw dikerjakan setiap hari namanya simple present tense/waktu hari ini sederhana. Klw yg "My daily activities during the holiday were reading novels and comics", pake ing. itu namanya continous tense, waktu yg sedang berlangsung/now. jadi, yg benar adalah yg bawah

10. either my father or my uncle ........ batik shirt in their daily activities


entah ayah saya atau paman saya ........ baju batik dalam kegiatan sehari-hari mereka

either my father or my uncle wears batik shirt in their daily activities.

Penjelasan singkat: either...or.. adalah salah satu paired conjunction (kata hubung yang memiliki pasangan).Ada 2 paired conjunction, Either.. or.. dan Neither...or... .

Either my father or my uncle wears batik shirt in ther daily activities.

Artinya: Bapak saya atau Paman saya memakai baju batik di dalam aktivitas sehari-hari. Makna dari kalimat ini adalah, kalau bukan Paman saya yang memakai baju batik maka Bapak saya lah yang memakai baju batik di dalam aktivitasnya sehari-hari.

Kata wear (memakai) bisa diubah sesuai kalimat. Semoga membantu
