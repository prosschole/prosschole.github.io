16. Bagaimana Cara membuat jus tomat dengan menggunakan bahasa inggris beserta artinya


Homemade Tomato Juice Recipe

Prep time: 15 minutes
Cook time: 30 minutes
Yield: Makes about 1 quart

Some tomatoes are sweeter than others, depending on their ripeness and the variety of tomato. Use the ripest tomatoes you can. Added sugar will balance the natural acidity of the tomatoes, use more or less to taste. Tabasco hot sauce is also to taste, depending on your desired level of spiciness.

INGREDIENTS

3 pounds very ripe garden tomatoes, roughly chopped
1 1/4 cups chopped celery with leaves
1/3 cup chopped onion
2 Tbsp sugar (to taste)
1 teaspoon salt
Pinch black pepper
A couple shakes of Tabasco sauce, about 6-8 drops (to taste)

METHOD

1 Put all ingredients into a large non-reactive pot (use stainless steel, not aluminum). Bring to a simmer and cook, uncovered, until mixture is completely soupy, about 25 minutes.

2 Force mixture through a sieve. Cool completely.



*********Meaning**********
RESEP JUS TOMAT HOMEMADE (BIKINAN SENDIRI)

Waktu persiapan: 15 menit
Waktu memasak: 30 menit
Hasil: Menghasilkan sekitar 1 liter

Beberapa tomat lebih manis dari yang lain, tergantung pada kematangannya dan berbagai tomat. Gunakan tomat paling matang yang anda bisa. Gula yang ditambahkan akan menyeimbangkan keasaman alami tomat, gunakan lebih banyak atau lebih sedikit secukupnya. Tabasco hot sauce juga sesuai selera, tergantung pada tingkat kepedasan yang anda inginkan.

BAHAN

3 pon tomat kebun yang sangat matang, dicincang kasar
1 1/4 cangkir seledri cincang dengan daun
1/3 cangkir bawang cincang
2 sendok makan gula (secukupnya)
1 sendok teh garam
Lumuran lada hitam
Beberapa kocok saus Tabasco, sekitar 6-8 tetes (secukupnya)

METODE

1 Masukkan semua bahan ke dalam panci besar non-reaktif (gunakan baja tahan karat, bukan aluminium). Didihkan dan masak, terbuka, sampai campuran benar-benar pekat, sekitar 25 menit.

2 Saring campuran melalui saringan. Dinginkan sepenuhnya.

There are many different varieties of tomatoes. There are yellow, orange, green, and red tomato. But most of them are red. An average common tomato weights is 100 grams (400onz) and their shape is round and vary in size from a small cherry to handful size.

Tomatoes are from Mesoamerica which used by Aztecs and other people in their cooking. They are brought by Spanish sailor to many places in the world.

100 grams of tomatoes have some nutritions. They 22% vitamin C and 16% vitamin A. They also contain many Lycopene. Lycopene is an antioxidant which fights free radicals and prevent many types of cancer.

Tomatoes can be eaten in many ways. Tomatoes can be made for salads and processed into ketchup or tomato soup. They also can be made for juice. In Italia, tomatoes are special ingredient for pizza and they are commonly used in pasta sauce. They are also used in Gazpacho (Spanish cuisine).
