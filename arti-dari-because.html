9. Q. englandrule:• jelaskan ( penggunaanya, artinya )• berikan arti• jangan ngasal• jangan coppy / paste​• gunakan because / because of


Jawaban:

1. English: I didn't ride the roller coaster Because I was scared.

Indonesia: Saya tidak menaiki roller coaster nya Karena saya takut.

2. English: my parents came to school Because it was parents day.

Indonesia: orang tuaku datang ke sekolah Karena hari orang tua.

3. English:it was very cold today Because of a snowstorm that hit the city.

Indonesia : cuaca hari ini sangat dingin Dikarenakan badai salju yang menabrak kota.

4. English: parents day was canceled Because of heavy rain.

Indonesia: hari orang tua dibatalkan Dikarenakan hujan deras.

5. English: Soo-jin was mad Because she has to do a lot of work.

Indonesia: Soo-jin sangat marah Karena dia harus melakukan banyak pekerjaan.

Penjelasan:

- Penggunaan kata "Because of" artinya sama saja dengan kata " Dikarenakan" dalam bahasa Inggris seperti " saya tidak masuk sekolah ya hari ini Dikarenakan cuaca yang buruk" ( Menjelaskan dengan kata "Noun" atau kata benda mati )

- Penggunaan kata "Because" artinya "Karena" yang dicontohkan seperti "saya tidak masuk sekolah ya hari ini Karena saya sakit" ( Menjelaskan dengan kata "Verb" atau kata kerja ).

23. tolong buatkan kalimat dengan kata because,because of,but,although masing" 5 beserta artinya


# Because

1. He is sad BECAUSE his pet died ( Dia sedih karena peliharaannya mati )

2. He didn't come BECAUSE he is sick ( Dia tak datang karena dia sakit )

3. Her eyes are red BECAUSE she watched the TV for too long ( Matanya        merah karena dia terlalu lama menonton TV )

4. Acok was disqualified BECAUSE he was cheating ( Acok di diskualifikasi karena dia curang

5. Marwan got perfect score BECAUSE he studied hard ( Marwan mendapatkan skor sempurna karena ia belajar dengan giat )

# Because of

1. He cried BECAUSE OF her ( Dia menangis karenanya )

2. He got expelled BECAUSE OF it ( Dia di DO karena hal tersebut )

3. BECAUSE OF Acok, they didn't make it to the finals ( Karena Acok, mereka tak berhasil ke final )

4. He changed BECAUSE OF them ( Ia berubah karena mereka )

5. This all happened BECAUSE OF her ( Ini semua terjadi karenanya )

# But

1. She is pretty BUT sadly, she is mean ( Dia cantik tapi sayangnya, ia jahat )

2. It's cool BUT dangerous ( Itu keren tapi berbahaya )

3. He looks scary BUT he is actually kind ( Dia terlihat menakutkan, tapi sebenarnya ia baik hati )

4. He wanted to buy it, BUT it was too expensive ( DIa ingin membelinya tapi terlalu mahal )

5. I like playing games, BUT i'd rather doing sports ( Saya suka bermain game, tapi saya lebih memilih berolahraga )

# Although

1. ALTHOUGH she is pretty, she is actually evil ( Meskipun ia cantik, ia sebenarnya Jahat )

2. ALTHOUGH they didn't make it to the finals, we still have to give them credits ( Meskipun mereka tak mencapai final, kita tetap harus mengapresiasi mereka )

3. ALTHOUGH he looks scary, he is actually a good boy ( Meskipun ia terlihat menakutkan, ia sebenarnya anak yang baik )

4. ALTHOUGH it's hard, i will still do my best ( Meskipun susah, saya akan tetap berusaha sebaik mungkin )

5. ALTHOUGH it's sad, it is the truth ( Meskipun menyedihkan, itulah kenyataannya )

25. Contoh kalimat Because , Because Of , dan Due to masing-masing 5 dan artinya


Answer: 

The word 'because', 'because of' and 'due to' are the clause of reason. The use of those three connectors are slightly different. 
'Because' is followed by phrase or sentence.
'Because of' and 'due to' are followed by noun or gerund. 

'Because'
1. The car is broken because the engine has been old. 
(mobilnya rusak karena mesinnya sudah tua)
2. He played well because he was in his best condition. 
(Dia bermain dengan baik karena dia dalam kondisi terbaik)
3. The book is gone because someone stole it. 
(Buku itu hilang karena ada yang mencurinya)
4. I can't swim because I'm afraid of water. 
 (Saya tidak bisa berenang karena saya takut air.)
5. The teacher will delay the test because there is an accident. 
 (Guru akan menunda tesnya karena ada kecelakaan)

Because of 
1. The match is delayed because of the weather. 
(pertandingan ditunda disebabkan oleh cuaca)
2. The boss holds the meeting soon because of the emergency situation.
(Sang boss mengadakan pertemuan segera karena situasi darurat)
3. I have to come early because of the meeting.
(saya harus datang lebih awal karena ada rapat)
4. The girl is sad because of losing her friend. 
(Gadis itu sedih karena kehilangan temannya)
5. He succeeded in finishing the project because of his good plan. 
(Dia berhasil menyelesaikan proyeknya karena perencanaan baiknya)

Due to 
1. They respect him due to his achievements.
(mereka menghormatinya karena prestasinya)
2. I salute him due to his patience.
(saya menganguminya karena kesabarannya)
3. The team lost the game due to his goalie's blunder. 
(tim itu kalah dalam pertandingan karena kesalahan kipernya)
4. He was lucky to be with Tanya due to his wealth.
(Dia beruntung bisa bersama Tanya karena kekayaannya)
5. The man was killed due to the accident. 
(seorang pria terbunuh karena kecelakaan)

Kelas: SMP
Mapel: English
Kategori: clause of reason
