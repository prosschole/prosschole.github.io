4. TULISKAN ARTI KAIN ULOS DAN PENJELASAN MYA ATAU ASAL USUL ULOS DAN KEUNIKANNYA MENNGUNAKAN BAHASA INGGRIS ??​


Jawaban:

         Ulos including hereditary clothing that has been developed by the Batak community, North Sumatra. Meanwhile, for making Ulos fabric, it is almost the same as making a typical Palembang songket, because both of them use looms and do not use machines.

Ulos was originally used as a shawl, which symbolizes the bonding of parents' love to their children and between someone and others.  This is in accordance with the Batak philosophy of "ijuk niihot ni hodong " which means that the palm fibers are attached to the stem and for Ulos, the bond of affection between people.

Each Ulos has a state, nature, function and relationship with certain things. There are 3 most basic elements in human life, namely breath, blood and heat. For the two previous elements, which are God-given and for the third element, they are not. Among Batak people it often sounds glossy which means giving Ulos.

Batak people believe that if Tondi needs to be in Ulos, then men who have hard souls have heroism and virility.  While for women, they have the characteristics of resilience against infertility or witchcraft. There are some rules that must be obeyed so that others should not just mangulosi. Parents are only allowed to mengulosi children, but children are not allowed to mengulosi parents.

It can be concluded that the principle of Batak kinship called Dalihan Na Tolu consists of elements of Hula Hula Boru and Dongan Sabutuha. For a person just now it is not right to take care of his hula.  For Ulos given, it is not permissible to do carelessly writing, both in terms of how to make it or others. For example Ulos ragidup which will be given to new people, then will give birth to his eldest child must meet certain requirements, namely Ulos Sinagok.

In ancient times the ancestors of the Batak tribe lived in mountainous areas. The majority of the habits of the ancestors are working in the fields so they are accustomed to fighting the extremely cold weather. That is why Ulos began to be made and interpreted as a blanket that is able to warm the body while protecting itself from exposure to cold enough air because Ulos fabric is very warm.

According to the ancestors of the Batak tribe, there are three sources of warmth, namely fire, sun and Ulos. Of the three ancestors assume that the source of warmth most comfortable to use for everyday life is Ulos. At first Ulos had a function as a warmer for the body. but over time Ulos was used by traditional elders.

27. Cara Membuat Bunga dari Kain Flanel Dlam Bahasa Inggris


How to make a flower from felt material

how to make a flower bouquet from flannel

tools and materials:

-flannel

-sewing needle and thread

-round cardboard pieces 24 cm in diameter

-funnel with a diameter of 20 cm

-spunbond fabric

how to make

1.cut the flannel cloth into a circle, the shape doesn't need to be a perfect circle, so you can trace the round object. Make a circle with a diameter of 10,2-25,4 cm depending on how big the rose you want to make

2.use scissors to spiral from a circle of flannel cloth. Starting from the edge and the spiral scissors inward until they reach the center of the circle

3.the next step, roll the spiral. Starting from the outer edge of the spiral and starting to roll inward. Pinch the spiral base of the flower. After the spiral is completely rolled, it will produce a beatiful rose shape

4.then sewing flower. Flip the flower and use threads and needles to sew the flower so they don't open. Make a knot at the end of the thread and add a few stitches to keep the rose layer ini place. Flower are finished

5.do it repeatedly until you are approximately enough for the bouquet of flower you want

6.next, make a bouquet of flower. Cover the cardboard with flannel cloth so that the bouquet is neater and does nok look cardboard

7.stick the outside of the flannel with a cardboard covered with flannel cloth

8.spunbond cloth scissors and attach to the flannel. Then form like flower petals

9.set the position of the flannel flower you have made before, the glue matches the position you have set

10.Finished

30. Apa bahasa inggrisnya saya mengambil pakaian/kain jemuran salam bahasa inggris?


Jawaban:

Penjelasan:

Semoga membantu = I hope this helps

Jawaban:

- I took down the clothes from the clothesline. (kalau baru nurunin)

- I'm taking down the clothes from the clothesline. (kalau lagi/mau nurunin)

- I've taken down the clothes from the clothesline. (kalau udah nurunin)

Penjelasan:

Actually the English for kain jemuran = clothes which are hung to dry, which is too long to say. So, the most appropriate way to say "saya mengambil kain jemuran" depends on the time as well, resulting in 3 answers:

I took down the clothes from the clothesline. = saya baru nurunin pakaian dari jemuran

I'm taking down the clothes from the clothesline. = saya sedang/lagi nurunin pakaian dari jemuran / saya mau nurunin pakaian dari jemuran.

I've taken down the clothes from the clothesline. = saya sudah nurunin pakaian dari jemuran.
