23. 72 pics berapa lusin


1 lusin = 12 pics

1 pics = 1/12 lusin


72 pics

= 72 x 1/12 lusin

= 72/12 lusin

= 6 lusin


Kelas 4

Pelajaran Matematika

Bab 3 Pengukuran

Kata kunci : -

Kode kategorisasi : 4.2.3


Internal link

https://brainly.co.id/tugas/10612903

Penyelesaian =

1 pcs = 1 buah
72 pcs = 72 buah
1 lusin = 12 buah
Maka 72 : 12 = 6

Jawaban =

6 Lusin
Jadi 72 pcs = 6 lusin
