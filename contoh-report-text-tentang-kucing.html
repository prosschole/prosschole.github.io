11. contoh teks report bahasa Inggris kucing dong​


Cats

Cats are a type of mammal from the felidae family. Cats are included in the carnivorous class that is meat-eating animals.

Cats can be grouped into 2, namely large cats and domestic cats. Big cats include tigers, lions, cheetahs, and others.

Whereas a house cat is a type that is very popular as a pet.

House cats can reach 16 kg in weight, 60 cm in length and 45 cm in height. In addition, cats have pointed fangs and claws that can be pulled and stretched as needed. Cat's eyesight is very good.

This type of cat also includes loners where each cat has its own area. If there are foreign cats that enter usually there will be a brief fight.

A cat who is fighting will straighten his body hair and bend his body to make it look bigger.

Cat attacks are generally in the form of scratches, bites, and slaps. Active males will often get into fights.

Usually pet cats are not dangerous to humans because of their small body. But did not rule out this animal can transmit disease.

Diseases that may be transmitted include rabies due to bites and respiratory problems due to fur.


12. berikan contoh report text tentang hewan kucing [Ujian Akhir Semester UAS 2015]



Cat

Cats are the most popular pet and can be found everywhere on the earth. They have a Latin name Felis Catus Silvertis. They have become tame animals and humans were functioned them as pets since 6000 BC. In ancient times, the Egyptians used them as an animal to hunt the rats.

When they were born, they are blind and deaf. Then, they opened the eyes when they have aged between 8 to 10 days. Furthermore, their sexual hormones are active at the age of 10 to 15 months. On average, they have a weight ranging from 2.5 kg to 7.0 kg. Meanwhile, the length of them is varies, but mostly they can grow from 15 to 30 cm. Cats are lazy animals where they just spend time at home to sleep. However, not all the cats have that such behavior, one of the active cat is “Kucing Kampung” which is the icon of Indonesian cat. Those cats always try to go out and to look for food.

They can live up to the age of 20 years, and the world’s oldest cat lived about 36 years. Meanwhile, Kucing Kampung which are from Indonesia usually live only between 2 until 5 years. Cats are omnivore because we are very often seeing them eat fish, meat, rice, and even vegetables. Nevertheless, many people also refer them as a carnivore. They have small teeth and claws that are not harmful to humans. But, you have to be careful because a cat bite can cause rabies and their claws are so painful.

Arti:

Kucing

Kucing adalah hewan peliharaan yang paling populer dan dapat ditemukan di mana-mana. Mereka memiliki nama latin Felis Catus Silvertis. Mereka telah menjadi hewan jinak dan manusia memfungsikan mereka sebagai hewan peliharaan sejak 6000 SM. Pada zaman kuno, orang Mesir menggunakan mereka sebagai hewan untuk berburu tikus.

Ketika lahir, mereka buta dan tuli. Kemudian, kucing membuka mata ketika mereka telah berusia antara 8 sampai 10 hari. Selanjutnya, hormon seksual mereka aktif pada usia 10 sampai 15 bulan. Rata-rata, mereka memiliki berat mulai dari 2,5 kg menjadi 7,0 kg. Sementara itu, panjang tubuh kucing sangatlah bervariasi, tetapi sebagian besar dari mereka dapat tumbuh dari 15 sampai 30 cm. Kucing adalah hewan malas di mana mereka hanya menghabiskan waktu di rumah untuk tidur. Namun, tidak semua kucing memiliki perilaku seperti itu, salah satu kucing yang aktif adalah “Kucing Kampung” yang merupakan ikon kucing Indonesia. Mereka selalu mencoba untuk pergi keluar dan mencari makanan.

Mereka dapat hidup sampai usia 20 tahun, dan kucing tertua di dunia hidup sekitar 36 tahun. Sementara itu, Kucing Kampung yang berasal dari Indonesia biasanya hidup hanya antara 2 sampai 5 tahun. Kucing merupakan hewan omnivora karena kita sangat sering melihat mereka makan ikan, daging, beras, dan bahkan sayuran. Namun demikian, banyak orang juga merujuk mereka sebagai karnivora. Mereka memiliki gigi kecil dan cakar yang tidak berbahaya bagi manusia. Tapi, Anda harus berhati-hati karena gigitan kucing dapat menyebabkan rabies dan cakar mereka begitu menyakitkan.

28. Contoh teks report tentang kucing


Jawaban:

Cat

Cats or as we usually called it as the domestic cat are four-legged carnivorous mammal. Their latin name is Felis catus or Felis silvestris catus. The domestication of cats is believed to have started since ancient Egypt 9,500 years ago. Since that, cats have become humans companion. Nowadays it is the most popular pet in the world and also the second most popular pet in the US and they are often called as the house cats. It is believed that there are more than 70 cat breeds now in the world.

Most cats are furry, only some of them such as Sphynx cat born with less fur on their body. Some cats have a long tail and some others have a short tail. It also has a very flexible body and sharp claws which can be retracted. Usually cats can grow up to 4 to 5 kg in weight and 23 to 25 cm in length, but sometimes it can be smaller or grow even bigger. Their eyes allow them to see in the dark and their ears allow them to hear sound with a high frequency that human could not hear. They are a good solitary hunters who have a really good sense of smell. There are various colors of cats in the world such as white, brown, grey, black, stripes and even multi color. They are a social species and they can make various sound such as mewing, purring, hissing and growling.

30. report text tentang kucing beserta bahasa Indonesianya


Cat is a domesticated animal known as Felis Catus. Cats are known as pets everywhere in the world. They belong to the Felidae family from the carnivore order and are the youngest members of the family.

Like all Felids family, cat is known for its lean and low body, streamlined head and tail for balance. Teeth and little paws are designed for a great adaptation in hunting. Cats are carnivores with great agility and strength.

Cats purr as a sign of relaxation and contentment, they are so cute and adorable, no wonder that many people adopt them as their pet.

Kucing adalah hewan peliharaan yang dikenal dengan nama Felis Catus. Kucing dikenal sebagai hewan peliharaan di seluruh dunia. Mereka milik keluarga Felidae dari ordo karnivora dan merupakan anggota termuda dari keluarga.

Seperti semua keluarga Felid, kucing dikenal dengan tubuhnya yang ramping dan rendah, kepala dan ekor yang ramping untuk keseimbangan. Gigi dan cakar kecil dirancang untuk adaptasi yang hebat dalam berburu. Kucing adalah karnivora dengan kelincahan dan kekuatan yang luar biasa.

Kucing mendengkur sebagai tanda relaksasi dan kepuasan, mereka sangat lucu dan menggemaskan, tidak heran banyak orang mengadopsi mereka sebagai hewan peliharaan.
