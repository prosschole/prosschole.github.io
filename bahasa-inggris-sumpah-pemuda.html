10. Sejarah sumpah pemuda dalam bahasa inggris dan arti bahasa indonesianya!


Sejarah Sumpah Pemuda dalam Bahasa Inggris

The Youth Pledge is an important milestone in Indonesia's long journey to independence. The Youth Pledge was formulated at the Second Youth Congress in 1928, which was attended by students from all over the archipelago who were members of the Indonesian Student Association (PPPI).

The congress was held in three different buildings and divided into three meetings. The first meeting was held on Saturday, October 27, 1928, at the Katholieke Jongenlingen Bond (KJB) Building, Lapangan Banteng. This meeting discussed the meaning and relationship of unity with the youth. The next day, Sunday, October 28, 1928, another meeting was held at the Oost-Java Bioscoop Building to discuss education and the importance of nationalism and democracy.

Before the congress was closed, the song "Indonesia" (Indonesia Raya) by Wage Rudolf Supratman was sung for the first time. Then the formulation of the congress results was announced to close the congress known as the Youth Pledge, which reads:

We, the sons and daughters of Indonesia, claim to swear one blood, the Motherland of Indonesia.

We, the sons and daughters of Indonesia, claim one nation, the Indonesian Nation.

We, the sons and daughters of Indonesia, uphold the language of unity, the Indonesian language.

Pembahasan

Terjemahan Sejarah Sumpah Pemuda

Sumpah Pemuda merupakan tonggak sejarah penting dalam perjalanan panjang Indonesia dalam merebut kemerdekaan. Sumpah Pemuda merupakan hasil rumusan dalam Kongres Pemuda II pada tahun 1928 yang dihadiri para pelajar dari seluruh wilayah Nusantara yang tergabung dalam Perhimpunan Pelajar Pelajar Indonesia (PPPI).

Kongres dilaksanakan di tiga gedung yang berbeda dan dibagi dalam tiga kali rapat. Rapat pertama dilaksanakan pada hari Sabtu, 27 Oktober 1928, di Gedung Katholieke Jongenlingen Bond (KJB), Lapangan Banteng. Rapat ini membahas mengenai arti dan hubungan persatuan dengan pemuda. Keesokan harinya, Minggu, 28 Oktober 1928, rapat kembali diakan di Gedung Oost-Java Bioscoop untuk membahas masalah pendidikan serta pentingnya nasionalisme dan demokrasi.

Sebelum kongres ditutup, lagu “Indonesia” (Indonesia Raya) karya Wage Rudolf Supratman dikumandangkan untuk pertama kalinya. Kemudian Rumusan hasil kongres diumumkan untuk menutup kongres tersebut yang dikenal sebagai Sumpah Pemuda, berbunyi :

Kami putra dan putri Indonesia, mengaku bertumpah darah yang satu, Tanah Air Indonesia.

Kami putra dan putri Indonesia, mengaku berbangsa yang satu, Bangsa Indonesia.

Kami putra dan putri Indonesia, menjunjung bahasa persatuan, Bahasa Indonesia.

12. Jelaskan sejarah sumpah pemuda dalam bahasa inggris


The Youth Pledge is a major milestone in the history of Indonesia's independence movement. This pledge is considered as a crystallization of the spirit to affirm the ideals of the establishment of the Indonesian state.

What is meant by "Youth Pledge" is the decision of the Second Youth Congress held two days, 27-28 October 1928 in Batavia (Jakarta). This decision confirms the ideal of ""homeland of Indonesia, "" Indonesian nation, "and" Indonesian. "This decision is also expected to be a principle for every" Indonesian national association "and" to be broadcast in all newspapers and read before assemblies of assemblies ".
The term "Youth Pledge" itself does not appear in the congress decision, but is given thereafter.

19. Sejarah singkat sumpah pemuda dalam bahasa inggris


Soempah Pemoeda or Sumpah Pemuda is the recognition of young Indonesian youth who pledged one homeland, one nation and one language. The Youth Pledge was read out on October 28, 1928, the result of the formulation of the Second Youth Congress which is now commemorated as the Youth Pledge Day.

The second Congress is a continuation of congress I conducted 2 years earlier. The Youth Congress I was held in Batavia [now Jakarta] on 30 April to 2 May 1926 by the Jong Indonesia Congressional Committee headed by Tabrani.

28. bahasa inggrisnya hari sumpah pemuda


Bahasa Inggrisnya Hari Sumpah Pemuda adalah The Youth Pledge Day.

Bila diartikan, day adalah hari. Pledge adalah sumpah. Youth adalah pemuda. Menggunakan the karena menunjukkan suatu kejadian yang penting.

Pembahasan

Definition of the youth pledge day is a day when the young Indonesian nationalists declared three values, one motherland, one nation, and one language on October 28th, 1928.

Dalam bahasa Indonesia, definisi hari Sumpah Pemuda adalah hari di mana par nasionalis muda Indonesia mendeklarasikan tiga hal, satu tanah air, satu bangsa, dan satu bahasa pada 28 Oktober 1928.
