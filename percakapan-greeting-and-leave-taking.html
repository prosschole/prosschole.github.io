1. buatlah percakapan tentang greeting and leave taking


Greeting and Leave-Taking Conversation:

A: Good morning, B!

B: Oh, hi, A! Good morning!

A: I haven't seen you since the last time we met at your cousin's birthday dinner.

B: Yes, you're right. I stayed at my aunt's house since that day.

A: Oh, I see. Is everything in a good condition?

B: Sure. Oh, may I know your number? I really want to talk to you about many things, but I have to go now. My sister is waiting for me.

A: Of course. Here it is.

B: Okay, catch you later on chat. See you.

A: Bye!

Pembahasan:

Translation:

A: Selamat pagi!

B: Oh, hai! Selamat pagi!

A: Aku belum bertemu denganmu lagi sejak terakhir kali kita bertemu di acara makan malam ulang tahun sepupumu.

B: Ya, kamu benar. Aku tinggal di rumah bibiku sejak hari itu.

J: Oh, begitu. Apakah semuanya dalam kondisi baik?

B: Tentu. Oh, bolehkah aku tahu nomor telepon gawaimu? Aku benar-benar ingin berbicara denganmu tentang banyak hal, tetapi aku harus pergi sekarang. Adikku sedang menungguku.

A: Tentu saja. Ini dia.

B: Oke, sampai jumpa di obrolan virtual. Sampai jumpa.

A: Sampai jumpa!

Ekspresi salam perjumpaan (greeting) dalam teks di atas adalah:

A: Good morning, B!

B: Oh, hi, A! Good morning!

Ekspresi salam perpisahan (leave-taking) dalam teks di atas adalah:

B: Okay, catch you later on chat. See you.

A: Bye!

27. materi tentang Greeting and leave taking


Greeting.
When we meet someone we know, we usually greet him/her whatever formal or informal.
The formal greetings are usually used when we meet someone whom we respect.
Informal greeting is used when we meet someone we already know eachother, like classmates, close friend, or friend. 

Leave taking/partings
Leave taking usually used if we part with someone we know. It’s divided into formal and informal parting
