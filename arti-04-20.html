10. 04. Nilai dari cos 80° + cos 40º - cos 20°​


[tex]\begin{aligned}&\cos80^{\circ}+\cos40^{\circ}-\cos20^{\circ}\\=&2\cos60^{\circ}\cos20^{\circ}-\cos20^{\circ}\\=&2\cdot\frac{1}{2}\cdot\cos20^{\circ}-\cos20^{\circ}\\=&\cos20^{\circ}-\cos20^{\circ}\\=&0\end{aligned}[/tex]

cos 80° + cos 40° + cos 20°

= (cos 80° + cos 40°) + cos 20°

= 2 cos ½ (80° + 40) cos ½ (80° – 40°) + cos 20°

= 2 cos ½ (120°) cos ½ (40°) + cos 20°

= 2 cos 60° cos 20° + cos 20°

= 2 (½) cos 20° + cos 20°

= cos 20° + cos 20°

= 2 cos 20
