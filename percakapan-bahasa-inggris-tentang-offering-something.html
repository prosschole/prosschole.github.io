A : hello B how are you ?
B : I'm fine thank you and you ?
A : I'm fine too thank you. You look so busy today. Do you need help ?
B : that would be great. I'm going to make a birthday party for my brother.
A : ok so let's get started and don't let the time runs out
B : ok and thank you.
A : you are welcome

(A sama B nya bisa diganti sama nama orang lain )

7. percakapan bahasa inggris offering for help and refusing for help


Offering for help
A: Good Morning B, what are you doing?
B: Good morning A, uh, i'm a little bit hard to find connection in here.
A: Can i help you? i know about technology a little bit.
B: Thanks A! you really helpful

Refusing Help
C: Hi D! what's wrong?
D: I can't do my homework, it's so damn hard
C: Let me help you.
D: Thanks C, but i think i have to solve this by myself.. i need more concentration to solve this..

30. apa bahasa indonesia dari offering something,accepting something,dan refusing something


offering something = menawarkan sesuatu.
accepting something = menerima sesuatu.
refusing something = menolak sesuatuarti
