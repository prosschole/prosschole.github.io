1. berapa menitkah 12km/jam=....m/menit


1 km = 1.000m
1 jam = 60 menit

12km/jam = 12 × (1.000 × 60) m/menit
= 12 × 60.000 m/menit
= 720.000m/menit

waktu : t = 6 menit,

v (kecepatan) =.....?

v = s / t

v = 12 km / 0,1 jam

v = 120 km/jam
