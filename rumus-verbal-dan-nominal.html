5. Tulislah Rumus Simple present verbal dan nominal!​


Jawaban:

Rumus Present Tense

1. Verbal Sentence

- Kalimat yang ada kata kerja (verb) di dalamnya.

(+) S + V1 (-s/es) + O/C

(-) S + Do/Does + not + V1 + O/C

(?) Do/Does + S + V1 + O/C?

- Subject + Auxiliary

I, you, we, they + do

He, She, it + does

Contoh:

(+) they go to school every morning

(-) they do not go to school every morning

(?) do they go to school every morning?

2. Nominal Sentence

- Kalimat yg tidak ada kata kerja di dalam kalimatnya.

(+) S + Tobe (is/am/are) + Adj/Adv/Noun

(-) S + Tobe (is/am/are) + not + Adj/Adv/Noun

(?) Tobe (is/am/are) + S + Adj/Adv/Noun?

- Subject + Tobe

I + am

You, we, they + are

He, She, it + is

Contoh:

(+) They are students

(-) They are not students

(?) Are they students ?

20. apa rumus simpel pasten ? dan berikan contoh kalimat verbal dan nominal nya !


rumus simple past tense : Subject + Verb2 + object + etc... Ex : - i studied english last week - my father worked at seoul hospital last year.The Pattern of Simple Past Tense:

Subject + V2 + Object/ time signal ( for verbal )

Subject + to be (was/were) + object/ time signal ( for nominal )

Example:

Verbal: She sang
( "She" is the subject and "sang" is the v2 from sing )

Nominal: She was a teacher
( "She" is the subject, "was" is to be and "a teacher" is the object )

22. rumus past perfect tense kalimat verbal dan nominal


verbal past perfect : (+) had + v3. (-) had not + v3. (?) had + s + v3? nominal past perfect : (+) had been + noun. (-) had not been + noun. (?) had + s + been + noun?Kalimat verbal:
1. Kalimat aktif:
    Kalimat positif: Subjek + Had + Kata Kerja III + Komplemen
    Kalimat negatif: Subjek + Had + Not + Kata Kerja III + Komplemen
    Kalimat interogatif: Had + Subjek + Kata Kerja III + Komplemen
2. Kalimat pasif:
    Kalimat positif: Subjek + Had + Kata Kerja III + By + Objek 
    Kalimat negatif: Subjek + Had + Not + Kata Kerja III + By + Objek
    Kalimat Interogatif: Had + Subjek + Kata Kerja III + By + Objek

Kalimat nominal:
    Kalimat positif: Subjek + Had + To Be III (Been) + Bukan Kata Kerja
    Kalimat negatif: Subjek + Had + Not + To Be III (Been) + Bukan Kata Kerja
    Kalimat interogatif: Had + Subjek + To Be III (Been) + Bukan Kata Kerja

RUMUS SIMPLE PAST VERBAL
(+) Subject + V2 + O
(-) Subject + did + not + V1 + O
(?) Did + Subject + V1 + O

RUMUS SIMPLE PAST NOMINAL
(+) Subject + Was/Were + Adjective/noun
(-) Subject + Was/Were + Not + Adjective/noun
(?) Was/Were + Subject + Adjective/noun

WAS => I, she, he, it
WERE => You, they, we
