16. ceritakan pengalaman hari raya idul Fitri dalam bahasa Inggris ​


Jawaban:

Last Eid I had a very happy and enjoyable day and experience with friends and family.

On the eve of Eid Al-Fitr with my friends in the village we went to the mosque together and recited takbir all night. We were very. There was a lot of food in the mosque for adults and everyone who were doing takbiran.

In the morning my family went to pray Eid in an open field in our village. We left at 7 A.M, with our best clothes. We performed Eid prayers and listened to the sermon. Then we went home to eat a lot of Eid food. There was a lot of Lebaran cuisine and there were very delicious and many Eid cakes.

Then we all visited and gathered at Grandpa's house with the whole family. It was fun and I'm waiting for the next Eid.

Penjelasan:

Terjemahan recount text di atas adalah:

Pada hari Idul Fitri yang lalu saya mempunyai hari yang dan pengalaman yang sangat gembira dan menyenangkan dengan sahabat dan keluarga.

Pada malam Idul Fitri saya bersama teman-teman saya di desa pergi ke masjid bersama dan bertakbir semalam suntuk. Kami sangat bergembira dan bersyukur. Ada banyak makanan di masjid untuk anak-anak dan orang dewasa yang sedang melaksanakan takbiran.

Pada pagi hari kami sekeluarga pergi melaksanakan sholat Idul Fitri di lapangan terbuka yang ada di desa kami. Kami pergi pada jam 7 pagi, memakai baju-baju terbaik kami. Kami melaksanakan sholat Id dan mendengarkan khutbah. Kemudian kami pulang ke rumah untuk menyantap makanan Idul Fitri yang sangat banyak dan ada kue-kue Lebaran yang sangat enak dan banyak.

Kemudian kami mengunjungi dan berkumpul di rumah kakek bersama seluruh keluarga. Sangat menyenangkan dan saya selalu menunggu Idul Fitri berikutnya.

17. Tolong buatkan cerita tentang liburan idul fitri di rumah aja dalam bahasa Inggris menggunakan simple past tense


Cerita tentang liburan Idul Fitri di rumah dalam bahasa Inggris:

This year's Eid holiday is very pleasant. Since my grandparents lived with my family, so we decided to celebrate Eid at home.

The day before Eid al-Fitr, my family divided the task of preparing things. My mother and my grandmother were in charge of cooking the dishes that we would eat tomorrow. My grandfather and my father were in charge of cleaning the house. I also participated in the preparation so our work was quickly completed.

The next day, we and our family performed Eid prayers in the morning. After we prayed, we apologized to each other and then followed by eating together.

After we ate together, we were shocked by the arrival of our relatives who live outside the city. Then we talked and I got some religious holiday allowances from my family.

This Eid holiday is a lot of fun!

Pembahasan:

Terjemahan cerita tentang liburan Idul Fitri di rumah:

Libur lebaran tahun ini sangat menyenangkan. Karena kakek dan nenek saya tinggal bersama keluarga saya, jadi kami memutuskan untuk merayakan Idul Fitri di rumah.

Sehari menjelang Idul Fitri, keluarga saya membagi tugas untuk mempersiapkan berbagai hal. Ibu dan nenek saya bertugas memasak hidangan yang akan kami santap esok hari. Sedangkan kakek dan ayah saya bertugas untuk membersihkan rumah. Saya pun turut ikut serta dalam persiapan tersebut sehingga pekerjaan kami cepat selesai.

Keesokan harinya, kami sekeluarga melaksanakan sholat Idul Fitri pada pagi hari. Setelah beribadah, kami saling bermaaf-maafan yang dilanjutkan dengan makan bersama.

Setelah makan bersama, kami dikejutkan oleh kedatangan kerabat kami yang tinggal di luar kota. Lalu kami pun berbincang-bincang dan saya mendapatkan uang THR dari keluarga saya.

Liburan lebaran kali ini sangat menyenangkan!

Teks diatas merupakan contoh recount text. Recount text merupakan salah satu jenis teks dalam Bahasa Inggris yang berisi cerita tentang kegiatan penulis. Kegiatan yang dimaksud merupakan pengalaman dari penulis yang diungkapkan melalui recount text. Tujuan recount text adalah untuk menghibur pembaca serta memberikan informasi tentang pengalaman penulis.

Recount text memiliki generic structure yakni Orientation, Events, dan Reorientation.

21. karangan bahasa inggris tentang idul fitri


Eid al- Fitr Eid al- Fitr is the feast awaited by all Moslem. I am so waiting it so much and want to feel the sensation of the feast. In the morning, my mother awakened and reminded me for carrying morning pray. When I left the room, my father and my mother had been carrying out the Subuh pray. However, my brother was still sleeping soundly and my mother told me that he was very hard to be awakened. After I did the Subuh pray, I rushed a shower and then wore my best clothes to go to the mosque. 30 minutes later, we were ready and immediately went to the mosque to perform Eid pray. The exclamations were reverberated when we walked into the mosque, and the mosque became a crowded atmosphere because almost all the people in my village had entered the mosque. My father, my brother, and I were pretty hard to find a place to sit because the mosque was so crowded. Meanwhile, my mother had gotten a seat in the back, at the row of women. When we all had sat down, the prayer began. Eid prayer is a solemn moment and should be implemented by earnest. We were praying for five to ten minutes. After the prayer had completed, all the worshipers in the mosque were required to sit and listen to lectures. Lectures that delivered by the Islamic preacher aimed to motivate all Muslims to be able to become a better person after the month of Ramadhan. Lecture proceeded for about ten minutes and we went home soon when the preacher had ended his talk. On the way home, my family and I met a lot of people and we shake hands with to be able to forgive each other. After I got home, I went to the kitchen and ate delicious food while my parents and my brother arranged the cake in the living room. I did not really care about it and more care about the food on the table. A few minutes later, neighbors came to the house for having the fellowship and I rushed into the living room to meet them. My family and I also walk around the village to visit all neighbor. Then, we spent the whole day at Grandma and Grandpa’s house. That was my Eid al-Fitr.

Pembahasan

Cerita karangan di atas termasuk ke dalam kategori cerita/paragraf/karangan narasi.

Karangan narasi adalah karangan cerita yang menyajikan serangkaian peristiwa kejadian.

Teks narasi tersusun secara kronologis sesuai dengan urutan waktunya (runtut).

Struktur teks narasi:

- Orientasi merupakan bagian yang berisi pengenalan tokoh. Biasanya berada di awal paragraf.

- Komplikasi merupakan bagian yang berisi permasalahan terhadap tokoh.

- Resolusi merupakan bagian yang berisi tentang cara menangani masalah/konflik yang sedang terjadi.

- Koda merupakan bagian akhir teks. Ada 2 macam koda, yaitu happy ending dan sad ending. Namun ada juga cerita yang akhirnya menggantung.
