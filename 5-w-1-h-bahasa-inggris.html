3. Buatlah pertanyaan dalam bahasa Inggris dari 5 W 1 H?​


Where does he work?

When will you go to Bali?

Why do you learn English?

Who are you?

What is his name?

How do you make the cake?

5W + 1H adalah singkatan dri Who (siapa), What (Apa), Why (kenapa), Where (dimana), When (kapan) dan How (bagaimana).

Contoh:

Who = who are you?

What = what are you doing?

Why = why are you late to this class?

Where = where are you right now?

When = when are we going to the zoo?

How = how many books do you want to buy?
