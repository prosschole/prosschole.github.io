1. how to make indonesia chicken stay page 14


'How to Make Indonesian Chicken Satay'

ingredients/material :
• 3 tablespoons (45ml) soya sauce
• 3 tablespoons tomato sauce
• 1 tablespoon (15ml) peanut oil
• 2 cloves garlic, peeled and crushed
• 1 pinch ground black pepper
• 1 pinch ground cumin
• 6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cubed
• satay sauce
• 1 tablespoon (15ml) vegetable oil
• ¼ cup grated onion, about half a medium onion
• 1 clove garlic, peeled and crushed
• 1 cup (250ml) water
• 1/2 cup (125g) chunky peanut butter
• 2 tablespoons (30ml) soya sauce
• 2 tablespoons caster sugar
• 1 tablespoon (15ml) lemon juice, about one small lemon
• skewers
• barbeque grill
• coal

steps : 
Making the sauce:
1. finely chop the garlics and shallots
2. Saute the chooped garlic and shallots until fragrance in medium-high heat
3.add the grinded roasted/fried peanuts and mix it well in low heat.
4. add ¾ cup f water and mix it well. If you think its too thick, you can use 1 cup of water instead ¾ cup of water.
5. After mix it well, add 1 ts of salt and 1 tbs of sugar and mix it again.
6. Last touch, add 3 tbs of kecap manis or sweet soya sauce and mix well

Making the chicken
- cut the chicken breast fillet into 1 cm dice. (cube cut or macedoine cut)
- put it into the skewer (3-4 pieces in one skewer)
- add 3 tbs of the sauce and lots of sweet soya sauce on top of it. Mix well.
- Marinate the chicken in 5 minutes and start to grill it. Tips: brush the chicken with the marinate sauce while grilling. It will become more delicious.
- Occasionaly turn the chicken until its cooked. The chicken its easily cooked so be careful.
- After all the satay its cooked, put the rest of the sauce, sweet soya sauce (around 5 tbs spoon or more), kaffir lime juice (half or 1 of it) and mix it together.
- Put the fried shallots on top of it. Serve with nice warm rice.

tips : cook the satay until the color is dark brown.

Follow the instuction and you will get the best food ..

12. jelaskan apa yang dimaksud dengan portrait dan landscape pada Ms Word


Berikut ini adalah penjelasan dari portrait dan landscape pada Ms Word yaitu:

Portrait yaitu merupakan menu yang berguna untuk mengatur bentuk halaman menjadi lebih memanjang ke atas seperti bentuk buku pada umumnya.Landscape yaitu merupakan menu yang berguna untuk mengatur bentuk halaman secara memanjang ke arah samping, seperti pada bentuk buku gambar.Pembahasan

Pada waktu ingin memasukkan tabel, diagram, atau gambar dalam dokumen, halaman landscape akan sangat diperlukan. Berikut langkah-langah dalam mengatur cara untuk membuat portrait dan landscape dalam satu dokumen yaitu:

Buka Ms. Word yang ada di laptop atau PC.Selanjutnya pisahkan halaman portrait dan landscape dengan Section Break. Section Break termasuk bisa kamu temukan dengan membuka menu yaitu Page Layout tab --> Page Setup --> group - Breaks --> Section Breaks –-> Next Page.Kemudian klik selanjutnya pada halaman section tersebut yang pada Page Layout tab dan pilih Page Setup group yang kemudian klik Orientation dan pilih Landscape.Pelajari lebih lanjutMateri penjelasan tentang lanscape yaitu pada link

How to Cook Fried Rice

>Ingredients:

-2 cups of cooked rice

-5 chilies or more (depends on your spicy level)

-1 clove of garlic

-2 onions

-2 tablespoons of butter

-1 egg, slightly beaten

-Soy sauce

-Salt

>Steps of cooking fried rice:

1) First, puree the garlic, onion, and chilies with the help of pestle, or you can use blender as an alternative way.

2) Next, heat the butter on frying pan and wait for a while until it’s melted.

3) Then, pour the puree seasoning and stir-fry.

4) After that, add the slightly beaten egg and mix it well.

5) Next, add salt and soy sauce.

Pour the cooked rice and mix them well.

6) Check the taste whether it’s tasty enough or not, add salt if it’s less salty.

7) Finally, the simple fried rice is ready to serve.

24. the first page of a story book is numbered from page 1 onwards. all the pages in storybook are numbered. to print all the page numbers of storybook, 65 digits needed. how many pages are there in the storybook?​


Jawab:

There are 37 pages in the storybook.

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

The sequence of one-digit page numbers constructs a 9 digit number like this: 123456789.And then, it comes to two-digit page numbers. Each one of these needs 2 digit. So, from 10 to n (n is a two-digit number), there will be 2(n-9)-digit number.

Therefore, the addition of both rules will result in the total digits needed to print all the page numbers in sequence, starting from number 1.

    9 + 2(n-9) = 65

⇔ 2(n-9) = 65 - 9

⇔ 2(n-9) = 56

⇔ n-9 = 56/2

⇔ n-9 = 28

⇔ n = 28+9

⇔ n = 37

Conclusion: There are 37 pages in the storybook.

Proof:

From 1 to 9: 9 digits needed.From 10 to 19: 20 additional digits needed, so the total is 29 digits.From 20 to 29: 20 additional digits needed, so the total is 49 digits.From 30 to 37: 16 digits additional needed, so the total is 65 digits.

The 65-digit number is:

25. Amir is buying cloth to make curtains. He needs4.6 m of cloth but it is only sold in whole metres.How much cloth does he need to buy?​


Solution + Answer:

If Amir wanted to buy curtains, he actually needs 4.6 metres of cloth. To sold it in whole metres, all you need to do is rounding it to the nearest tenth (for the metres) or either rounding it to the nearest hundred (especially for centimetres) , so here's the solution:

4,6 metres = 460 centimetres:

Since 6 is bigger than 5, we're rounding off 460 centimetres into 500 centimetres, which is equal to 5 metres.
