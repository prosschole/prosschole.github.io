Dijawab
panjang : lebar = 3 : 2
Panjang =  3x
Lebar     =   2x

3x . 2x = 2400
6x ^(2) =2400
x^(2)=400
x=20

panjang = 3 . 20= 60

lebar = 2 . 20 = 40

keliling = 2(60+40)
keliling = 100
