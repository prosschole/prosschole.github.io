8. Write a short dialog by using expression offering a help/service?


Ada beberapa frase yang bisa dipakai untuk menawarkan bantuan. Misalnya:


Can I _____? (diikuti kata kerja)

Contoh: Can I bring your bag?


Shall I _____? (diikuti kata kerja)

Contoh: Shall I bring your laptop?


Would you ____? (diikuti kata benda atau kata kerja + to)

Contoh: Would you like a drink? Would you like me to open the door?


Do you ____? (pakai ini kalau tidak yakin apakah bantuannya akan diterima)

Contoh: Do you need something?

I'd be glad to ____.  

Contoh: I'd be glad to do the project.


Pembahasan

Ira: Hey, Ria. What are you doing?

Ria: Oh hi, Ira. I am doing my homework. This is difficult.

Ira: Can I help you?

Ria: No, I don't want to bother you.

Ira: It's not a problem. I'd be glad to help you with homework.

Ira: Thank you.

13. write a dialog using the expression of offering help or service


Ada beberapa frase yang bisa dipakai untuk menawarkan bantuan. Misalnya:

Can I _____? (diikuti kata kerja)

Contoh: Can I bring your bag?

Shall I _____? (diikuti kata kerja)

Contoh: Shall I bring your laptop?

Would you ____? (diikuti kata benda atau kata kerja + to)

Contoh: Would you like a drink? Would you like me to open the door?

Do you ____? (pakai ini kalau tidak yakin apakah bantuannya akan diterima)

Contoh: Do you need something?

I'd be glad to ____.

Contoh: I'd be glad to do the project.


Menerima bantuan:

Yes please, I’d like to. / Yes please, I’d love to.

If you wouldn’t mind.

Thank you, that would be great.

Menolak bantuan:

It's okay, I can do it myself.

Don’t worry, I’ll do it.

No, thank you.


Pembahasan

Dita: Mom, do you want me to help you cooking?

Mom: Thank you, Dita. That would be great.

Dita: Shall I boil the water?

Mom: Yes, please.

Dita: Would you like me to chop the carrot?

Mom: No, it's okay. I'll do it myself. Knives are dangerous.

Dita: Okay, mom.

28. Ada yang bisa buat dialog OFFERING help/service about covid19? minimum 10parts


Jawaban:

A: Can COVID-19 spread through food?

B: Coronaviruses cannot multiply in food; they need an animal or human host to multiply.

A: Will I get more severe symptoms of COVID-19 if I drink alcohol?

B: Consuming alcohol will not destroy the virus, and its consumption is likely to increase the health risks if a person becomes infected with the virus.

A: Is the coronavirus disease more severe than the flu?

B: While many people globally have built up immunity to seasonal flu strains, COVID-19 is a new virus to which no one has immunity. That means more people are susceptible to infection, and some will suffer severe disease.

A: How dangerous is COVID-19?

B: Although for most people COVID-19 causes only mild illness, it can make some people very ill. More rarely, the disease can be fatal. Older people, and those with pre- existing medical conditions (such as high blood pressure, heart problems or diabetes) appear to be more vulnerable

A: What are some safety precautions against the coronavirus disease?

B: By following good respiratory hygiene, you protect the people around you from viruses such as cold, flu and COVID-19.

Stay home and self-isolate even with minor symptoms such as cough, headache, mild fever, until you recover. Have someone bring you supplies. If you need to leave your house, wear a mask to avoid infecting others
