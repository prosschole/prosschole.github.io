Here is the application letter written by Lilis.

(1)  Sender : Pengirim

Lilis Handayani

Jl. A. Yani 389 Surabaya, 65151

April 19, 2015  

(2) Receiver  : Penerima

Mr. Frank Peterson, Personnel Manager Jeans and Co.

Jl. Raya Pandaan 186

Pandaan Pasuruan, Jawa Timur, 98502

(3) Salutation : Salam pembuka

Dear Mr. Peterson:  

(4) Content : Isi

I am writing to you in response to your advertisement for a local  branch manager newspaper appeared in the Jawa Pos on Sunday,  June 15. As you can see from my enclosed resume, my experience and  qualifications match this position's requirements.  

Saya menulis kepada anda atas iklan yang mengenai lowongan manager cabang lokal di Jawa Pos, hari minggu, 15 Juni. Sebagaimana yang anda lihat pada resume saya, pengalaman dan kulifikasi saya cocok dengan posisi yang ditawarkan

(5) My current position, managing the local branch of a national shoe  retailer, has provided the opportunity to work under a high-pressure,  team environment, where it is essential to be able to work closely  with my colleagues in order to meet sales deadlines. In addition to my  responsibilities as manager, I also developed time management tools for  staff using Access and Excel from Microsoft's Office Suite.

Posisi saya sekarang, memanajeri cabang lokal dari retailer nasional sepatu yang telah memberikan kesempatan untuk bekerja dnegan tekanan tinggi, lingkungan tim, dimana penting sekali untuk dapat bekerja dengan rekan untuk memenuhi target penjualan. Sebagai tambahan, kewajiban saya sebagai manajer, saya juga mengembangkan alat pengaturan waktu untuk pegawai menggunakan Access and Excel from Microsoft's Office Suite.

(6) Closing paragraph : Paragraf penutup

Thank you for your time and consideration. I look forward to the  opportunity to personally discuss why I am particularly suited for this  position. Please call me after 4.00 p.m. to suggest a time we may meet. I  can be reached via telephone number 031-858564 or by email at lilish@  yahoo.com

Terima kasih atas waktu dan pertimbangannya, saya berharap dapat mendapatkan kesempatan untuk mendiskusikan mengapa saya adalah orang yang tepat untuk posisi ini. Tolong hubungi saya setelah pukul 4.00 p.m untuk menyarankan waktu kita bertemu. Saya dapat dipanggil di nomer

031-858564 atau email saya ilish@  yahoo.com

(7) Signature : Tanda tangan

Sincerely,

Lilis Handayani

Pembahasan

1. To whom is the letter sent? To Personnel Manager of Jeans and Co.

2. Who wrote the application letter? Lilis Handayani

3. What is the purpose of writing the letter? To apply for a job

4. What position is being advertised? Manager of local branch

5. How did Lilis know the vacancy? She knew the information in the Jawa Pos on Sunday,  June 15.

6. What is Lilis' current position? Local branch manager of a national shoe  retailer.

7. What has current position provided with? It provided with the opportunity to work under a high-pressure,  team environment, where it is essential to be able to work closely  with my colleagues in order to meet sales deadlines.

8. What other responsibilities does she have at the moment? She also developed time management tools for  staff using Access and Excel from Microsoft's Office Suite.

9. Do you think that Lilis is confident about her competence? How do you know? Yes, she is. She stated that she look forward to the  opportunity to personally discuss why she is particularly suited for this  position

10. Does Lilis indicate her willingness for an interview? Find the evidence from the text! Yes, she does. She is willing to be called for the interview by giving her contact and email in her letter.

Evidence: Please call me after 4.00 p.m. to suggest a time we may meet. I  can be reached via telephone number 031-858564 or by email at lilish@  yahoo.com

PEMBAHASAN:

Application Letter, also known as a Cover Letter is a formal letter or document sent to an organization or company in order to apply for a job. It is usually sent along with a Resume (Curriculum Vitae) to provide detailed information on why you are qualified for the position you are applying for.

(Surat Lamaran, juga dikenal sebagai Surat Pengantar adalah surat atau dokumen resmi yang dikirim ke suatu organisasi atau perusahaan untuk melamar pekerjaan. Biasanya dikirimkan bersama dengan Resume (Curriculum Vitae) untuk memberikan informasi terperinci tentang mengapa Anda memenuhi syarat untuk posisi yang Anda lamar)

The Social function of application letter is to apply for a particular position offered by a company or an organization.  

(Fungsi Sosial dari surat lamaran adalah untuk melamar posisi tertentu yang ditawarkan oleh perusahaan atau organisasi)
