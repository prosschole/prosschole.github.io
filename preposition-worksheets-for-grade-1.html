17. 1. Apa preposition itu?2. Sebutkan macam-macam preposition?3. Berilah contoh kalimat disetiap macam preposition!Tolong di jawab plis.. solanya mau di kumpulkan sebentar lagi​


Jawaban:

1. Preposition adalah kata yang dikombinasikan dengan noun atau pronoun, membentuk phrase (frasa) yang menerangkan verb, noun, atau adjective.

2. Macam-Macam Preposition:

- Preposition At/In/To/Into

- Preposition On/Onto

- Preposition With/By

- Preposition Since/For/From

- Preposition Beside/Besides

- Preposition Between/Among

- Preposition Among/Amongst

3. Contoh Preposition At/In/To/Into:

- She is at home

- The train is in motion

- There is only one student in the class

- Look to the left! What a beautiful view

- She jumped into the water

- The boy changed into a man

Contoh Preposition On/Onto

- She was sitting on that chair for five minutes

- Snow fell on the hills

- People climbed onto their roofs

Contoh Preposition With/By:

- This letter was written by Charlie with a pencil

Contoh Preposition Since/For/From:

- I had been ill since Monday

- We walked for miles

- She’s out of the office for a few days next week

- Drinks will be served from seven o’clock

Contoh Preposition Beside/Besides:

- He was sitting beside John

- He has a car besides a motor cycle

Contoh Preposition Between/Among:

- We’ll have to portion the money out between the six of us

- I saw a few familiar faces among the crowd

Contoh Preposition Among/Amongst:

- They distributed the foods among/amongst the poor

- They distributed the foods amongst us

Penjelasan (Informasi tambahan):

Penggunaan Preposisi yang Tidak Perlu:

Where is your brother at?

Seperti yang telah dijelaskan dalam aturan penggunaan Preposition bahwa terdapat beberapa preposisi yang terletak pada awal atau bahkan akhir kalimat. Namun, kita perlu hati-hati dalam menggunakannya. Terkadang kita justru terjebak dalam penggunaanya. Kalimat di atas adalah kalimat yang tidak perlu menggunakan preposition. Dengan menambahkan preposition, hal tersebut justru membuat grammatical error pada kalimat tersebut.

Semoga bermanfaat


18. What is preposition ??? Please search for me I want to study but i don't know what preposition


Jawaban:

A preposition is a word used to link nouns, pronouns, or phrases to other words within a sentence. They act to connect the people, objects, time and locations of a sentence.  Prepositions are usually short words, and they are normally placed directly in front of nouns. In some cases, you’ll find prepositions in front of gerund verbs.

Possessive adjectives: my, our, your, his, their, her, and its.

Penjelasan:

Possessive pronouns biasanya ditempatkan di akhir kalimat. Contoh:

This house is mine. (rumah ini adalah milikku).

That cat is yours. (kucing itu adalah milikmu).

Possessive adjectives adalah kata sifat kepemilikan dan diletakkan sebelum kata benda. Contoh:

This is my book. Kata book adalah kata benda (Noun) maka possessive adjective adalah kata yang diletakkan sebelum kata benda, yaitu kata my.

That is your apple. Kata apple adalah kata benda (Noun) maka possessive adjective adalah kata yang diletakkan sebelum kata benda, yaitu your.
