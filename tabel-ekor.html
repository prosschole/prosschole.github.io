360°=180+210+150+320+220

360°=1080

Lele= 180/1080=x/360°

=60°

tawes= 210/1080=x/360°

=70°

mujaher= 150/1080=x/360°

=50°

emas= 320/1080=x/360°

=106,67°

nila= 220/1080=x/360°

=73,33°

60+70+50+106,67+73,33=360°
