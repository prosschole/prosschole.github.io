12. bahasa Inggris class: VIII activity 9​


Jawaban:

1. The smartphone is cheaper than the computer.

2. My Dad is older than my Mom.

3. Rafflesia Arnoldi is the biggest flower in the world.

4. A cheetah runs faster than a deer.

5. Jakarta is much bigger and busier than Semarang.

6. A whale is the largest sea living mammal.

7. Daisy: Do you know the highest structure in the world?

8. But, the dictionary is thicker.

9. My pencil is shorter than yours.

10. Nile river in Africa is the longest river in the world.

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran Degrees of Comparison (derajat perbandingan) dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Degrees of Comparison

Comparative degree dibentuk dengan menambahkan akhiran "-er" untuk satu suku kata. Jika kata sifatnya terdiri lebih dari satu suku kata, maka harus ditambahkan kata more.... than....

Contoh : Tony is younger than i am. (Tony lebih muda dariku)

This novel is more interesting than that novel. (novel ini lebih menarik daripada novel itu)

Superlative degree dengan menambahkan akhiran "-est" dan memiliki arti sebagai yang paling sehingga tidak bisa dibandingkan lagi. Jika kata sifatnya terdiri lebih dari satu suku kata, maka harus ditambahkan kata the most di depan kata sifat (Adjective)nya tersebut.

Contoh : I am the youngest person in my family. (Aku adalah orang termuda / yang paling muda di dalam keluargaku)

We'll try to answer this..

1. Which segment of the business is looking for a sales manager?

(Bagian bisnis mana yang sedang mencari manajer penjualan?)

Di kalimat pertama ada kalimat "A multinational company seeks a sales manager for its office products division."

(Sebuah perusahaan mencari sebuah manajer penjualan untuk divisi produk kantorannya.)

berarti, opsi yang benar adalah A.) The office products division

2. What type of experience is required for the job?

(Apa tipe pengalaman yang dibutuhkan untuk pekerjaanya?)

Di kalimat kedua ada kalimat "Candidate must have 10 years experience in sales development and management in the field and the ability to motivate and train incoming sales staff."

(Kandidat harus memiliki pengalaman 10 tahun di pengembangan penjualan dan manajemen di lapangan dan kemampuan untuk memotivasi dan melatih penjual yang akan datang.")

Berarti, opsi yang benar adalah C. Ten years in office management.

(Kemana pelamar harus mengirim lamaran mereka?)

Di bagian terakhir tertera alamat "The Daily News, Box 8552,1627, Elra Street, Adelaide, Australia" yang menunjukkan alamat kantor di Australia...

Berarti opsi yang benar adalah A. To the company (Ke kantornya langsung)

15. activity 6 lks bahasa inggris kelas 9 halaman 78


Bahasa Inggris adalah salah satu bahasa yang bersifat universal dan berlaku di seluruh dunia sebagai bahasa internasional. Berdasarkan soal, dapat disimpulkan bahwa:

My friend and I are riding a bicycle arround the village now.We actually are living in the city at present.She always obeys the rules in our school.However, she often has a desire to do something out the box.Right now, we are enjoying the green views.Alika wears a shirt inscribed with "I'm bold" on the back side.Everything is going so far.And I hope she isn't do something that I don't expect this time.After cycling we intend to buy fresh fruit.We rarely go to a country side.

16. Bahasa inggris kelas 8 semester 2 activity 13​


Jawaban:

1. Mother: I'll be at the store for a while. I want you to clean the house while I'm away. I'll be back as soon as possible.

Me: Okay, I'll clean the house right away Mom.

2. Me: I can't come to our Japanese course this afternoon. Would you please tell our tutor about my absence, please?

My Friend: Yes, I'd tell him later.

3. Me (Leader): Please forward this message to everyone regarding our working bee plan to clean the environment. Thank you!

Member: Yes, sir! Right away!

4. Me: I'm sorry that I can't come on time to join our English club activity because I have to help my sister do her homework first.

My friend: That's okay, don't worry about that! It's better late than never.

5. Teacher: We are going to have a virtual trip to a zoo tomorrow! I want everyone to prepare their laptop, smartphone and internet connection ready!

Students: Yes teacher!

Penjelasan:

Untuk nomor 1. pola kalimat mengikuti Simple Present Tense & Future tense karena menyatakan kegiatan di masa depan.

(+) Subject + Verb-1 S/ES + Object (Simple Present Tense)

(+) Subject + Will + Verb-1 + Object (Simple Future Tense)

Untuk nomor 2. pola kalimat memakai Modal Verb:

(-) Subject + can + not + Verb-1 + Object

(?) Would + Subject + Verb-1 + Object + ?

Untuk nomor 3. Memakai pola kalimat Imperative:

Polite form: (+) Please, Verb-1 + Subject + Object

Untuk nomor 4. mengikuti pola kalimat Simple Present Tense dan Modal Verb:

Nominal: (+) Subject + Tobe (is/am/are) + Adjective (kata sifat)

Modal Verb: (+) Subject + can + Verb-1 + Object

Untuk nomor 5. mengikuti pola kalimat Simple Future Tense dan Simple Present Tense:

(+) Subject + Tobe (is/am/are) + going to + Verb-1 + Object (Simple Future Tense)

(+) Subject + Verb-1 S/ES + Object

Penjelasan:

Teks prosedur yang berhubungan dengan pertanyaan di atas adalah

PEPES IKAN

(Grilled marinated fish wrapped in Banana leaf)

1. Cut fish fillet into cubes.

2. Season fish fillet with salt, pepper, lime juice and yellow paste.

3. Place the marinated fish cubes on the banana leaf with sliced tomato, lemon basil, salam leaf, and kefir lime leaf on top. Fold into a parcel, securing the ends with toothpicks.

4. Grill over charcoal or sear in a frying pan for about 10 minutes on each side, until the leaf edges are getting charred.

5. Serve with steamed rice and vegetable.
