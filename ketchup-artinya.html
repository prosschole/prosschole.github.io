4. Can you give me ....., please? I want a cup of sweet coffee. *1 poina. vinegarb. sugarc. pepperd. ketchup​


(B) Sugar

PEMBAHASAN

Can you give me ___ please? I want a cup of sweet coffee.

Pada soal, terdapat seseorang meminta suatu barang karena dia ingin kopinya manis. Benda yang memberikan rasa manis salah satunya adalah gula. Gula dalam bahasa Inggris adalah sugar.

➥ So, the answer is B (sugar)

—

Vinegar = Cuka

Cuka memberikan rasa asam.

Sugar = Gula

Gula memberi rasa manis.

Pepper = Lada, cabai

Cabai maupun lada memberi rasa pedas pada suatu makanan.

Ketchup = Kecap

Kecap ada dua jenis. Kecap manis untuk memaniskan makanan dan kecap asin untuk mengasinkan. Di samping itu, kecap bukanlah penambah rasa untuk kopi.

25. Andre (bring) a bottle of ketchup. (+)....... (-)....... (?)...... Ayo dong dijawab kak... Untuk besuk nih kak


Jawaban:

Simple present tense

Andre (bring) a bottle of ketchup.

(+) Andre brings a bottle ketchup

(-) Andre doesn't bring a bottle ketchup

(?) Does Andre bring a bottle ketchup ?

Present Continuous

Andre (bring) a bottle of ketchup.

(+) Andre is bringing a bottle ketchup

(-) Andre is not bringing a bottle ketchup

(?) Is Andre bringing a bottle ketchup ?

Simple past tense

Andre (bring) a bottle of ketchup.

(+) Andre brought a bottle ketchup

(-) Andre did not bring a bottle ketchup

(?) Did Andre bring a bottle ketchup ?

Simple future tense

Andre (bring) a bottle of ketchup.

(+) Andre will bring a bottle ketchup

(-) Andre will not bring a bottle ketchup

(?) Will Andre bring a bottle ketchup ?

Penjelasan:

Simple present tense

Pattern : Subject + Verb1 (s/es) + Object

Present Continuous

Pattern : Subject + be + Verb1-ing + Object

Simple past tense

Pattern : Subject + Verb2 + Object

Simple future tense

Pattern : Subject + Will + Verb1 + Object
