7. Apa arti Stay humble?​


In English, the meaning of stay humble is to keep a low profile, do not boast yourself otherwise you will be considered cocky. In Indonesian, it can be translated as 'tetap rendah hati'.

Penjelasan:

To find the meaning of a word or a phrase, you can always rely on the dictionary, or if you have to do it in tjr middle of reading long passage, using dictionary might not be allowed and it consumes too much of your time. Thus, guessing the meaning from the context can be helpful too but it needs some practice for it.

For the phrase 'stay humble', it is quite easy to find its meaning as the word 'humble' have a straight forward meaning. Such phrase is often uttered as an advice to anyone who might experience a stunning success or quick fortune. Such often overwhelmes people and they often boast or show off easily to others without realising it. The usage of 'stay' here means to keep things on a course, not to ruin or blow it out of proportion. Here's the example:

Try to stay humble even if you have been loaded and full of fortune

In Indonesian, there are several phrases for translating it

tetap rendah hatiselalu merendahkan hatijangan sombongjangan tinggi hatiselalu bersahaja

21. apakah arti stay safe​


The phrase 'stay safe' is one of the most common and recently popular expression in this outbreak period. It is normally uttered among people to one another to show their care.  The meaning of this phrase is 'jaga diri'.

Penjelasan:

In doing translation work, one has to keep in mind about doing it properly. It means one needs to ponder the main elements in translation. They are

context in the target language and in the original onevocabulary

Translating the phrase 'stay safe' is quite tricky. If one does literal translation to find its meaning it would sound absurd. Here's the example:

'stay' is tinggal/ menginap

'safe' is aman

but the meaning is not 'menginap/ tinggal aman'. One has to see the context first. The easy way to do it is by deciding from the situation the people. People tend to say it as a hope for the others to take care of themselves and become safe everywhere. So the proper meaning in Indonesian language would be 'jaga diri' or 'jaga diri baik-baik'.

25. apa arti dari stay strong?


Arti dari ungkapan "stay strong" adalah "tetap teguh".

[tex]\\[/tex]

PEMBAHASAN

Ungkapan "stay strong" merupakan ungkapan dalam bahasa Inggris yang digunakan untuk memberi dukungan atau semangat kepada seseorang atau sekelompok orang.

Ungkapan tersebut merupakan kalimat yang menggunakan imperative mood (modus imperatif) yang artinya kata kerja yang digunakan dalam kalimat digunakan dengan maksud menyatakan arahan, permintaan, perintah, larangan, dsb. Dalam bahasa Inggris, kalimat yang menggunakan modus imperative memiliki struktur sebagai berikut.

[+] (You) + Verb 1 (+ Complement). Contohnya:

Shut up!Let's go!Turn down the volume, please.Put tea into teapot and add hot water.Let him play with his friends.

[-]  (You) + do + not + Verb 1 (+ Complement). Contohnya:

Don't go!Do not smoke here.Do not ever pop your pimples yourself!Don't listen to him! Don't cry, sweetie.

Bisa dilihat bahwa kalimat yang menggunakan modus imperatif dalam bahasa Inggris, subjeknya selalu orang kedua (second person). Subjek kalimatnya—yaitu you—sangat jarang disebutkan karena sudah dipahami bahwa subjek kalimat bermodus imperatif dalam bahasa Inggris selalu adalah pronomina orang kedua.

26. Apa arti ungkapan"cool beans"?


Sesuatu yang keren dan bersifat baik( tidak negatif) ungkapan cool beans populer diungkapkan oleh BONO U2. cool beans bukan berarti sesuatu yang luar biasa (cool diartikan bagus, luar biasa), tetapi merupakan suatu ironi yang menyindir diri sendiri. jadi, jika berkata 'cool beans' bukan berarti yang mengatakan dirinya keren atau luar biasa, tapi dia sedang mengatakan dirinya jauh dari keren atau tidak luar biasa sama sekali.
