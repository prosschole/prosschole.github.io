Berikut ini adalah satu contoh cerita dengan menggunakan past tense dan present perfect.

The head coach of Barcelona, Xavi called up his players the day after Blaugrana's devastating loss at home while playing host to Cadiz.

"Lads, what on earth was going up there?  Did you realise what it cost for our defeat on the weekend? Look, I know our team is not in their ideal best but I expected you to make a positive response there."

"Coach, my apology, we were exhausted and just unlucky to break the deadlock.", said Barca's new signings, Auba.

"Yes, coach, I knew we lost our chance for stopping Madrid from snatching the silverware early but we were just unfortunate.", responded Ousmane.

"Sorry, lads, but you are in FC Barcelona. Unlucky is not the reason. This is the elite and if you wanna be apart of the elite, you must prepare to suffer for the high demands here.", replied Xavi.

"Fellows, our coach is right. OK we lost against Frankfurt and last weekend versus Cadiz. Yet, in Barcelona, we can not afford to lose again. If you want to be apart of Blaugrana's history, we should bounce back in San Sebastian later.", added the captain, Gerard.

They then started their Monday training session and Xavi talked to his assistant about who shall be in the line up. He then had a conversation with Gerard regarding his injury.

Finally, the D-day has come. The team left Camp Nou early morning to San Sebastian and immediately did the final session of the tactical training on Thursday afternoon. Xavi then met his team before getting ready.

"OK, lads, we've come this far, made a great progress this year and beaten Madrid in Bernabeu. Now go out there and show Sociedad who we really are. Vamos!!!", said the coach.

"Vamos!!!", replied his men.

Penjelasan:

Cerita yang menggunakan past tense dan present perfect adalah jenis cerita yang mengandung peristiwa masa lampau dan kegiatan, pencapaian atau hal yang telah dilakukan. Maka dari itu kisah tentang sebuah pengalaman atau pencapaian seseorang adalah yang paling pas.

Berikut ini adalah pola dari dua tenses tersebut.

Past tense: S + Verb 2 + O + complementPresent perfect tense: S + have/has + V3 + complement.

9. Contoh present perfect


•ᴥ• Materi: Simple Present Perfect Tense

–·––·––·––·––·––·––·––·––·––·––·––·–

I have drink a glass of milk. I haven't drink a glass of milk. Have I drink a glass of milk? He has finished his task. He hasn't finished his task. Has he finished his task?

PEMBAHASAN

① Definisi dan Kegunaan

Simple present perfect berfungsi untuk menyatakan/mengungkapkan suatu kegiatan/peristiwa yang telah dilakukan di masa lampau namun masih berlangsung hingga sekarang atau sudah selesai pada waktu tertentu namun efeknya masih berlanjut.

② Pola Kalimat

(+) S + has/have + V3 + O/C.(–) S + has/have + not + V3 + O/C.(?) Has/have + S + V3 + O/C + ?

③ Penjelasan

Pada present perfect, kata kerja (verb) yang digunakan adalah bentuk ketiga (V3).'Has' digunakan untuk subjek: he, she, it, dan nama tunggal.'Have' digunakan untuk subjek: I, you, we, they, dan nama banyak.

④ Contoh Kalimat Berdasarkan Fungsinya

•ᴥ• Mengungkapkan suatu kegiatan/peristiwa yang meskipun telah dilakukan/terjadi namun masih ada akibat yang dirasakan dari aksi tersebut saat pembicaraan berlangsung.

► I have eaten. (Aku sudah makan.)

Saat kalimat di atas diucapkan, akibat yang dirasakan oleh si subjek kalimat adalah ia masih merasa kenyang karena telah makan sebelumnya.

► They have swept the floor. (Mereka sudah menyapu lantai.)

Dampak dari aksi di atas, lantai yang masih bersih karena telah disapu sebelumnya.

•ᴥ• Menyatakan suatu kegiatan/peristiwa yang telah terjadi pada suatu waktu di masa lampau namun masih berkaitan dengan masa sekarang.

► We have lived here since 2021. (Kami sudah tinggal di sini sejak tahun 2021.)

Saat kalimat tersebut diucapkan, si subjek kalimat masih tinggal di tempat yang dimaksud.

► We have made these crafts for 2 hours. (Kami sudah membuat kerajinan tangan ini selama 2 jam.)

Saat kalimat di atas diucapkan, subjek kalimat masih membuat kerajinan tangan tersebut.

•ᴥ• Menyatakan suatu kegiatan yang sudah dilakukan, tetapi belum terselesaikan hingga saat ini.

He has not finished his tasks yet. (Dia belum menyelesaikan tugasnya.)

•ᴥ•  Membicarakan aktifitas/peristiwa yang baru saja selesai/berakhir.

He has just finished his tasks. (Dia baru saja menyelesaikan tugasnya.)

•ᴥ• Menceritakan tentang pengalaman namun tidak spesifik.

I have eaten too many eggs three times. Have you ever done it? (Aku pernah makan terlalu banyak telur. Apakah kamu pernah melakukannya?)

•ᴥ• Menyatakan kegiatan/peristiwa yang berulang antara masa lalu dan masa sekarang, waktu pada bentuk ini tidak dijelaskan secara spesifik.

We have visited our grandmother's house frequently. (Kami sering mengunjungi rumah nenek kami.)

•ᴥ• Membicarakan suatu pencapaian.

He is a good doctor, he has cured many Covid-19 patients. (Beliau adalah dokter yang hebat, beliau telah menyembuhkan banyak pasien Covid-19.)

•ᴥ• Membicarakan perubahan.

My situation has significantly improved since I decided to forgive the people who had hurt me. (Keadaan saya benar-benar membaik ketika saya memutuskan untuk memaafkan orang-orang yang telah menyakiti saya.)

•ᴥ• Menceritakan kegiatan/peristiwa yang telah terjadi pada periode waktu yang berbeda.

The cat has looked for the kitten for ten minutes, but the cat can't find it. So, let's help. (Kucing itu sudah mencari-cari anaknya selama sepuluh menit, tapi dia tidak menemukannya. Jadi, mari kita bantu.)

16. buatkan cerita yang mencakup simple present, present continous, present perfect, present perfect continous, simple past, past continous, past perfect, past perfect continous


Explanation:

- Simple present tense
This tense is used to tell about an action that happens at the present time or habitual activity. It also can be used to tell about general truth.
The structure: S + V1 (+ s/es) + O

- Present continuous tense
This tense is used to tell about something that is in progress right now.
The structure: S + to be (am / is / are) + O

- Present perfect tense
This tense is used to tell that the action is completely done in an unspecific time before now.
The structure: S + has/have + V3 + O

- Present perfect continuous tense
This tense is used to tell that the action starts at an unspecific time in the past and continue to the present.
The structure: S + has/have + been + O

- Simple past tense
This tense is used to indicate an action that is happened in the past. It began and ended in the past.
The structure: S + V2 + O

- Past continuous tense
This tense is used to indicate an action that was in progress at a particular time in the past.
The structure: S + to be (was/were) + V-ing + O

- Past perfect tense
This tense is used to indicate an action which was completely done before another time in the past.
The structure: S + had + V3 + O

- Past perfect continuous tense
This tense is used to indicate an action which was in progress before another event in the past. This tense is used to show the duration of the first event.
The structure: S + had + been + V-ing + O

PEMBAHASANSimple Past Perfect

•ᴥ• Definisi dan Fungsi:

Digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu kegiatan/pekerjaan/peristiwa yang telah selesai dilakukan di masa yang telah berlalu sebelum aksi yang lainnya terjadi. Dengan kata lain, past perfect memiliki ciri di mana waktu terjadinya suatu kegiatan/peristiwa dinyatakan dengan jelas. Contoh (dalam bahasa Indonesia):

Kami sudah mengunjungi nenek sebelum kami berangkat ke Bali.

•ᴥ• Pattern:

Simple Present Perfect Continuous Tense

•ᴥ• Definisi dan Fungsi:

Digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu kegiatan/pekerjaan/peristiwa yang telah dilakukan di masa yang telah berlalu namun masih berlangsung sampai saat ini. Contoh (dalam bahasa Indonesia):

Kami (sudah) sedang menyelesaikan karya tulis untuk Desember mendatang.

— Ini berarti, proses penyelesaian karya tulis tersebut sudah dilakukan sejak lama dan masih berlangsung hingga saat ini.

•ᴥ• Pattern:

(+) S + has/have + been + V-ing + O/C(–) S + has/have + not + been + V-ing + O/C(?) Has/Have + S + been + V-ing + O/C + ?

•ᴥ• Keterangan:

“Has” digunakan untuk subjek: he, she, it, dan nama makhluk/benda tunggal. “Have” digunakan untuk subjek: I, you, we, they, dan nama makhluk/benda banyak. ∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙∙Detail Jawaban:

Haunted theatre
Last week, me and my family went to XXI in Kuningan city mall of Jakarta. When we arrived at the mall, we directly rushed to XXI cinema and bought the tickets. After we bought the tickets, we went to have lunch. We had our lunch in Ichiban sushi. We checked the time and it's our turn to watch so we decided to hurry and go to the cinema. When we arrived, the cinema was very deserted and quiet. Then we finally realized that there was no one. Not a single soul except the silent waiter, the ticket cashier and us. The atmosphere was really quiet and my mother was scared so we entered the the studio quickly. Inside the studio, there was no one again. I started to feel the chills and cringed. Not long later the lamps was turned off and the movie began. During the movie, I felt something or someone touched me. I was curious so I looked behind. I saw a shadow of a man without the person. I thought my minds was playing tricks with me so i just forget about the shadow and enjoyed the movie. After the movie finished, I asked my mother and father whether they had felt someone touched them. They said that they also felt the same. Suddenly, a nearby lamp explodes by itself! Me and my family was really shocked and ran away to the parking lot. We rushed home as fast as we can. At home, I did a research about the cinema and found out that there was a person called Mr. Jenovocic who died during the fire in the cinema. When I read the review, people said that his shadow and soul never left the cinema and always appear on 23rd May, the day he died. I also found out that 23rd May was the day when I and my family went there. I faced left and right to check if there are any shadows. Fortunately, there are no shadows. But, I forgot to face behind. When I faced behind I saw a shadow and went into a coma for two whole days. When I woke up I realized that shadow was my own shadow.

Penjelasan:

PRESENT PERFECT TENSEPresent perfect tense reveals the action happened ( or never happened ) before now , at an unspecified time in the past. The exact time the action happened is not necessary to tell. Present perfect time also reveals the repetition of an activity before now.So, present perfect tense mendiskripsikan suatu kejadian yang telah terjadi  di masa lalu dan masih ada kaitannya sampai saat ini  serta akan diselesaikan baru baru ini. Present perfect tense mendiskripsikan kejadian yang pernah terjadi ataupun belum pernah terjadi  sama sekali sebelum saat ini,  pada waktu yang tidak ditentukan di masa lalu. Pernyataan tepatnya waktu kejadian tidak dianggap suatu keharusan dijabarkan dalam present perfect tense. Present perfect tense lebih menjabarkan pada keterkaitan dan kesinambungan dari suatu tindakan sampai saat ini.Pada umumnya menggunakan keterangan waktu seperti : since, for, ever,never,already, yet, still, and just.

PATTERN :

(+) Subject + has/have + verb.3 + O / C                 

(-)  Subject + has/have + not + verb.3 + O /C        

( ?) Has/have + Subject + Verb.3 + O/C?               

Contoh :

I have never seen snow
