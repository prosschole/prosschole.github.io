12. how to make Tofu crispy ​


Step 1: Preheat oven to 375 F (190 C) and wrap your extra firm tofu (this is important – anything less than extra firm won’t crisp up as fast or as well) and set something heavy on top like a cast iron skillet.

Step 2: Cube tofu and add to a mixing bowl. Season with salt and, depending on what type of dish you’re adding it to, season accordingly (i.e. curry powder for a curry dish, or a bit of tamari and/or chili garlic sauce for a stir-fry). I was adding mine to a green curry (coming later this week!), so I went with curry powder.

Step 3: Sauté in oil over medium heat for about 5 minutes or until browned on all sides (shaking the pan as needed to cook all sides).

Step 4: Finish baking in the oven for about 15 minutes or until crispy and golden brown.

Jawaban:

Bahan-bahan

1/2 ekor ayam (1 ekor aku belah 2) dipotong kecil2 kayak buat sop atau selera ya

Bahan pencelup:

4 sdm tepung

1 sdm tepung tapioka

2 sdm tepung instan sasa kentucky

sejumput soda kue

air es atau ditambah es batu boleh

lada bubuk

garam dan kaldu ayam bubuk

1 butir telur kocok lepas

4 siung bawang putih haluskan

Bahan lumuran:

1 saset tepung instan sasa kentucky (yg kecil)

1 sdm tepung tapioka

1 sdm tepung terigu

lada bubuk secukupnya (dikira2 aja)

secukupnya kaldu ayam bubuk dan garam

soda kue sejumput (dikit aja)

Langkah

Cuci ayam, kucuri jeruk nipis dan garam, diamkan -+ 15 menit, lalu bilas bersih kembali tiriskan.

Siapkan dlm wadah berbeda bahan pencelup, (larutkan semua bahan dgn air dingin (boleh pake es batu) jika sudah, baru masukan telur yg sudah dikocok lepas, biar gak grindil) dan masukan ayam, aduk merata, simpan dikulkas -+30 menit biar bumbu meresap, tp kalo buru2 boleh langsung dimasak.

PENTING!! lumuri ayam dg adonan kering pada saat mau digoreng saja, jangan nyetok ayam dilumuri adonan kering pd saat menunggu ayam pertama digoreng, nanti blenyek dan gak bisa krispy

19. Crispy hash brown recipe


Ingredients:

2 medium russet potatoes, peeled and shredded

1 egg

1/4 cup shredded parmesan

3 Tablespoons chives, thinly sliced

2 Tablespoons all-purpose flour

2 teaspoons seasoned salt

1/2 teaspoon chile powder

1/2 teaspoon dried basil

1/4 teaspoon ground cumin

1/4 teaspoon black pepper

1 cup oil for frying, or as needed

Directions:

Rinse shredded potatoes until water is clear, then drain and squeeze dry. Place shreds in a large bowl.

In a small bowl, whisk together egg, parmesan, chives, flour, seasoned salt, chile powder, basil, cumin, and pepper. Add to shredded potatoes and mix until evenly distributed.

Heat about 1/4 inch of oil in a large heavy skillet over medium-high heat. When oil is sizzling hot, scoops out 1/4 cup potato mixture into the pan and flatten to a 1/2 inch thick layer. Cook until nicely browned on the bottom, then flip over and brown on the other side. It should take at least 5 minutes per side.

Remove from pan, and drain on paper towels. Season with a little extra salt and pepper, if desired, and serve immediately.
