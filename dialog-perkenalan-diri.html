24. Dialog memperkenalkan teman kepada saudara


Dialog memperkenalkan teman kepada saudara adalah menggunakan ungkapan introducing oneself and other. Beberapa contoh kalimat yang bisa kalian gunakan adalah:

Introducing oneself

My name is...Hello, I'm...Nice to meet you, I'm...Let me introduce myself, my name is...Hey, I'd like to introduce myself. I'm...

Introducting others

Hana, please meet TinaHana, have you met Tina?I'd like you to meet HansI'd like to introduce you to AlanHans this is Alan. Alan, this is Hans.
Pembahasan

Paula: Hey, Klara! How are you long time no see?

Klara: Hey, Paula! I'm great how are you?

Paula: I'm good too. I was buying an ice cream and saw you were passing, so I decided to greet.

Klara: It's great that your greeting me! Oh by the way this is Alan my cousin. Alan, this is Paula my friend.

Alan: Hello Paula nice to meet you.

Paula: Nice to meet you too, Alan

Paula: Oh, apparently I have to go now. My mother is calling. Bye guys, take care.

Klara and Alan: Bye Paula!

30. dialog memperkenalkan kampus


Dialog memperkenalkan kampus dalam Bahasa Inggris.
Contoh percakapan 2 orang yang memperkenalkan kampus seperti berikut ini:

Tania: Welcome to Dale University! I'm Tania and I will give you a quick tour of the university.
Paul: Awesome! I've always wanted to see the campus!
Tania: First, we are going to see the Music Department building. 
Paul: I heard it is the biggest building in the campus. Is that right?
Tania: Yes, music is one of our strongest departments in this university. A lot of donors have given us money to build a state-of-the-art building. We also have 4 concert halls inside the building. You can see the building on your left side.
Paul: Wow! the building looks so cool! what about the science department? I'm actually interested in taking science major.
Tania: Follow me please....... as you can see, the building with the triangular roof over there is the Science Department. Just recently, one of our professors received a Nobel Prize in physics!
Paul: Oh yeah! I've heard about that! Is it possible for me to check the science department out?
Tania: Yes, you can check it out later but now I will show you where the students live. We are going to see the university dormitory.
Paul: If I stay there, will I get my own room?
Tania: Sorry, you will have to share a room with a roommate. The university doesn't offer a single room, this is due to limited space.
Paul: oh ok, I understand. Is the building in front of us the dormitory?
Tania: Yes, it is. As you can see, we have 5 apartment buildings that were transformed into a dormitory.
Paul: That place looks decent. I like it.
Tania: Next, we will go to the student hall for an introductory speech from the headmaster. Follow me please. It's this way.
Paul: Okay.
