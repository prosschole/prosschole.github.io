3. contoh biodata dalam bahasa Sunda


Bahasa Sunda adalah salah satu jenis bahasa yang dipakai dalam komunikasi sehari-hari masyarakat dalam berinteraksi antar sesama manusia yang ada di Jawa Barat dan sekitarnya. Contoh biodata dalam bahasa Sunda antara lain sebagai berikut:

Pembahasan:

Manusia selalu berinteraksi dengan manusia lainnya menggunakan bahasa masing-masing daerah. Setiap daerah memiliki bahasa daerah yang berbeda-beda. Bahasa daerah tersebut memiliki keunikan dan ciri khas masing-masing. Salah satunya yaitu Bahasa Sunda. Sikap yang harus kita kembangkan untuk menghadapi keberagaman bahasa tersebut yaitu tenggang rasa dan toleransi. 6. biodata koala bahasa inggris


Koala (Phascolarctos cinereus) is one of the marsupials (marsupial) typical of Australia and is the sole representative of the family Phascolarctidae.

In general, many say that the word koala comes from indigenous Australia, which means not drinking. Koala actually drinking water but is very rare because of the food, eucalyptus leaves, it contains enough water so that the koala does not have to come down from the trees to drink.

Koala can be found along the east coast of Australia from Adelaide to Cape York Peninsula, and as far inland as there is adequate rainfall to support forests suitable for koalaKoala like wombat (their closest relatives), but has a fur thicker and soft, larger ears, hands and feet longer equipped with a large and long claws to help her climb.

Weighing range from 14 kg to a large southern male, to 5 kg for a small northern female. They are usually silent, but the male koala has a strong tailwind screams that can be heard almost a kilometer in the mating season.
Ecology and behavior
Looks a koala was eating eucalyptus leaves.

Koalas live only on eucalyptus leaves. Eucalyptus leaves contain low amounts of protein, undigested substances in high levels, and contain phenol and terpene compounds that are toxic to other species. Such as wombats and lemurs, koalas have a low metabolic rate for a mammal and the rest motionless about 20 hours a day, and most of the time used for sleeping. They eat does not depend on time, but usually at night. Koala generally consume 500 grams of eucalyptus leaves per day, chewing them until it becomes a smooth paste before swallowing. His heart separates toxic ingredients and ready to be discharged, and "hind gut" her (particularly the cecum) were more likely to take the maximum amount of nutrients from food is low quality.

9. Biodata dalam bahasa korea


바이오 데이 타 = baio dei ta

Halo! = 안녕하세요 (Annyeonghaseyo)


Sepertinya hampir semua orang sudah tahu kalimat ini artinya apa. Jika kita ingin menjawab salam, kita dapat mengucapkan 안녕하세요(an-nyeong-ha-se-yo) balik kepada lawan bicara. Jangan sekali-kali menggunakan kata 안녕 (an-nyeong) kepada orang yang baru kalian kenal. Please jangan, itu tidak sopan.


●Nama saya adalah … = 제 이름은 …. 입니다 (Je i-reum-eun…im-ni-da).


Mungkin ungkapan ini terdengar kurang natural ya, jadi lebih baik menggunakan :


저는….입니다 (Jeo-neun…im-ni-da) = Saya adalah…


Dua kalimat diatas merupakan kalimat formal (bisa dilihat dari penggunaan akhiran -imnida), kalau kalian ingin menggunakan yang semi-formal (masih sopan juga) pakai kalimat-kalimat ini :

제 이름은 … 이에요/예요 (je i-reum-eun ….i-e-yo/ye-yo)

atau

저는 …. 이에요/예요 (jeo-neun … i-e-yo/ye-yo)

*note :

Jika nama kalian berakhir dengan 받침 (konsonan di akhir suku kata) semisal 정신 (jeong-shin) — lihat suku kata terakhir “신” (sin), dia diakhiri “ㄴ” (n) yang merupakan konsonan — maka gunakan 이에요 (i-e-yo). Jika tidak diakhiri 받침 (batchim) misalnya 현기 (hyeon-gi) maka gunakan 예요 (ye-yo).


Cara lain untuk memperkenalkan nama :

저는 ….(이)라고합니다/(이)라고해요 (jeo-neun … (i)-ra-go-ham-ni-da/(i)-ra-go-hae-yo) yang artinya semacam “orang memanggil saya … ”

*note :

Sama seperti di atas, gunakan tambahan 이(i) dibelakang nama kalian jika nama kalian diakhiri 받침(batchim).
