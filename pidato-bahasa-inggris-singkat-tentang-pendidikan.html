Globalisasi berasal dari kata global yang memiliki arti menyeluruh. Globalisasi berarti proses penyebaran unsur-unsur baru yang mencangkup informasi dari seluruh dunia melalui media cetak atau elektronik.

Globalisasi mempunyai pengaruh yang hampir meliputi semua aspek lini kehidupan.

Misalnya, dalam bidang transportasi. Ada begitu banyak kendaraan yang dapat kita jumpai di jalan raya setiap harinya. Transportasi ini membuat orang lebih mudah untuk melakukan aktivitas ataupun pekerjaannya.

Hal ini tentu berbanding terbalik dengan zaman dahulu yang untuk ke suatu tempat yang diinginkan, seseorang harus rela menempuh perjalanan dengan jalan kaki.

Begitu banyak aspek yang mampu dipengaruhi globalisasi. Hal ini jelas sebagai bukti bahwa setiap masyarakat mampu merasakan pengaruhnya.

Beberapa aspek yang telah dijelaskan di atas, menandakan bahwasanya pengaruh global sudah hampir merasuk dalam seluruh lini aspek kehidupan.

Semua orang tentunya merasakan pengaruh yang terjadi akibat globalisasi. In praise of Islamic civilization
Speech of Carly Florina (CEO Hewlett Packard)

There was once a civilization that was the greatest in the world.

It was able to create a continental super-state that stretched from ocean to ocean, and from northern climes to tropics and deserts. Within its dominion lived hundreds of millions of people, of different creeds and ethnic origins.

One of its languages became the universal language of much of the world, the bridge between the peoples of a hundred lands. Its armies were made up of people of many nationalities, and its military protection allowed a degree of peace and prosperity that had never been known. The reach of this civilization’s commerce extended from Latin America to China, and everywhere in between.

And this civilization was driven more than anything, by invention. Its architects designed buildings that defied gravity. Its mathematicians created the algebra and algorithms that would enable the building of computers, and the creation of encryption. Its doctors examined the human body, and found new cures for disease. Its astronomers looked into the heavens, named the stars, and paved the way for space travel and exploration.

Its writers created thousands of stories. Stories of courage, romance and magic. Its poets wrote of love, when others before them were too steeped in fear to think of such things.

When other nations were afraid of ideas, this civilization thrived on them, and kept them alive. When censors threatened to wipe out knowledge from past civilizations, this civilization kept the knowledge alive, and passed it on to others.

While modern Western civilization shares many of these traits, the civilization I’m talking about was the Islamic world from the year 800 to 1600, which included the Ottoman Empire and the courts of Baghdad, Damascus and Cairo, and enlightened rulers like Suleiman the Magnificent.

Although we are often unaware of our indebtedness to this other civilization, its gifts are very much a part of our heritage. The technology industry would not exist without the contributions of Arab mathematicians. Sufi poet-philosophers like Rumi challenged our notions of self and truth. Leaders like Suleiman contributed to our notions of tolerance and civic leadership.

And perhaps we can learn a lesson from his example: It was leadership based on meritocracy, not inheritance. It was leadership that harnessed the full capabilities of a very diverse population–that included Christianity, Islamic, and Jewish traditions.

This kind of enlightened leadership — leadership that nurtured culture, sustainability, diversity and courage — led to 800 years of invention and prosperity.

In dark and serious times like this, we must affirm our commitment to building societies and institutions that aspire to this kind of greatness. More than ever, we must focus on the importance of leadership– bold acts of leadership and decidedly personal acts of leadership.

Dear friends,

Education is not just about literacy, it is about having a goal - the goal of equipping an individual to face life well. Education is not just having academic knowledge about school subjects, but it also covers the holistic development of a student - intellectual, emotional, spiritual, physical, and cultural.

One of the hallmarks of a good education is how it has helped an individual put the knowledge he has gained into practical use. Without this practical wisdom, we could say the education is a failure. This practical wisdom helps people aware of civic responsibility.

Let’s take the awareness to throw garbage properly as an example. If educated people keep thoughtlessly throw garbage on the roadside or public area, then what is the point of his education?

Unfortunately, our education system has become a business in many circles. That adds to the cut-throat spirit of competition in the marketplace. On the other hand, education should vigorously aim to instill moral values in students.

As a final point, let me say that teachers and education should enable an individual to see, observe, think, talk, and act for the good of the community. Without the development of integrity, character, and sense mutual respect; education cannot be said to have hit the mark. But when it ignites the mind and inspires the heart, then the shine of truly light will conquer the darkness!.

That is all and thank you.

Artinya :

Teman-teman,

Pendidikan bukan hanya tentang melek huruf, ini tentang memiliki tujuan - tujuan membekali individu untuk menghadapi kehidupan dengan baik. Pendidikan tidak hanya memiliki pengetahuan akademik tentang mata pelajaran sekolah, tetapi juga mencakup perkembangan holistik siswa - intelektual, emosional, spiritual, fisik, dan budaya.

Salah satu keunggulan dari pendidikan yang baik adalah bagaimana hal itu membantu seseorang menggunakan pengetahuan yang diperolehnya untuk digunakan secara praktis. Tanpa kebijaksanaan praktis ini, kita bisa mengatakan pendidikan tersebut gagal. Kebijaksanaan praktis ini membantu orang sadar akan tanggung jawab sebagai warga.

Kita ambil contoh saja kesadaran untuk membuang sampah pada tempatnya. Jika orang terpelajar terus membuang sampah sembarangan di pinggir jalan atau area publik, lalu apa gunanya pendidikannya?

Sayangnya, sistem pendidikan kita telah menjadi bisnis bagi banyak kalangan. Hal tersebut menambah ketatnya kompetisi yang di persaingan pasar. Di sisi lain, pendidikan harus dengan penuh semangat bertujuan untuk menanamkan nilai-nilai moral pada siswa.

Sebagai poin terakhir, perkenankan saya mengatakan bahwa guru dan pendidikan harus memungkinkan seseorang mampu untuk melihat, mengamati, berpikir, berbicara, dan bertindak untuk kebaikan masyarakat. Tanpa pengembangan integritas, karakter, dan rasa saling menghormati; pendidikan tidak bisa dikatakan telah mengenai sasaran. Tetapi ketika hal tersebut menyadarkan pikiran dan mengilhami hati, maka sinar terang yang sejati akan mengalahkan kegelapan!.

Sekian terima kasih.
