Semarang-Tuban = 5 jam
Tuban-Surabaya = 2 jam

Maka
Solo-Surabaya =
Solo-Semarang + Semarang-Tuban
+ Tuban-Surabaya =
3+5+2 jam=
10 jam

Tuban - Surabaya = 2 Jam

= 3 + 5 + 2

= 8 + 2

= 10 Jam

07.00 + 04.30 = 11.30
