This is my story when I went to study in an English village with my school friends. An experience that will never be forgotten.

Class 26 Bosowa Bina Insani Middle School departs on Thursday, April 4 2019. We settled in the English Village of Bandung for 10 days. We departed from Ampera at 7:30 a.m. and arrived at Pondok Lembang around 1 o'clock. Arriving there we immediately carried out zhuhur prayers followed by lunch and sharing rooms. Male students settled in Maribaya Guest House for 3 days and we studied there also only consisted of 4 groups for 3 days of adjustment. The tutors there are so fun that every time we study is always taken relaxed and also after each study is always interspersed with exciting games. My group is called Man-Squad and we got a female tutor named Ms. Shintya. Day 3, the last day of the adjustment period. In the evening we performed by singing English songs per study group which was divided into two, that night was a quite memorable night. After the adjustment period is complete, we meet with several new tutors. We also began to be grouped according to our abilities, I got a 1B group with a tutor named Ms. Naury and Ms. Indri. Our group 1B named Lucifer. 7 days with Lucifer is an experience that I will remember until old age. Our 4th day to 7th day and the 9th day we live as usual, but for us it is precious days because we will not meet again with exciting tutors. And also on the 5th day some of the tutors we met had to stop teaching us, because the time was up, from some of the tutors, Ms. Shintya and Ms. Naury must go home. It feels very sad because Ms. Shintya is a tutor who can enliven the atmosphere when we feel bored, as well as Ms. Naury. Ms. Naury is a very talented person who is a teacher, every thing he teaches is very easy for us to digest. Day 8, we conducted a city tour in Bandung, where we visited the Bandung Future Plan Museum, then to the Great Mosque of Bandung. We also interviewed traders at Parahiyangan Plaza, then we played on Ciwalk. Day 10, a sad day because we had to part with the tutors, on the day of farewell, we performed English-language drama, there was a drama about friendship, even some about romance after everything was over we were greeting and getting ready to go home. That's a story that I will never forget.

Drama adalah sebuah karya sastra yang dibawakan secara teatrikal. Drama dapat berupa cerita yang diadaptasi dari kisah nyata, fiksi atau legenda yang kemudian diperankan oleh aktor dan dihadirkan di hadapan penonton. Drama tutur merupakan jenis drama yang lebih menekankan pada dialog antar karakter dan penokohan. Dalam drama tutur, para aktor akan berbicara secara bergantian dan mengutarakan perasaan, pendapat atau reaksi mereka atas situasi yang terjadi dalam cerita.

Drama rakyat merupakan jenis drama yang berasal dari tradisi rakyat suatu daerah atau negara. Drama rakyat biasanya mengandung unsur-unsur mitologi, legenda atau cerita rakyat dan memiliki fungsi sosial juga ritual dalam kehidupan masyarakatnya. Drama rakyat bisa pula berfungsi sebagai media penyampaian nilai-nilai budaya dan moral untuk generasi muda. Contoh drama rakyat di Indonesia antara lain wayang kulit, randai dan reog.
