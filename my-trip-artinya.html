13. Buatlah Karangan Yang berjudul My Trip dengan menggunakan Future Tense?


Contoh:

My Trip

Hi, my name is (nama). I am going to have a long holiday next month. It will be about two weeks. My family and I are going to go to (tujuan). We are going to spend five days there. We have planned a trip to (tujuan) since January.

(tujuan) is my first place abroad that I will travel to. I am so excited about it. This month we will arrange our trip itineary. So, after school I’ll browse some places to visit. I’ll also watch some (tujuan) travel videos.

(tempat wisata) is a place that will be put in our first list. Besides, we’ll also go to (tempat wisata) because my brother really wants it. I am sure we’ll have so much fun there.

p.s: tujuan, adalah kota/negara yg akan dikunjungi

tempat wisata, adalah tempat di kota/ negara trsbt yg ingin dikunjungi

17. essay about a trip to our my grandfather orchard​


(Remember you have to use simple past tense when you want to write an essay about recount text. Ok?)

   A trip to our my grandfather orchard​

  Last holiday, I went to my grandfather's village in Desa Oetune. My grandfather lived with my grandmother, they have very large fruit orchards, there are many kinds of fruits; watermelons, oranges, rambutans, grapes, and melons. My grandparents really love gardening.

  It was a school holiday, my family and I prepared to go to the village and I was really happy, we went there by my father's car because we lived in the downtown so it just need maybe 9 hours to get there.

 On our trip we through the hill roads and I could see the view of the downtown from the high above and it was very amazing, the weather was cool and it made me cold then my mother hugged me, suddenly I slept for 5 hours and when I woke up we have arrived at my grandparents' village and I was really happy then I ran to my grandfather, he hugged me and smiled so happy.

 Then he took me to his orchards and I felt so amazed because there were many fruits and I asked him to pick the rambutans together. I ran and picked the rambutans and also ate a lot. I love this trip, this village and of course my grandparents' orchard.

Jawaban:

I went to my grandfather's home last week in Malang city with my family. He has a wide apple garden nearby his home. There are lots of apple trees, almost 100 trees which was planted. The other plants that grew in the garden are tomatoes, chili peppers, and eggplants.

Fortunately, it was harvesting season, so we helped our grandfather to pick apples. I picked lots of apples, but I did not know which one has ripened or not because all apples are green and small. Nevertheless, my father involved up to harvest apples, he got almost 10 baskets of ripe apples for a day.

Harvesting apples in my grandfather garden was a nice vacation during school holiday.

24. my wonderful trip tolong translate ke bahasa Indonesia ya kawan


Jawaban:

Perjalanan Hebatku

Minggu lalu saudara saya dan saya pergi ke Tokyo untuk berlibur. Saya melakukan surfing web sebelum melakukan perjalanan ke sana. Saya memilih untuk menggunakan transportasi umum Tokyo karena banyak orang mengatakan bahwa Tokyo memiliki transportasi terbaik yang disebut MRT (Mass Rapid Transport).

MRT dikenal sebagai komuter bawah tanah, begitu banyak orang yang dipindahkan untuk pergi ke tempat kerja mereka setiap hari. Kami naik MRT di stasiun Chiba. Ketika kereta bergerak, kami merasa seperti berada di dalam jet. Itu sangat cepat karena rute itu di bawah tanah. Kami tidak bisa melihat apa pun kecuali kegelapan

kedua sisi

Di dalam komuter, orang tidak diperbolehkan membawa makanan dan minuman apa pun. Peraturan itu bertujuan menjaga kebersihan area komuter. Kami kagum dengan cara orang mematuhi aturan yang diberikan, meskipun tidak ada penalti yang disebutkan dalam peraturan tersebut

Setibanya di stasiun Ariake, kami berjalan di sekitar stasiun. Perjalanan kami benar-benar nyaman dan menyenangkan. Saya bermimpi tinggal di sini bersama keluarga saya. Itu adalah perjalanan yang benar-benar hebat dan luar biasa.
