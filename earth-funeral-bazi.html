17. Mendeskripsikan "Earth" Tulislah 10 kalimat yang mendeskripsikan planet "Earth". ​


Earth is the third planet from the Sun which is the densest planet and the fifth largest of the eight planets in the Solar System. Earth is also the largest of the four terrestrial planets of the Solar System. Earth is sometimes referred to as the world or the Blue Planet. [23]

Earth formed about 4.54 billion years ago, and life appeared on its surface at least 3.5 billion years ago. [24] The Earth's biosphere then slowly changes the atmosphere and other basic physical conditions, allowing for the proliferation of organisms and the formation of the ozone layer, which together with Earth's magnetic field blocks harmful solar radiation and allows microscopic living things to reproduce safely on land. [25] Physical properties, geological history, and Earth's orbit allowed life to survive.

The Earth's lithosphere is divided into several rigid segments, or tectonic plates, that experience movement across the Earth's surface for millions of years. More than 70% of the Earth's surface is covered by water, [26] and the remainder consists of continents and islands that have many lakes and other water sources that contribute to the formation of the hydrosphere. Earth's poles are mostly covered in ice; solid ice in Antarctica and sea ice in polar ice packs. The Earth's interior remains active, with an inner core consisting of solid iron, while the outer core is a fluid that creates a magnetic field, and a thick, relatively dense layer in the mantle.

Earth interacts with gravity gravity with other objects in space, especially the Sun and Moon. When circling the Sun in one orbit, the Earth rotates on its axis 366.26 times, which creates 365.26 solar days or a sidereal year. [Note 7] Earth's rotation on its axis is tilted 23.4 ° from the plane of the orbit, which causes the difference seasons on the Earth's surface with a period of one tropical year (365.24 solar days). [27] The Moon is Earth's only natural satellite, which began orbiting Earth about 4.53 billion years ago. The gravitational interaction between the Moon and Earth stimulates the formation of sea tides, stabilizes the tilt of the axis, and gradually slows down Earth's rotation.

The earth is home to millions of living things, including humans. [28] The Earth's mineral resources and other biosphere products contribute to providing resources to support the global human population. [29] The areas of the Earth inhabited by humans are grouped into 200 sovereign states, which interact with each other through diplomacy, travel, trade and military action.
