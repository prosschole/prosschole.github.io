19. kak tolong buatkan dialog tentang asking for attention, checking understanding, appreciation and compliment, asking for and giving opinion, tapi bertema lingkungan​


PEMBAHASAN:

Sebelum membuat dialog mengenai asking for attention, checking understanding, appreciation and compliment, and asking for and giving opinion, kita sebaikya mengetahui kalimat seperti apa yang dapat digunakan untuk mengekspresikannya.

1. Expression of asking for attention

- Attention, please!

- Stop talking please!

- Everybody may i have your attention, please?

- Please, look at me

2. Expression of checking someone's understanding

- Do you get it?

- Do you understand what i'm saying?

- Are you with me?

- Do you get what i mean?

3. Expression of giving compliment/ appreciation

- What a ...

- How ...

- That's a ....

- You're so ...

4. Expression of Asking and giving opinion

- What you do think about ... ?

- What do you think of ... ?

- Do you think ... ?

- I think ...

- I feel ...

- In my opinion, ...

- For me, ...

Berikut contoh dialognya

Student A: Attention, please! (asking for attention)

Student B: What's up, A?

Student A: I believe everyone has heard about the earthquake which happened in Lombok, right?

Other students: Yes.

Student A: So, i have an idea. What if our class collects money, groceries, or used clothes to be donated to them. What do you think about it, guys? (Asking opinion)

Student C: Wow, What a great idea! (giving apprecation/ compliment)

Student D: Yeah, I agree with you. I think we should help our friends who are in Lombok. (giving opinion)

Student E: But how do we send our donation?

Student A: My cousin will be a volunteer in Lombok, He will go to Lombok On Sunday. I can ask him to bring our donation.

Student E: Great. We have to collect our donation as soon as possible.

Student A: Yes, You're right. The due date is on Saturday. Do you get it, guys? (checking understanding)

Other students: Yes!

Mudah-mudahan jawaban diatas dapat membantu menjawab pertanyaanmu.

26. buatlah dialog asking and giving attention,checking for understanding,showing appreciation,asking and giving opinion-response(dengan 5 orang)​


Sarah: Everyone, attention, please!

Luna: What is it?

Sarah: Listen! The school makes a traditional dress contest for Kartini Day.

Nuna: Tell us more about it!

Sarah: So, for those that willing to join the contest, they should dress themselves with traditional dress and take a walk like a supermodel in front of others. Some teachers will give score for it. Do you get the point?

Lani: Sure, we do. What about the prize?

Sarah: The winner will be the cover model of our school magazine and get some money. What do you think about that?

Bela: Wow! I think it’s an interesting contest. We can introduce our traditional dress and have some fun at the same time.

Nuna: That’s right! I agree with you.

Lani: So, Nuna…do you want to join it?

Bela: Sure you do. You won’t miss this opportunity, will you?

Nuna: I’d love to. But I don’t have any dress.

Lani: My mother has some. You may choose it freely.

Bela: Also, my mother has a beauty salon. I’ll give you the help that you need.

Sarah: And I have an extra loud voice to support you and give you the big applause.

Nuna: I don’t think I can repay you, guys. Thank you. I will be forever grateful.

 

Pembahasan

 

1. Asking for attention (meminta perhatian): ekspresi yang menunjukkan meminta perhatian kepada lawan bicara.

2. Giving attention (memberikan perhatian): ekspresi yang menunjukkan bahwa perhatian sudah diberikan kepada lawan bicara.

3. Checking understanding (mengecek pemahaman): ekspresi yang digunakan untuk mengetahui apakah lawan bicara mengerti atau paham dengan yang dibicarakan.

4. Showing understanding (menunjukkan pemahaman): ekspresi yang menunjukkan bahwa telah paham atas apa yang dibicarakan.

5. Asking opinion (meminta pendapat): ekspresi yang digunakan untuk meminta pendapat atau opini akan sesuatu hal kepada lawan bicara.

6. Giving opinion (memberikan pendapat): kalimat yang menunjukkan pendapat atau opini atas sesuatu hal.

7. Responding to opinion (jawaban dari pendapat): ekspresi yang disampaikan setelah opini seseorang akan sesuatu hal disampaikan.

8. Showing appreciation (menunjukkan rasa terima kasih): ekspresi yang ditunjukkan sebagai wujud terima kasih.

30. Deskripsi ttg asking for attention and checking for understanding menurut pendapat kalian​


Jawaban:

Asking for attention : it will be used when we need attention from people, when we want to convey something. There are times when we are in conditions that are not conducive, but want to say something, which we want to be heard.

Example :

May I have your attention, please? ( Bolehkah saya meminta perhatianmu?)Attention please! ( Tolong perhatiannya!)

Checking understanding : Checking for understanding is an expression used to check the understanding of someone or the other person we are talking to about what we convey to them.

Example :

Do you understand? (Apakah anda mengerti?)Do you know what I mean? (Apakah anda mengerti yang saya maksud?)
