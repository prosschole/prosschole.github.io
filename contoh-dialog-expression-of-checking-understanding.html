7. contoh dialog checking understanding


Berikut ini adalah contoh dialog tentang checking understanding

Xavier: Abdul, I wonder if you could help me out.

Abdul: Sure, mate, what's up?

Xavier: It's about the Cambridge English test. I don't really get it.

Abdul: Oh, ok, that happens to be my expertise. What test will you take?

Xavier: FCE (First Certificate of English)

Abdul: OK, FCE is one of the mid high-level of English exams, precisely just two more levels to the top level, CPE (Cambridge Proficiency English). You know, unlike TOEFL or IELTS, in Cambridge system, there are levels in its English exams, like five levels for adult general English. FCE is on level 3. The order is KET, PET, FCE, CAE and CPE. Each is more challenging than the previous one.

Xavier: I see, hmm, so is it like TOEFL then? I mean the format.

Abdul: The format is not exactly the same, but the skill to be tested is similar, you know, speaking, listening, writing, reading and grammar. Do you get it?

Xavier: I get it now.

Abdul:Oh, perhaps one major difference than the exams is the speaking.

Xavier: What about it?

Abdul: you have to do it in pairs, directly in front of two native speakers.

Xavier: Oh really? that's quite daunting then.

Abdul: No, it's fine. Oh, the listening is also in British English, sometimes with kinda strong accent, you know. So, do you understand it now?

Xavier: Yeah, man, thanks.

Abdul: Just log on to its website for more info.

Xavier: Sure, I will.

Abdul: Good luck for the test then.

Xavier: Thanks, mate.

8. Contoh dialog expression attention dan checking understanding sama teman sebangku yg simple besok di kumpulin


Pembahasan:


Sebelum membuat dialog yang menggunakan expression of asking for attention dan expression of checking someone's understanding, kita sebaiknya mengetahui bagaimana kalimat yang digunakan untuk mengekspresikannya. Berikut contohnya.


Expression of asking for attention

- Attention, please!

- Stop talking please!

- Everybody may i have your attention, please?

- Please, look at me

- Hey!

- Kamu juga dapat meminta perhatian orang lain dengan menyebut namanya


Expression of giving attention

- Really?

- Then?

- What's next?

- Ok

- Is that all?

- I see


Expression of checking someone's understanding

- Do you get it?

- Do you understand what i'm saying?

- Are you with me?

- Do you get what i mean?

- With me, so far?


Berikut dialognya.

A: Hey, B!

B: Hey. What's up?

A: I want to ask how to make this instant cup noodle.

B: Oh, it's easy. first, open the cup noodle package then take out the seasoning and the plastic fork, after that pour hot water into the cup noodle until this mark. next, close it and wait for about five minutes. Finally, mix it with the seasoning.

A: Wait, i don't get it. can you explain it slowly?

B: Alright. First, open the package and take out the seasoning and the plastic fork. Do you get it?

A: Yes.

B: Then, pour hot water into the cup noodle until this mark. Can you see the mark?

A: Yes, and then?

B: next, close it and wait for about five minutes. With me, so far?

A: Yes, i'm following

B: After that, Mix the noodle and the seasoning, then you can enjoy it.

A: Oh, i see. thank you B

B: You're welcome.

11. contoh dialog tentang checking understanding


Checking for understanding (menanyakan kepahaman)(semua pernyataan berikut berarti “apakah Anda mengerti”)
(Do you) know what I mean?
Do you know what I’m saying?
Do you understand?
Are you following me?
Are you with me (so far)?
Have you got it?
Any questions?
Got it?
Showing Understanding (menyatakan pemahaman)(semua pernyataan berikut berarti “saya mengerti”)
I see.
I understand.
I get it./I got it.
Gotcha. (Informal)
Expressing Lack of Understanding (menyatakan kurang paham)(semua pernyataan berikut berarti “saya tidak/kurang mengerti”)
I don’t get it.
(I’m sorry.) I don’t understand.
What do you mean?
I’m not following you.
I don’t quite follow you.
I’m not sure I get what you mean.
What was that again?

(Hai, Tom!)

(Hai, Li. Apa yang terjadi? Kamu terlihat sangat kebingungan.)

Li: I don’t understand what the teacher said in Biology class.

(Saya tidak mengerti apa yang diucapkan oleh guru di kelas Biologi.)

(Biarkan saya membantu kamu. Yang mana kamu tidak mengerti?)

Li: This one. It is about ecosystem.

(Yang ini. Ini tentang ekosistem.)

Tom: Oh, in an ecosystem there is a living organisms such as humans, animals, plants and also non living components such as mineral, air, and water. Have you got it?

(Oh, di sebuah ekosistem ada makhluk hidup seperti manusia, hewan, tumbuhan dan juga komponen yang tidak hidup seperti mineral, udara, dan air. Apa kamu mengerti?)

Li: Yeah, but I’m still not sure about it. Can you teach it to me?

(Ya, tetapi saya masih belum yakin dengan itu. Bisakah kamu mengajari saya?)

Tom: Yeah sure. Should we study about it in our school library?

(Ya tentu. Haruskah kita belajar tentang itu di perpustakaan sekolah?)

Li: Yeah sure, let’s go now.

(Ya tentu, ayo pergi sekarang.)
