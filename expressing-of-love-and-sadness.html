19. Experience of sadness , love and annoyance


Expression of sadness
Express sadness: if you have a big problem and you don’t know what to do
1. I’m Oh no/ oh dear!
2. Poor …!·
3. I’m really sad to know it.
4. What a pity!
5. I’m not in a good mood.
6. Please, don’t disturb me,
7. I have a trouble.
8. I am very sad about…
EX : I’ve lost my mobile phone and now I'm not in a good mood.

Expressing of Love
Expressing Love: expressing great liking of someone or something
-use to express love, happy, if you like something or someone, you express love
1. I think I love you.
2. I do love you.
3. Let me love you.
4. I fall in love with you.
EX: You are the only person I love. I fall in love with you. Do you return my love?

Expressing of Annoyance
Expressing Annoyance: expressing the feeling of being annoyed. 
The following are some expressions of expressing Annoyance:
1. ..really makes me mad
2. I’m annoyed
3. I had enough with it
4. I can’t bear it any longer
5. Stop doing that! It’s really annoying.
EX: well, you know. I am very annoyed. Why did you come late? You should have been here earlier.
