Telling Times (Menyatakan Waktu)

Versi British (Menit + Past/To + Jam)  

07:40 ➔ It's twenty to eight 11:25 ➔ It's twenty-five past eleven 04:53 ➔ It's seven to five 08:28 ➔ It's twenty-eight past eight 05:15 ➔ It's (a) quarter past five 09:45 ➔ It's (a) quarter to ten 10:30 ➔ It's half past ten

Penting!

Menit 1-29 gunakan (menit + past + jam berada) Menit 31-59 gunakan (sisa menit + to + jam menuju) Menit ke 15 gunakan (a quarter past + jam berada) Menit ke 45 gunakan (a quarter to + jam menuju) Menit ke 30 gunakan (half past + jam berada)
