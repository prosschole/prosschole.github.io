4. buatlah dialog (10 baris) berisi 2 proverbs:"dont judge the book by its cover " dan "actions speak louder than words" jadi 2 kalimat itu dijadiin 1 dialog yaahelp gez​


Jawaban:

Conversations:

David: Hi Mark, have you met the new kid in class?

Mark: Yeah, I saw him. He looks kind of weird.

David: Well, you know what they say, "don't judge a book by its cover."

Mark: What do you mean?

David: I mean that we shouldn't judge someone based on their appearance alone. Maybe he's really nice and we just haven't got to know him yet.

Mark: That's true. But what if he's not nice?

David: Then we'll find out soon enough. As another proverb goes, "actions speak louder than words." We'll see what kind of person he is by the way he behaves, not just by how he looks.

Mark: Okay, I think I should meet and start a conversation with him.

David: That's a good idea.

Mark: Thanks David.

David: You are welcome.

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran Conversations (percakapan) yang berisi tentang Proverbs (pepatah) dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Percakapan:

David: Hai Mark, apakah kamu pernah bertemu dengan anak baru di kelas?

Mark: Ya, aku melihatnya. Dia terlihat agak aneh.

David: Nah, kamu tahu apa yang mereka katakan, "jangan menilai buku dari sampulnya."

Mark: Apa maksudmu?

David: Maksudku, kita tidak boleh menilai seseorang hanya dari penampilannya saja. Mungkin dia sangat baik dan kita belum mengenalnya.

Mark: Itu benar. Tapi bagaimana jika dia tidak baik?

David: Kalau begitu kita akan segera tahu. Seperti kata pepatah lain, "tindakan berbicara lebih keras daripada kata-kata." Kita akan melihat orang seperti apa dia dari caranya berperilaku, bukan hanya dari penampilannya.

Mark: Oke, kupikir aku harus bertemu dan memulai percakapan dengannya.

David: Itu ide yang bagus.

Mark: Terima kasih David.

David: Sama-sama.

Semoga membantu ya.

26. Meaning(Task 1)what does it mean?Find the meaning of the following proverb!1.Better late than never.2.Don't go too far in small3.By learning to obey we know to command.4.A friend in need is a friend indeed.5. Actions speak louder than words.​


Jawaban:

1.Better late than never.

This means that it is better to do something late than to not/never do it at all.

2.Don't go too far in small.

This means not to overreact to small things/problems.

3.By learning to obey we know to command.

This means that we must learn to obey orders before we are qualified to give them. 

4.A friend in need is a friend indeed.

This means that a friend who helps you when you really need help is a true friend.

5. Actions speak louder than words.

This means that what you do is more important and shows your intentions and feelings more clearly than what you say.
