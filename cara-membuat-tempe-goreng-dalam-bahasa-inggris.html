9. cara membuat mie goreng dalam bahasa inggris?


ingredients

Noodles
Onions
Garlic
Olive oil
Tomatoes
Lemoncarrots

Steps

1. Prepare all the necessary ingredients on the table or another flat surface. Make sure you have everything you need. Chop the onions, garlic, carrots, and tomatoes on the cutting board. Cut the ingredients into very small cube-like pieces so the noodles will get the taste of every ingredient.


2. Heat your frying pan and add some olive oil in it while it's heating up.


3. Open the noodles from the package and crumble them into average pieces (not too small though). Smaller pieces have a higher chance of cooking all the way through.


4. Your crumbled noodles should look a bit small like you see in the picture.


5. After your frying pan is hot enough with the oil and such, just put all of the chopped ingredients into the frying pan.Leave it there for a while and stir.


6. If the chopped ingredients are ready after some time, add the crumbled noodles there. Stir it for a while.


7. If the noodles smell great with the others, add the seasoning that came with the packet. If you didn't get one, then just add a pinch a salt or more according to your taste. Stir it up again. Now add some water in it. The noodle shouldn't sink in the water. Only 75 % of it should be.


8. Keep out creation in the frying pan until all the water soaks up in the noodles.Now, squeeze about half a lime or according to your taste. There you have it! You're now ready to serve the president some nice fried noodles.

How to Fry Sausage (Cara membuat sosis goreng)

1. Heat a pan on the stove (panaskan penggorengan di atas kompor
2. Add a little oil (tambahkan sedikit minyak)
3. Lay the sausages on the hot surface (taruh sosis diatas permukaan teflon)
4. Cook until brown on both sides (masak sampai bewarna kecoklatan di kedua sisi)
5. Put the sausages on the plate (taruh sosis di atas piring)
6. Fried sausages is ready to serve (sosis goreng siap disajikan)How to make/cook fried sausage

1. Put oil/butter (to be natural) in the heated frying pan
2. Wait for the oil to heat up
3. After it's been heated up, put those sausages on that frying pan
4. Roll it, squeeze it (with a spatula) till the sausage is fried (has brown color)
5. Put some tissue on the plate where you want your sausage to be served
6. Wait for the oil to be soaked  with the tissue then you enjoy your MASTERPIECE

Terjemahan:
1. Taruhlah minyak / mentega di panci yang dipanaskan diatas kompor
2. Tunggulah untuk minyaknya untuk panas 
3. Sesudah minyaknya panas, taruhlah sosismu diatas panci itu
4. Gulung, Timpa, Pencet (dengan spatula) sampai sosismu tergoreng (memiliki warna cokelat)
5. Taruh tissue diatas piring dimana kau mau sosismu dihidangkan
6. Tunggu sampai minyaknya diserap tissue, lalu nikmatilah maha karyamu

19. Cara membuat pisang goreng dalam bahasa inggris


Jawaban:

How to Make Fried Bananas

Ingredients:

- 4 ripe bananas

- 1/2 cup all-purpose flour

- 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

- 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg

- 1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom

- 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger

- 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves

- 1/2 teaspoon salt

- 1/2 cup white sugar

- 1/2 cup vegetable oil

Instructions:

1. Peel the bananas and cut them into 1/2-inch thick slices.

2. In a shallow bowl, mix together the flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, cardamom, ginger, cloves, and salt.

3. In a separate shallow bowl, mix together the sugar and oil.

4. Dip the banana slices into the flour mixture, then into the sugar and oil mixture.

5. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the banana slices and fry until golden brown, about 2 minutes per side.

6. Remove the fried bananas from the skillet and place on a plate lined with paper towels to absorb any excess oil.

7. Serve warm and enjoy!
