8. buatlah pengalaman yang berkaitan dengan simple past tense dan narative


Title: Borobudur temple

Orientation

Last week I was visiting Borobudur temple. It was a great temple and one of the biggest Buddhist temples in the world.

I had been there for several times and I still love to go there again and again. I know a little bit about the history of the restoration of the Borobudur Temple.

Event

Borobudur temple was built in 9th-century in (now) Magelang city, Central Java, Indonesia.

That temple was built during the reign of Sailendra Dynasty as the symbolic monument retelling the life of Buddha in which people can learn about Buddhist religion.

The temple was design in Javanese Buddhist architecture as the acculturation of Indian and Javanese culture.

Some evidences and theories suggest that Borobudur temple was disappeared during the reign of Hindu kingdoms and Islam in java in 14th-century and the years after it.

It was disappeared because of the eruption of Merapi Mountain that buried most of the surrounding area including Borobudur.

But because of that eruption then the temple was survived and untouchable until in 1814 during the British colonialization, Sir Thomas Stamford Raffles (the British ruler of Java at that time) got information that there was a hill with carved texture.

After that Thomas Stamford Raffles made a project to dig and clear that area and after 2 mounts working the Borobudur temple was appeared.

But of course at that time the face of Borobudur was extremely different from it appearance today.

Since it was found the British government restored that temple and continued by the Dutch government. The largest restoration was undertaken by Indonesian government and UNESCO between 1975 until 1982.

Roerientation

Since that now we can enjoy and learn about the beauty of the Borobudur Temple.

12. Ceritakan pengalaman menggunakan simple past tense


Sebelum masuk ke penggunaan Simple Past Tense, saya akan menjelaskan sedikit hal mengenai Simple Past Tense.

Simple Past adalah kata kerja yang digunakan untuk menceritakan atau menjelaskan sesuatu yang telah terjadi. Simple Past Tense menunjukkan bahwa anda sedang berbicara mengenai sesuatu yang telah terjadi. Tidak seperti Past Continous Tense yang digunakan untuk kejadian yang telah terjadi pada suatu periode, Simple Past Tense menekankan pada kejadian yang telah selesai. 
Anda juga dapat menggunakan Simple Past Tense untuk berbicara mengenai kondisi sesuatu atau seseorang, seperti perasaan seseorang terhadap sesuatu.
Untuk regular verbs, kita hanya perlu menambahkan -ed pada akhir kata kerja atau hanya -d jika kata kerja tersebut berakhir dengan huruf E. Contoh play - played, type - typed, listen - listened.Untuk irregular verbs, penambahan akan semakin rumit. Beberapa kata kerja mempunyai model Simple Past Tense yang sama dengan kata kerja dasarnya. Contoh put - put, cut - cut, set - set, hit - hit.Untuk irregular verbs yang lainnya, termasuk penambahan kata kerja to be, bentuknya menjadi semakin rumit lagi. Contoh see - saw, build - built, go - went, do - did, am/is/are - was/were.
Contoh penggunaan Simple Past Tense dalam sebuah kalimat adalah sebagai berikut
I joined the English debate last week. It was an interesting activity for me. I learnt a lot about grammars especially the pronouncation. My parents supported me throughout the process. They came to see me. Although I studied a lot, I didn't win the debate.However, my parents were still proud of me.

15. tulis kegiatan atau pengalaman keluarga Anda dengan menggunakan present simple, present continuous, present perfect , past simple dan future simple tens


Jawaban & Penjelasannya

Present Simple: My family and I often visit my grandparents on weekends.

Penjelasan: Present Simple digunakan untuk menggambarkan suatu kebiasaan atau aktivitas rutin yang terjadi di masa sekarang.

Present Continuous: We are currently planning a holiday to Bali.

Penjelasan: Present Continuous digunakan untuk menggambarkan aktivitas yang sedang terjadi saat ini.

Present Perfect: We have already bought tickets for the trip.

Penjelasan: Present Perfect digunakan untuk menggambarkan aktivitas atau kejadian yang terjadi di masa lalu dan masih berlaku di masa sekarang.

Past Simple: Last summer, we went to the beach for a picnic.

Penjelasan: Past Simple digunakan untuk menggambarkan aktivitas atau kejadian yang terjadi di masa lalu dan sudah selesai.

Future Simple: Next year, we will go camping in the mountains.

Penjelasan: Future Simple digunakan untuk menggambarkan aktivitas atau kejadian yang akan terjadi di masa depan.
