Ingredients
• 150gram of butter.
• 150gram of dark chocolate.
• 300 gram of flour
• 4 eggs.
• 600 gram of regular sugar
• 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps
1. Melt the chocolate with the butter.
2. Stir the eggs with the sugar and the vanilla extract.
3. Preheat the oven at low temperature
4. Combine the mixes you made.
5. Add the flour and stir with a wooden spoon.
6. Grease a brownie tin.
7. Add a little flour to cover the tin.
8. Add the brownie dough.
9. Bake for 10-30 minutes.
10. To check the brownies, put a knife in the mix. The knife must be moist!
11. Eat them 15 minutes after you took them out of the oven.

7. bagaimana cara membuat kue dalam bahasa inggris yang dengan 10 steps


Ingredients

1. Wheat flour

2. Baking powder

3. Sugar

4. Eggs

5. Chocolate powder

6. Cooked chocolate. Heat chocolate until it melts before chocolate is used.

7. Milk

8. Salt

9. Margarine or butter

10. Chocochips. Can be replaced with other ingredients according to your taste.

11. Muffin cake mold.

Steps and how to make muffins

1. Mix multipurpose flour, baking powder and chocolate powder into the mixer. Stir well until all ingredients are well blended.

2. Then add milk, salt and sugar. Stir until all ingredients are well blended, then add eggs. Stir again until all ingredients are well blended.

3. Take the flour mixture in the first step and pour it little by little into the milk and egg mixture above while stirring to mix well. Repeat until the flour mixture is used up and all ingredients are evenly mixed.

4. Pour the butter or margarine and cook the dark chocolate that has been melted beforehand into the mixture little by little while stirring.

5. Add half the choco chips, cashews or other additives to the mixture above. Stir again so that all ingredients are evenly mixed.

6. Take the muffin cake mold that has been prepared above. Pour 2/3 part chocolate muffin mixture.

7. Sprinkle the remaining chocolate chips onto muffin paper.

8. Put the muffins in the oven. Arrange with a temperature of around 180 degrees Celsius and bake until cooked or approximately bake for 18 to 20 minutes.

9. Remove the baked chocolate muffin from the oven after cooking.

10. Muffins are ready to be served

13. Contoh teks prosedur membuat kue dengan bahasa inggris


How to Make Bolu Cake.


Ingredients:

12 eggs

1/4 kg of butter

3/8 kg of sugar

2 sachet of vanilla

1/2 tbs of Ovalet

3 1/2 ons of wheat flour

5 tbs of milk powder

1 package of chocolate powder.


Steps:

1. Remove the egg yolk and albumen and place them in different bowl.

2. Mix the butter, sugar, vanilla, and ovalet for 10 minutes.

3. Put the yolk into the dough and blend again at least 10 minutes.

4. Pour the wheat flour little by little into the dough and stir well.

5. Pour the milk powder little by little into the dough and stir well.

6. Pour the chocolate powder little by little into the dough and stir well.

7. Blend the albumen around 5 minutes.

8. Pour the albumen into the dough. Blend them around 10 minutes.

9. Prepare a baking pan lubricated with butter. Pour the dough. Put into the oven and bake around 40 minutes in 25 degrees celcius.

10. Lift the cake and put on the plate. Spread some sugar on it. The bolu cake is ready to serve.

23. contoh teks prosedur bahasa inggris membuat kue


How to Make Cake


Ingredients:
12 eggs
1/4 kg of butter
3/8 kg of sugar
2 sachet of vanilla
1/2 tbs of Ovalet
3 1/2 ons of wheat flour
5 tbs of milk powder
1 package of chocolate powder.
Steps:
Remove the egg yolk and albumen and place them in different bowl.
Mix the butter, sugar, vanilla, and ovalet for 10 minutes.
Put the yolk into the dough and blend again at least 10 minutes.
Pour the wheat flour little by little into the dough and stir well.
Pour the milk powder little by little into the dough and stir well.
Pour the chocolate powder little by little into the dough and stir well.
Blend the albumen around 5 minutes.
Pour the albumen into the dough. Blend them around 10 minutes.
Prepare a baking pan lubricated with butter. Pour the dough. Put into the oven and bake around 40 minutes in 25 degrees celcius.
Lift the cake and put on the plate. Spread some sugar on it. The bolu cake is ready to serve.

How to Make Chocolate Sponge Cake

Materials:

8 tablespoon flour

2 tablespoon cornstarch

2 tablespoon cocoa powder

6 tablespoons of chocolate

7 tablespoon cooking oil

2 eggs

5 tablespoon sugar

1/2 tablespoon vanilla

1/2 tablespoon SP

1/2 tablespoon baking powder

How to make:

The first step : Mix eggs, sugar and SP

Second step : Mix at high speed until fluffy.

Third step : Then add flour, cocoa powder, cornstarch and baking powder. Then mix at medium speed until well blended.

Last step : add SKM chocolate, vanilla and cooking oil, stir until smooth. Pour into a tin that has been greased with margarine and bake until cooked.
