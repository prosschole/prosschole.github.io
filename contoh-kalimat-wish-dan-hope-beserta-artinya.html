8. pengertian hope and wish beserta contoh dan penggunaannya


artinya sama2 harapan/berharap, namun dalam penggunaannya wish biasa digunakan dalam percakapan sehari-hari yg biasa saja misal; wish u all the best. kalo hope itu benar2 mengharap/ harapannya lebih dalam misal; i hope i can finished this work 
hope dan wish berarti semoga,berharap

contoh :
1. Present Wishes 
S+Wish+S2+V2

Example :
I Wish,I know what I to do

2. Future wishes

S+Wish+S2(subject ke-2)+could/would(kata lampau dari can/will)
Example :
They Wish the could visit paris

3. Past Wishes 
S+Wish+S2+Had+V3

Example :
I Wish my teacher had given mean A+

The sentence with hope and wish have some differences in their pattern and meaning, although it may sound quite similar at glance or in Bahasa Indonesia. Here they are

Hope is an expression about one's expectations in the future. The pattern is as follows :

        S + hope + future simple / present simple

In saying wishes, there are three kinds of it explained as followswish for the future with the pattern: S + wish + O + nounwish which is expressed for / due to the present mistakes. The pattern is S + wish + past simplewish for the past mistake with its social function as an expression of  regret. The pattern is S + wish + past perfect
