FISHING EXPERIENCE

On Sunday morning I was at home because it was a holiday. I was at home helping my parents. I helped my parents with enthusiasm. Then after I helped my parents then I watched television. I watch television until noon. Because on that day the cartoon program was gone, I turned off the television.Then my friend came to my house and he invited me to go fishing. Then I begged my parents for permission. Finally I was allowed to go fishing. Then I prepared alar fishing rods and my friend waited outside my house. After I finished preparing then me and my friend headed to the river.

After that we finally arrived at the river. Then I put a bait on my fishing line then I started fishing together with my friends. Me and my friends waited until there was a pull on the line. Finally I got a pull from the fish. Then I rolled the strings and finally I got a fairly large cork fish. Finally all my friends also get fish. Some get tilapia and some get wader fish. Then after we were all satisfied, we returned to our homes. After I got home, I fried the fish and I ate. The fish I fried was very tasty and delicious.

RECOUNT TEXT

 

Jawaban  

 

Pendahuluan  

Recount text adalah teks yang menceritakan kejadian atau pengalaman di masa lampau. Cerita tersebut bisa pengalaman sendiri maupun pengalaman orang lain.

Generic Structure dari Recount text:

Orientation: Memperkenalkan tokoh, setting tempat dan setting waktu.

Events: Menjabarkan rangkaian kejadian yang terjadi saat itu.

Reorientation: kesimpulan dari pengalaman yang dialami. Apakah menyenangkan, menyedihkan atau lain lain.

Language features pada recount text:

Menggunakan simple past, past continuous dan/atau past perfect tense

Pembahasan  

Contoh Recount text tentang liburan Natal:

My Christmas holiday

Orientation: Last Christmas my family and I went to my grandparents house in Semarang. We went there by car. It took about 7 hours traveling from Jakarta to Semarang.

Recount Text

Recount Text adalah teks yang bertujuan untuk menceritakan pengalaman atau kejadian yang dialami di masa lampau.

Generic Structure dari Recount text adalah:

Orientation: Berisi pengenalan tokoh, tempat, waktu dan tujuan cerita.Events: Berisi hal-hal yang terjadi pada waktu itu (lampau).Re-orientation: Berisi kesimpulan cerita atau kesan dari penulis/ tokoh terhadap pengalaman/ ceritanya.

Language features dari Recount Text:

Menggunakan Past tense (karena kejadian banyak yang terjadi di waktu lampau)Menggunakan action verb, seperti: do, go, play, use, dan lain lain.menggunakan thinking verb, seperti: think, feel, consider, dan lain lain

Trip to Jogjakarta

My family and I started to walk around the Prambanan temple. There, my family and I took pictures of ourselves. We also took some pictures with tourists from another country.

The second day we visited Jogjakarta palace. Just like Prambanan temple, that was also the third time I visited Jogjakarta palace. I was not really interest about the palace, because I thought that palace still like that. But, there was something different from the last time I visited it. My parents asked a guide that full of spirit to explain anything about the palace. He explained anything, from the statue, the building and the pictures in that palace.  

In the evening, my family and I went to “Malioboro”. We were so happy at “Malioboro” because there, we bought so many things until our legs got weak.
