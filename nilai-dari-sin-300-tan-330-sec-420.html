10. Tentukan nilai dari SEC 330. TAN 120. SIN 315


Tentukan nilai dari sec 330° . tan 120° . sin 315°

Pembahasan :

Kuadran 1 : nilai trigonometrinya positif semua

Kuadran 2 : hanya nilai sin α dan cosec α yang bernilai positif
=> (180° - α)

Kuadran 3 : hanya nilai tan α dan cot α yang bernilai positif
=> (180° + α)

Kuadran 4 : hanya nilai cos α dan sec α yang bernilai positif
=> (360° - α)

sec 330°
= sec (360° - 30°)
= sec 30°
= 1/(cos 30°)
= 1/(1/2 √3)
= 2/√3
= 2/√3 . √3/√3
= (2/3) √3

tan 120°
= tan (180° - 60°)
= - tan 60°
= - √3

sin 315°
= sin (360° - 45°)
= - sin 45°
= (-1/2) √2


sec 330° . tan 120° . sin 315°
= (2/3) √3 . - √3 . (-1/2) √2
= (2/3) . 3 . (1/2) √2
= 2 . (1/2) √2
= √2

15. Nilai dari sin 300° + tan 120° - sec 330° adalah...​

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

sin 300° + tan 120° - sec 330°

= sin (360° - 30°) + tan (180° - 60°) - sec (360° - 30°)

= - sin 30° - tan 60° - sec 30°

= - 1/2 - √3 - 2/3 √3

= -3/6 - 6/6 √3 - 4/6 √3

= -1/6 . (3 + 6√3 - 4√3)

= -1/6 . (3 + 2√3)

16. Sin 300°.tan 310°.sec 420° / cot 135°.cos 210°.csc 315°


[tex] \frac{ \sin( {300}^{ \circ} ) \tan( {210}^{ \circ} ) \sec( {420}^{ \circ} ) }{ \cot( {135}^{ \circ} ) \cos( {210}^{ \circ} ) \csc( {315}^{ \circ} ) } = \boxed{ \bold{ \frac{1}{3} \sqrt{6} }} \\ [/tex]

Pembahasan

Nilai sin, cos, tan, cot, sec, csc pada sudut - sudut relasi trigonometri :

[tex] \sin(2\pi - x) = - \sin(x) \\ \tan(\pi + x) = \tan(x) \\ \cos(\pi + x) = - \cos(x) \\ \cos(2\pi + x) = \cos(x) \\ \\ \sec(x) = \frac{1}{ \cos(x) } \\ \csc(x) = \frac{1}{ \sin(x) } \\ \\ [/tex]

Maksud soal mungkin seperti ini :

[tex] \: \: \: \: \: \frac{ \sin( {300}^{ \circ} ) \tan( {210}^{ \circ} ) \sec( {420}^{ \circ} ) }{ \cot( {135}^{ \circ} ) \cos( {210}^{ \circ} ) \csc( {315}^{ \circ} ) } \\ \\ = \frac{ \sin{(360 - 60)}^{ \circ} . \tan{(180 + 30)}^{ \circ}. \sec{(360 + 60)}^{ \circ} }{ \cot {(180 - 45)}^{ \circ}. \cos {(180 + 30)}^{ \circ} . \csc {(360 - 45)}^{ \circ} } \\ \\ = \frac{( - \frac{1}{2} \sqrt{3} )( \frac{1}{3} \sqrt{3} )(2) }{( - 1)( - \frac{1}{2} \sqrt{3})( - \sqrt{2} ) } \\ \\ = \frac{1}{ \frac{1}{2} \sqrt{6} } \\ \\ = \frac{2}{ \sqrt{6} } \\ \\ = \frac{1}{3} \sqrt{6} \\ \\ [/tex]

= 1/√3

= ⅓√3
