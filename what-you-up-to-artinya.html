You: Let's watch a movie this weekend.

Your friend 1: That's a good idea. It is a new movie, isn't it?

Your friend 2: Yes. I heard it is very interesting. I will join with you.

You: How about you? Would you join us to watch it?

Your friend 3: I am sorry. I can't. I have appointment with my brother.

You: Can't you change your time? This is our moment to gather at the weekend.

Your friend 3: I am really sorry. This weekend is his birthday. We will go fishing.

Your friend 1: Oh I see. How about you? Can you join with us?

Your friend 4: I am sorry too. I have to go with my mom. We are going to visit my grandparents at the village.

Your friend 2: Oh okay. So, what time will we go?

You: Let's meet at my house before we go at 06.00 pm tomorrow.

Your friend 1: Okay. See you tomorrow.

Your friend 2: See you.
