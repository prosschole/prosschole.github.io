21. cara membuat ikan bakar sambal terasi dalam bahasa inggris how to make nya yaaaa..


How to make Grilled Fish with Shrimp Paste

main ingredients:
1 medium sized snapper fish
2 tablespoons red chili
1 tablespoon shrimp paste
¼ teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons fish sauce
2 teaspoons sugar
½ teaspoon lemon juice
2 lime leaves
4 tablespoons cooking oil

Ingredients for shrimp paste sauce:
3 pieces of red pepper curls
1 teaspoon shrimp paste
3 clove onions
1/8 teaspoon sugar
1/8 teaspoon salt
8 tablespoons water

Steps:
- First, Clean the fish, slice and clean the scales then sprinkle the fish with lemon juice and salt. leave it for 10 minutes
- Second, mix all ingredients. Stir-fry until fragrant. Set aside
- Next, for shrimp paste sauce, blend chili, shrimp paste, onion slices, sugar, and salt. Then, Give some water and Stir well.
- After that, grill the fish and smear with the mixture of materials that have been stir-fry until cooked.
- Finally, the food is ready to be served
