6. buatlah dialog bahasa inggris tentang congratulationtolong besok kumpul​


║EXPRESSION OF CONGRATULATION║

Answer

Dialog of Congratulation

Dew       : I heard news from my friends that you won the English Speaking Contest last week. Is it correct ?

Sara       : Yes, it is. I won the English Speaking Contest last week. I got the first place and Mr. Johnson has offered me the scholarship.

Dew       : Warmest congratulations on your achievement, Sara! I'm very proud of you. You proved yourself. Wow, that's a good news. I'm glad to hear that. You always dream about it. ( They shake their hands and hug each other )

Sara      : Thank you for saying so, Dew ! I can't believe it. I have tried it so hard.

Dew      : You know, the hard work never denies the result. Keep it up, Sara! If you dream something, you must catch it and defeat your laziness!

Sara      : Thank you very much, Dew! Certainly, I do.

Explanation

CONGRATULATION  In daily, it’s familiar to congratulate someone’s success and the particular events.  Congratulation is an utterance used to express the appreciation toward someone’s achievement or praise someone’s good fortune and also used to congratulate on the other specific moments for good wishes/respecting each other.

There are variety of ways in expressing congratulation , such as :

Please , accept my warmest congratulationsI’d like to congratulate you on your successCongratulations, you deserve itI’m very proud of your success. CongratsLet me congratulate on your successI must congratulate you for this achievementWarmest congratulations on the birth of your sweet baby...I’d be the first to congratulate you on your successCongratulations. You made it.....Congratulations for being a father.....Happy anniversaryHappy Birthday

Here are the responses of congratulation :

Thank you very muchThank you  so much. It’s very kind of you to  say soThank you for saying so.Thanks, I really needed itDo you really think so!How nice of you to say soNot really, thanks!Thanks.  I am glad you think so

28. bahasa Inggris buat 20 kalimat dialog congratulation ​


Jawaban:

Kamu bisa memberi selamat dengan berbagai cara seperti:

Congratulations! (Selamat!)

Congrats apabila kamu ingin mengucapkan kepada teman sebaya. Ini merupakan ucapan selamat dalam bentuk informal.

congratulations for your graduation (selamat atas kelulusanmu)

Happy new year (Selamat tahun baru)

Happy birthday (Selamat ulang tahun)

Congratulations on your wedding (selamat atas pernikahannya)

Tidak hanya mengetahui ucapan selamat dalam Bahasa Inggris, kamu juga harus tahu bagaimana meresponnya.

Thank you so much (Terima kasih banyak)

Thanks for saying (Terima kasih atas ucapannya)

Yeah, I’m glad you think so (saya sangat senang kamu juga berpikir demikian)

I still must learn more (saya harus belajar banyak lagi)

Oh, it was really nothing (Oh, itu bukan apa-apa kok)
