9. What is the difference of expressing plan and intention?


Expression plan: bisa dilihat dari artinya, yaitu penggambaran rencana, artinya seseorang yang ingin melakukan sesuatu yang akan terjadi dan kemungkinan besar bisa terjadi.

Contoh: I am going to go to Japan Next Year (Aku akan pergi ke Jepang tahun depan)
Maksudnya, dia akan pergi ke Jepang tahun depan, artinya sudah terencana akan pergi ke Jepang tahun depan.

Intention: bisa dilihat lagi dari artinya, yaitu niat, artinya seseorang yang ingin melakukan sesuatu, tetapi hanya niat, yang belum terencana sama sekali.

Contoh: I will be a doctor
(Aku akan menjadi seorang dokter)
Maksudnya dia akan menjadi dokter, tetapi belum tentu akan terjadi

14. kata bantu dan keterangan waktu pada Expressing plan / intentions


Pembahasan

Kata bantu dan keterangan waktu pada Expressing plan/intentions adalah will, is/am/are going to, would like, dan would rather.

Expressing plan/intentions is to state plans or something intended to do in the future. Expressing plan atau Intention adalah sebuah ungkapan berisi harapan atau rencana seseorang berkaitan dengan hal yang akan dilakukan di masa depan/masa yang akan datang. Hal ini berarti ungkapan ini secara tidak langsung menyatakan bahwa kejadian tersebut merupakan sebuah kejadian yang belum terjadi karena masih berupa harapan atau rencana. Tenses yang digunakan adalah Future Tense.

Contoh Expressing plan/intentions:

1.  I am going to go to Bali next month.

2. I would rather redo the whole task than get a bad mark.

3. I will make an effort to come to your graduation party next Saturday.

4. He is thinking of moving to Singapore next semester.

5. I reckon I will visit my grandmother next Sunday.

6. I would like to buy all the camp equipment tomorrow.

7. It is my intention to prepare all the camp equipment tomorrow.

8. Would you like to accompany me to Adam's birthday party next Friday?

9. I would rather stay at home than go fishing.

10. They are going to celebrate their winning in the school yard tonight.

Pola Kalimat yang sering digunakan:

Subject + Would Like to + VI + …..

Subject +  will + VI + …..

Subject + is/am/are going to  + VI + …..

Subject + Would Rather + VI + than …..

20. Berikan 2 contoh soal beserta jawabannya tentang expressing plan/intention/purpose?


Explanation:

We can use simple future tense to express a plan or intention or purpose.
The structure: S + will + V1 + O
For example:
- I will go to the library tomorrow.
- She will visit the art museum next week.

Beside using simple future tense, we also can use the following expressions to express a plan or intention or purpose.
- I am going to ...
- I'm planning to ...
- I reckon I'll ...
- I'm thinking of ...
- I intend to ...

Answer:
Here are 2 example questions of expressing plan or intention or purpose.

1. Tomorrow is mother's day. I ________ a bouquet of roses for my mom.
a. am giving
b. have given
c. are going to give
d. will give
The correct answer is d

2. A: What are you planning to do on weekend?
B: I _______ my grandmother's house on Saturday.
a. am planning to visit
b. have visited
c. is going to visit
d. will be visited
The correct answer is a

27. Rumus untuk menulis expressing plan


Expressing Intention

Social function:To state plans or something intended to do in the future.v  
I would like to ......v   I will ......v   I want to ......v   I am going to ......v   I would rather ......
Examples of Expressing Intention:v  
I would like to tell about my familyv  
 I will visit museum todayv  
 I want to make a pancakev   
I am going to introduce my friendv   
I would rather stay at home than go fishing
To express willingness: use will(+) Subject + will + V1 + Object/Complement(-)  Subject + will not + V1 + Object/Complement(?) Will + subject + V1 + Object/complement
(+) She will visit her cousins on the next holiday.(-) She will not visit her cousins on the next holiday.(?) Will she visit her cousins on the next holiday?
To express a prior plan: Use only be going to
(+) Subject + Be going to + V1 + O/C(-) Subject + Be not going to + V1 + O/C(?) Be + Subject + going to + V1 + O/C
(+) She is going to visit her cousins on the next holiday.(-) She is not going to visit her cousins on the next holiday?(?) Is she going to visit her cousins on the next holiday?
