1. Apa perbedaan penggunaan am ,is,are dengan do,does dan was were


am is are adalah to be yang diletakkan setelah subjek. contoh I (am) a student
kalo do itu kata kerja yang subjek nya yaitu I, You,We, They
kalo does itu kata kerja yang subjeknya She, He , It
Was dan Were digunakan untuk kalimat yang kejadian nya sudah berlalu
contoh : I was there when you come to my home
contoh : We were arrived at 2 pm

3. Kapan kita menggunakan Was atau were sebagai be di Passive Report Structure. apa bedanya dengan menggunakan is atau are


was were itu masa lampau = dilihat dari objeknya kalau was itu he/she/it/nama orang kalau were itu untuk they/we/i/you

is dan are itu saat ini benda masih ada = dilihat dari objek kalau is sama dengan was kalu are sama dgn were objeknya

Kita menggunakan was were dalam bentuk lampau atau past, sedangkan kita menggunakan am ia dan are dalam bentuk sekarang atau present. Misalnya I was born in 2003 dan I am asked by my mother to clean my room every week. Kalimat pertama adalah kalimat yang menjelaskan kejadian pada masa lampau, sedangkan kalimat kedua menjelaskan kejadian yang terjadi di masa sekarang yang diulang-ulang.

10. buat kalimat tanya menggunakan is, am, are, was, were, masing² dua beserta Jawaban: Yes/ No Questions​


[Is]
1. Is Tina going to attend school tomorrow?
Jawaban - Yes, she is going to attend school tomorrow.
2. Is Andi afraid of the dark?
Jawaban - No, Andi is not afraid of the dark.

[Am]
1. Am I supposed to be in this meeting?
Jawaban - Yes, you are supposed to be in this meeting.
2. Am I the only one who dislike spicy food?
Jawaban - No, I also dislike like spicy food.

[Are]
1. Are they going to study for next week’s test?
Jawaban - Yes, they are going to study for next week’s test.
2. Are Lala and Jasmine sisters?
Jawaban - No, Lala and Jasmine are not sisters.

[Was]
1. Was your brother at my restaurant last night?
Jawaban - Yes, my brother was at your restaurant last night.
2. Was Lily hospitalized last week?
Jawaban - No, Lily was not hospitalized last week.

[Were]
1. Were they at the mall last night?
Jawaban - Yes, they were at the mall last night.
2. Were the older kids nice to you?
Jawaban - No, the older kids were not nice to me.
