15. contoh cerita liburan di malang dengan bahasa inggris


Writing Recount Text

My Holiday in Malang

Last month I went to Malang with my family for a ten days holiday. We visited many popular attractions there in Malang. We also visited my 80 years old maternal grandparents.

We stayed in Pohon Inn Hotel which located close to Batu Secret Zoo. There we visited animal museum. The zoo features a wide range of animals throughout various areas such as savannah, african village, eagles, fantasy land, river adventures, feeding birds, etc. I had so much fun there, and my brothers loved there too.

My second favorite destination that we visited was Museum Angkut. It is the first transportation museum in Indonesia. I saw many displays of transportation modes from countries and continents.

Overall I really enjoyed my holiday there and I can’t wait to comeback the end of this year. We are planning to visit Bromo next time.

Pembahasan

Recount text adalah sebuah bacaan yang memiliki fungsi untuk menceritakan kembali kejadian atau pengalaman di masa lalu. Tujuannya untuk menghibur dan memberikan informasi bagi para pembaca.

Ada beberapa macam recount text, pada teks soal merupakan contoh personal recount yang menceritakan ulang pengalaman pribadi penulis tentang liburannya bersama keluarga ke Malang.

26. contoh cerita liburan tahun baru dalam bahasa inggris dan terjemahannya


Contoh cerita liburan tahun baru. Libur tahun baru merupakan hari libur Internasional dalam rangka merayakan pergantian tahun masehi. Untuk menceritakan liburan tahun baru yang telah berlalu, menggunakan tensis kalimat yang telah terjadi.

Pembahasan

                                          New Year Holiday Story

The last school holiday, I spent the time with my family on vacation to a relative's house in Madiun, approximately 6 hour trip by bus from our home in Malang.

On the way there, we saw many beautiful views such as rice fields, mountains, etc. Having arrived at the uncle's house, we were greeted by our relatives who had not met us for a long time. The next day, we walked around the town of Madiun until the sky got dark. Finally we decided to go home at 6:00 p.m. on that day.

On the next day, I forgot that it was the New Year. Sudenly, one of my cousins ​​came and asked me to celebrate the New Year's Eve together. At night, I went together with him to the center of the city of Madiun which had already been so crowded because a lot of people who also wanted to celebrate the New Year with his family. When the countdown arrived, the sky which was dark at once then became so colorfully decorated by fireworks. We prayed together and watched the fireworks lightening the sky that night.

Suddenly, the holiday passed quickly. The last New Year's holiday experience in the city of Malang was so impressive for me.

Terjemahan dalam Bahasa Indonesia

                                       Cerita Liburan Tahun Baru

Liburan sekolah yang lalu, aku menghabiskan waktu bersama keluarga dengan berlibur ke rumah saudara di Madiun, kurang lebih 6 jam perjalanan menggunakan angkutan bis dari rumah kami di Malang.

Dalam perjalanan kami melihat banyak pemandangan yang indah seperti sawah, gunung ,dan lain lain. Sesampainya di rumah paman, kami disambut oleh saudara-saudara kami yang sudah lama tak berjumpa dengan kami. Keesokan harinya, kami jalan-jalan mengelilingi kota Madiun hingga sore hari. Akhirnya kami memutuskan untuk pulang pada pukul 18.00 pada hari itu.

Di hari berikutnya, aku lupa bahwa hari itu adalah tepat di tahun baru. Tiba - tiba salah seorang sepupuku datang dan mengajak untuk merayakan malam pergantian tahun bersama. Di malam harinya aku pergi bersamanya ke pusat kota Madiun yang sudah ramai sekali karena banyak orang yang juga ingin merayakan tahun baru bersama keluarganya. Saat perhitungan mundur tiba, langit yang tadinya gelap sekarang menjadi warna-warni dihiasi oleh kembang api. Kami berdoa bersama sambil menyaksikan kembang api yang menerangi langit malam itu.

Tak terasa libur pun berlalu dengan cepat. Pengalaman libur tahun baru yang lalu di kota Malang sungguh mengesankan bagiku.

Holiday in Singapore
During school holidays last time, my family and I went traveling to Singapore, I arrived in Singapore in the evening. We stayed in a hotel
The next day, we went to Universal studios in Sentosa Island. Just behind my hotel there are shops selling bus and entrance tickets to Universal Studios. My family and I had breakfast first before going there.
After breakfast, we came out and saw a bus heading to Universal studios had come. We then ran hastily to the ticket agent. In a hurried situation I said “I want to buy tickets”, because the seller speaks very fast, I’m difficult to understand what it means. She asked “Do you want to buy a bus ticket and entrance as well?” I replied, “Yes, I want to buy tickets for 3 adults and 1 child.” Then she told the price. We were shocked because the tickets were so expensive
We had fun playing at Universal Studios. We didn’t aware that it was afternoon. I’m very disappointed because we did not have enough time to play all the rides.
We had to go out and went back to hotel. I will never forget that holiday experience.

At The Seaside

Last year, Mr dan Mrs. Jones and their family went to the seaside for their summer holidays. They left the city very early in the morning on the 31st of July. The weather was not very fine and they wore their overcoats because they felt cold. At 10 o'clock, the sun began to shine, and two hours later, when I met them at the station the sun was shining brightly. They took off their overcoats and we all went to the same restaurant where we sat down to a good lunch. We ate fish and some very good mutton, and drank two bottles of excellent wine.

After lunch, we went to the sea, which my little girl Betty saw for the first time. We lay in the sun for two hours and then we went into the water which was very warm. When we came out of the water, the sun burnt us, so we sat down in the shade of some rocks. Mr. Jones lit a cigarette and I read the newspaper.

Then we all went back to our hotel. Mrs. Jones wrote a few letters and postcards, whilst Mr. Jones and I played a game of billiards.

After dinner, we went out and saw the sun set in a bright red sky. Then we went to bed, and slept very well.

The next morning, I awoke late, got up and had breakfast. My little girl wanted to fo into the sea again, but the wind blew so hard that it was not possible to bathe. We went out, however, and stood and looked at the sea for some time. It was very beautiful. I told Betty to hold her hat, but she held it only for a minute or two and then let it go. In the afternoon, the wind stopped and we went on the pier where we heeard a very good concert.
