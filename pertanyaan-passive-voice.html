3. Bikin kalimat passive voice in present tense 5 ada active voice sama passive voiceBikin kalimat passive voice in past tense ada 5 active voice sama passive voice​


Jawaban:

present tense

1. [Active Voice] My little sister asks the waiter to bring a water. (Adikku meminta pelayan untuk membawakan segelas air)

[Passive Voice] The waiter is asked by my little sister to bring a water. (Pelayan dimintai adikku untuk membawakan segelas air)

2. [Active Voice] Galang eats fried rice every moring. (Gembul makan nasi goreng pagi ini)

[Passive Voice] Fried rice is eaten by galang every morning. (Nasi goreng dimakan Gembul pagi ini)

3.[Active Voice] I ride a bike with my friends (Aku mengendarai sepeda dengan teman temanku)

[Passive Voice] The bike is ridden by me with my friends. (Sepeda dikendarai olehku bersama teman teman ku)

4. [Active Voice] Bambang washes those friuts in the river. (Bambang mencuci buah-buahan di sungai)

[Passive Voice] Those fruits are washed by Bambang in the river. (Buah-buahan dicuci bambang di sungai)

5. [Active Voice] I read some books every day. (Aku membaca beberapa buku setiap hari)

[Passive Voice] Some books are red by me everyday

past simple tense

1. [Active Voice] I bought this shoes yesterday. (Aku membeli sepatu ini kemarin)

[Passive Voice] This shoes was bought by me yesterday. (Sepatu ini dibeli olehku kemarin)

2. [Active Voice] She called me last night (Dia menelponku tadi malam)

[Passive Voice] I was called last night by her (Aku ditelpon tadi malam olehnya)

3. [Active Voice] Dibyo played soccer yesterday. (Dibyo bermain sepak bola kemarin)

[Passive Voice] Soccer was played by Dibyo yesterday. (Sepak bola dimainkan oleh Dibyo kemarin)

4.[Active Voice] I met them in the park. (Aku bertemu dengan mereka di taman)

[Passive Voice] They were met by me in the park. (Mereka ditemukan olehku di taman)

5. [Active Voice] She sent me a message an hour ago (Dia mengirim pesan padaku sejam yg lalu)

[Passive Voice] I was sent a message by her an hour ago. (Aku dikirimi pesan olehnya sejam yg lalu)


4. apakah perbedaan active voice dan passive voice ?


active voice yaitu kalimat aktif= S di depan
passive voice yaitu kalimat pasif= S di belakang
passive voice yaitu kalimat pasifkalau passive voice itu subject kalimat tidak melakukan aksi,melainkan menerima aksi. Sedangkan active voice subject berhubungan langsung dengan verb dengan bertindak sebagai pelaku atau aksi

5. Fungsi active voice dan passive voice


Active voice = Kalimat Aktif
Subjek - predikat - objek 
Rika menendang bola

Passive voice = Kalimat pasif
Objek - predikat - subjek
Bola ditendang Rika

kegunaan active voice adalah untuk menjelaskan subjek melakukan apa pada objek
the function of active voice is to describe what the subject do to the object

kegunaan passive voice adalah untuk menjelaskan objek diapakan subjek
the function of passive voice is to describe what happened to the object 

6. Apa perbedaan passive voice dan active voice?


Kalimat aktif (active voice) adalah kalimat dimana subject-nya melakukan pekerjaan, sebaliknya, kalimat pasif (passive voice) adalah kalimat dimana subject-nya dikenai pekerjaan oleh object kalimat. Active voice lebih sering digunakan dalam kehidupan sehari-hari dibandingkan dengan passive voice. active voice bsa menggunkan verb 1 dan 2, sdangkn passive voice menggunkn verb 3

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran bentuk kalimat Active dan Passive Voice dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Perlu diketahui bahwa terdapat 5 Non Convertible Tenses, artinya terdapat 5 bentuk kalimat yang tidak bisa dirubah ke dalam bentuk Passive Voice:

Present Perfect Continuous TensePast Perfect Continuous TenseFuture Perfect Continuous TenseFuture Continuous TenseFuture Perfect Tense

Kalimat aktif subjeknya aktif melakukan aksi dalam kalimat.

Kalimat pasif subjeknya pasif dikenai aksi dalam kalimat.

Active Voice:

1. Simple Present Tense:

(+) Subject + Verb-1 S/ES + Object

2. Present Continuous Tense:

(+) Subject + Tobe (is/am/are) + Verb-ing + Object

3. Present Perfect Tense:

(+) Subject + Have/Has + Verb-3 + Object

4. Simple Past Tense:

(+) Subject + Verb-2 + Object

5. Past Continuous Tense:

(+) Subject + Tobe (was/were) + Verb-ing + Object

6. Past Perfect Tense:

(+) Subject + Had + Verb-3 + Object

7. Simple Future Tense:

(+) Subject + Will + Verb-1 + Object

8. Future Continuous Tense:

(+) Subject + Will + Be + Verb-ing + Object

9. Future Perfect Tense:

(+) Subject + Will + Have + Verb-3 + Object

Passive Voice:

1. Passive Voice [Simple Present Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (is/am/are) + Verb3

2. Passive Voice [Present Continuous Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (is/am/are) + being + Verb3

3. Passive Voice [Present Perfect Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + have/has + been + Verb3

4. Passive Voice [Simple Past Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (was – were) + Verb 3

5. Passive Voice [Past Continuous Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (was – were) + Being + Verb3

6. Passive Voice [Past Perfect Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + had + been + Verb3

7. Passive Voice [Simple Future Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + will + be + Verb3

26. Berikan 10 kalimat passive voice beserta structure passive voice thanks :)


formula passive voice : S (direct object) + was/were + past participle + /-by
formula diatas untuk transitive verb
passive voice : S (object of preposition) + was/were + past participle + preposition + /-by ... (agent)
formula diatas untuk intransitive verb
ex :
1. The letter was left when he was away from his desk
2. sardines and rice were eaten for dinner by Tio

rumus kalimat pasif voice:
Subject penerima/Object + to be (sesuai tenses) + Verb III + by + subject pelaku + keterangan waktu

2. Lia was meet by Ani yesterday
3. A car is being washed by Tarjo
