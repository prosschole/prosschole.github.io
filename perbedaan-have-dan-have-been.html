1. apa bedanya have been dengan have?


1.) Have Been ; merupakah 'to be' yang dipakai pada jenis Perfect Tenses.
Misalnya, I have been here since 1997. (Saya sudah ada disini sejak 1997)

2.) Have ; to be yang digunakan pada kalimat jenis Present Tense
Misalnya, I have a furious spirit (Saya punya semangat membara)
 have artinya mempunyai. fungsi have dalam kalimat adalaha sebagia kata kerja bentuk pertama (tenses simple present) sedangkan have been adalah kata kerja bantu yang artinya sudah dalam tenses present perfect tense (kalimat pasif). have been diikuti oleh kata kerja bentuk ketiga dalam kalimat pasif contoh; the books have been bought by them. sementera untuk have been yang diikuti oleh kata kerja + ING adalah utk tenses present perfect continuous tense contoh they have been studying english since last year. ada juga tenses present perfect yang menggunakan kata have + V3 (have dsini berfungsi sbg kata kerja bantu) = kalimat aktif

9. Apa perbedaan "I have + Verb 3" dengan "I have been ..." tolong dong penjelasan nya terimakasih ​


꧁Simple Present Perfect Tense꧂

꧁Present Perfect Continuous Tense꧂

What is the difference between "I have + Verb 3" and "I have been..."?

Answer:

I Have + Verb3

"I have + V3" merupakan bentuk dari Simple Present Perfect, di mana bentuk ini digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu kegiatan yang telah terjadi di masa lampau/masa lalu. Contoh:

I have done mathematics task last week — Saya telah mengerjakan tugas matematika minggu lalu➺ Kata yang bercetak tebal merupakan keterangan waktu dari kegiatan tersebut yang berarti kegiatan tersebut sudah berlalu/terjadi, sedangkan kata yang bergarisbawah merupakan kata kerja dari kalimat itu sendiri___________________I Have Been

"I have been" merupakan bentuk dari Simple Present Perfect Continuous di mana kata "been" merupakan to be bentuk ketiga. Simple Present Perfect Continuous Tense digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu kegiatan yang sudah terjadi di masa lalu namun masih berlangsung hingga saat ini. Contoh:

I have been studying for two hours — Aku sudah belajar selama dua jam. ➺ Saat kalimat ini diucapkan, subjek (aku) dalam kalimat tersebut masih belajar. ___________________Kesimpulan

"I have + V3" merupakan bentuk dari Simple Present Perfect yang berfungsi untuk menyatakan suatu kegiatan yang telah dilakukan di masa lalu. Sedangkan "I have been" merupakan bentuk dari Simple Present Perfect Continuous yang berfungsi untuk menyatakan suatu kegiatan yang telah dilakukan di masa lalu namun masih berlangsung hingga saat kini. Contoh:

I have studied for two hours — Saya sudah belajar selama dua jam (Sudah berlalu) ➺ Simple Present Perfect ➺ I + have + V3 (studied) ➺ I have been studying for two hours — Saya sudah belajar selama dua jam (Sudah dilakukan selama dua jam dan masih berlangsung) ➺ Simple Present Perfect Continuous ➺ I + have + been + V-ing (studying)

PEMBAHASANSIMPLE PRESENT PERFECT➽ Definisi

Berfungsi untuk menyatakan suatu pekerjaan/kegiatan yang terjadi di masa lampau, namun masih ada kaitannya dengan masa sekarang.

➽ Pola Kalimat Secara Umum(+) S + has/have + V3 + O(-) S + has/have + not + V3 + O(?) Has/have + S + V3 + O + ?

➽ Keterangan

Has digunakan untuk subjek:

He ➺ Dia laki-lakiShe ➺ Dia perempuanIt ➺ Kata ganti benda/hewan (itu) Nama orang tunggal (Emdy, Seekor kucing, Kucing itu)

Have digunakan untuk subjek:

I ➺ SayaYou ➺ Kamu/kalianWe ➺ KamiThey ➺ MerekaNama orang banyak (Emdy dan Thian, Kucing-kucing itu)

➽ Contoh Kalimat Positive

❦ Subjek Tunggal [kecuali "Saya (I)]

➺ S + has + V3 + O + C

He has studied last nightA cat has eaten tuna fish since it was still two months oldEmdy has slept for two hours

❦ Subjek Jamak [termasuk "Saya (I)]

➺ S + have + V3 + O + C

I have studied last nightCat and Dog have played together since they were still one year oldEmdy and Thian have slept for two hours

➽ Contoh Kalimat Negative

❦ Subjek Tunggal [kecuali "Saya (I)]

➺ S + has + not + V3 + O + C

He has not studied last nightA cat has not eaten tuna fish since it was still two months oldEmdy has not slept for two hours

❦ Subjek Jamak [termasuk "Saya (I)]

➺ S + have + not + V3 + O + C

I have not studied last nightCat and Dog have not played together since they were still one year oldEmdy and Thian have not slept for two hours

➽ Contoh Kalimat Interrogative

❦ Subjek Tunggal [kecuali "Saya (I)]

➺ Has + S + V3 + O + C

Has he studied last night?Has a cat eaten tuna fish since it was still two months old?Has Emdy slept for two hours?

❦ Subjek Jamak [termasuk "Saya (I)]

➺ Have + S + V3 + O + C

Have I studied last night?Have cat and dog played together since they were still one year old?Have Emdy and Thian slept for two hours?________________

SIMPLE PRESENT PERFECT CONTINUOUS TENSE

Berfungsi untuk menyatakan suatu pekerjaan/kegiatan yang sudah terjadi di masa lalu namun masih berlangsung hingga saat ini.

➽ Pola Kalimat Secara Umum(+) S + has/have + been + V-ing + O(-) S + has/have + not + been + V-ing + O(?) Has/have + S + been + V-ing + O + ?

➽ Contoh Kalimat Positive

❦ Subjek Tunggal [kecuali "Saya (I)]

➺ S + has + been + V-ing + O

He has been studying for three hoursEmdy has been cooking for two hours

❦ Subjek Jamak [termasuk "Saya (I)]

➺ S + have + been + V-ing + O

I have been studying for three hoursThian and I have been cooking for two hours

➽ Contoh Kalimat Negative

❦ Subjek Tunggal [kecuali "Saya (I)]

➺ S + has + not + been + V-ing + O

He has not been studying for three hoursEmdy has not been cooking for two hours

❦ Subjek Jamak [termasuk "Saya (I)]

➺ S + have + not + been + V-ing + O

I have not been studying for three hoursThian and I have not been cooking for two hours

➽ Contoh Kalimat Interrogative

❦ Subjek Tunggal [kecuali "Saya (I)]

➺ Has + S + been + V-ing + O + C + ?

Has he been studying for three hours?Has Emdy been cooking for two hours?

❦ Subjek Jamak [termasuk "Saya (I)]

➺ Have + S + been + V-ing + O + C + ?

Have you been studying for three hours?Have Diana and Riana been cooking for two hours?

15. apa bedanya must been dengan must have?!!!!​


Jawaban :

(Must have)

Must digunakan untuk menyatakan keharusan yang berasal dari si pembicara, sedangkan have to menyatakan keharusan yang berdasarkan situasi.

Sedangkan "Must been" itu setau saya gaada, yang ada itu "Must have been", "Must have to"

Jadi "Must been" itu gaada, penulisan yang benernya dari "Must been" itu dibelakangnya pasti ada tambahan "Have", ya tapi kalau mau tau perbedaan "Must been ( Yang kata "Must been" ini sebenernya gaada klo ga pake "have")
Dan "Must have" ya sama aja.

Have bisa diartikan sebagai : pernah,punya, dan harus, dll.

Must bisa diartikan sebagai : Pernah

Intinya kalau mau nulis "Must been" itu pakai "Have" ya dibelakang kata "Been" nya. Gaada bedanya sih sebenernya mau pake "Must been" kek atau "Must have been" kek ya sama aja. Tapi, kalo "Must have been" ya lebih bener dan lebih lengkap aja ya.

28. The child ate up all the meal we had served. He .... Very hungry. A. Will have been B. Could have been C. Must have been D. Would have been E. Must have


He (C) must have been very hungry. Arti dari kalimat tersebut adalah dia pasti tadinya sangat lapar. Must have been adalah frasa yang digunakan sebagai dugaan akan kejadian di masa lampau. Simak penjelasan berikut.

Pembahasan

Must adalah salah satu bentuk modal yang artinya harus. Must dalam penggunaannya secara umum menunjukkan sebuah keharusan atau kewajiban.

Contoh:

You must wear uniform clothes to school.The students must submit their assignment today.

Must juga digunakan untuk menunjukkan dugaan atau prediksi di mana ada sejumlah bukti tertentu. Dalam bentuk sekarang, must diikuti verb base. Sedangkan dalam bentuk lampau, must dipasangkan dengan have menjadi 'must have' diikuti verb 3.

Contoh:

The bag is really nice. It must be expensive.Last night, you looked so tired. You must have worked really hard.
