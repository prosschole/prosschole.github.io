Came Late to school

Last wednesday, I came late to my school because I played games untill 2.00 am in the night. Because that I woke up late.

I woke up about 6.30 am and the class would be began at 7.00 am. I ran to bathroom to take a bath. I usually had a breakfast after took a bath, but in that day I did not do that.

I always went to school by my moyorcycle. But in that day, I forgot where I put the key. So, I went to the school by public transportion. It made me took a longer time. I arrived at school at 7.15 am, I ran to my class but I saw my teacher has stood in front of the class to teach. I entered to my class and of course my teacher was angry to me because I came late.

It was my bad experience and I hoped I would not do that again.

Rabu yang lalu, saya datang terlambat ke sekolah saya karena saya bermain game sampai jam 2 pagi di malam hari. Karena saya terbangun telat.

Saya bangun sekitar jam 6.30 pagi dan kelas akan dimulai pukul 07.00. Aku berlari ke kamar mandi untuk mandi. Biasanya saya sarapan setelah mandi, tapi pada hari itu saya tidak melakukan itu.

Itu membuat saya butuh waktu lebih lama. Saya tiba di sekolah jam 7.15 pagi, saya berlari ke kelas saya tapi saya melihat guru saya berdiri di depan kelas untuk mengajar. 10. buatlah satu cerita tentang amazing adventures sea ( pakai bhs inggris ) untuk storytelling


Ini narrative bukan?

Kalau narrative aku coba bikin cerita pendek. Kalau disuruhnya descriptive, jangan pakai ceritaku.

Emily wants to be a mermaid. She wants to be a mermaid because she was amazed at sea's beauty. Every Saturday, she went to the local aquarium shop just to see many kinds of aquatic life. Betta fishes, Catfishes, Regal Blue Tangs, Lobsters, but the most beautiful fish, that she loved are clownfishes.

One summer vacation, Emily went to a beach with her friends, Amy and Alicia. Things went well, until a crowd formed. They saw a baby whale stuck on the beachside.

A man called for help. But the others quickly took some pictures. The whale's skin became dry and drier every moment, and nobody noticed that.

"Wait, whales need water too!" Emily rushed to the poor baby whale, using her bottle, she poured the water to the whale. Alicia and Amy helped her, by getting more water.

Eventually everyone realized that the whale needs more help to stay wet. They filled their buckets, bottles, and even caps with sea water and kept on showering him until a tow boat came to the rescue. It turns out that his mother stayed as near as possible to make sure he's safe.

The whales did not leave. Instead they called the three girls, that helped him to stay dry. Of course Emily, Amy, and Alicia have to swim towards them.

"Thank you for saving me," he said.

"You can talk?" Asked Emily.

"You are amazing!" Amy cheered.

"That's cool, I have never seen a whale talk!" Alicia is impressed.

"You can ride us, and we will take you where ever you want!"

"But we are not mermaids," Amy said.

"We can't breathe underwater."

The mother whale said, "You don't have to be one. Just hold your breath and it will be fine."

"I think we know who really wants to go to the sea," Alicia winked.

"Okay, take us to the clownfishes' home!" Emily said.

The whales were being ridden by the three. Sometimes they get down to let the kids see the sea's beauty. Emily is very fascinated. She saw a turtle, a pack of jellyfish, some seahorses, a school of tuna, in their natural habitat.

When Emily can't see those animals, it's time to take a breath. "Guahhh..." she gasped, before sinking in the water again.

Finally, they saw a coral reef with some anemones.

"IT'S A CLOWNFISH!"

"Well, here you are, remember not to touch them or the anemones! And tell me if you are done, so we can return you."

Emily looked at her left. Not just one, two, but a school of them. She was very happy to see them in the sea, their home. Even though she would not see the fishes for a long time, because she had to go home with her friends.

And her friends seems to agree with her that sea life is beautiful.
