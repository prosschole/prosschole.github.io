1. Contoh Dialog Compliment (dalam bahasa Inggris) 2 orang (Tentang Prestasi)TOLONG JANGAN TERLALU SINGKAT​


Jawaban:

A: Hey, B! How was the essay competition? Did you win the trophy?

Hei, B! Bagaimana kompetisi esainya? Apakah Anda memenangkan trofi?

B: Thank God, I actually did. Fortunately, I succeeded in getting first place. It was so amazing.

Terima kasih Tuhan, saya benar-benar melakukannya. Untungnya saya berhasil mendapatkan posisi juara pertama. Itu sangat menakjubkan.

A: Wow that’s a great job, B. Congratulations on your success! I’m happy with that

Wow itu pekerjaan yang bagus, B. Selamat atas kesuksesan Anda! Saya turut bahagia atas itu.

B: Thank you very much for saying so, A. How about your English debate competition? Did you also win?

Terima kasih banyak telah mengatakan demikian, A. Bagaimana dengan kompetisi debat bahasa Inggris Anda? Apakah Anda juga menang?

A: Unfortunately, I didn’t get first place. I got second place but was thrilled because I’ve tried my best and put in all my hard work.

Sayangnya, saya tidak mendapatkan tempat pertama. Saya mendapat juara kedua tetapi sangat senang karena saya sudah mencoba yang terbaik dan melakukan semua kerja keras saya.

B: That’s the spirit, A! I am sure you did it very well. I congratulate you on your great success.

Itu semangatnya, A! Saya yakin Anda melakukannya dengan sangat baik. Saya ucapkan selamat atas kesuksesan besar Anda.

A: Thank you very much, B. I still have to fight for another English debate competition this month.

Terima kasih banyak, B. Saya masih harus berjuang untuk kompetisi debat bahasa Inggris lainnya bulan ini.

B: Cool! Let’s fight for it!

Keren! Mari kita perjuangkan!

28. contoh dialog compliment 2 orang tentang makan malam


compliment two person!

Lucy: Reply to the invitation soon. Did you receive an Evite?
Sarah: Yes, I got an electronic card sent by e-mail.
Lucy: Then you just need to click the link on the Evite email to RSVP.
Sarah: OK. Then what do I need to prepar
Lucy: Normally, you should bring a gift for your host. You can bring a bottle of wine or chocolates.
Sarah: I think I’ll bring chocolates. What about the dress?
Lucy: Well, it depends on who sent the invitation. If it is a friend or family member, you don’t need to be very formal, but don’t be underdressed.
Sarah: I see. Is there a specific etiquette that I need to follow during the dinner?
Lucy: Of course, there is. First, when sitting unfold your napkin and place it on your knee. When eating, if there are several cutlery sets beside the plate, start at the outside of your cutlery and work in.
Sarah: Table manner always makes me nervous. I’m afraid I make mistakes.
Lucy: Don’t worry. If you’re not sure what to do, just have a look to see what the other guests are doing.
Sarah: Can I cool food by blowing onto it?
Lucy: No, you can’t. And don’t leave lip-prints on glass
Sarah: Alright. Anything else?
Lucy: Don’t forget to compliment the food.
Sarah: That’s all? Thanks a lot, Lucy!
Lucy: Anytime, Sarah.
