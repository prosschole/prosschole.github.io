6. contoh cause effect and effect cause​


Jawaban :

Contoh 1

I was lost so I opened Google Maps (Saya tersesat sehingga saya membuka Google Maps)

Cause : I was lost

Effect : I opened Google Maps

Conjunction : so

Contoh 2

The journey was quite quick because the road was clear. (perjalanan cukup cepat karena jalanan bersih dari hambatan )

Cause : the road was clear.

Effect : the journey was quite quick

Conjunction : because

Contoh 3

As it is raining again, I will have to stay at home. (karena hujan sedang turun lagi, saya akan tinggal di rumah)

Cause : it is raining again.

Effect : I will have to stay at home.

Conjunction : as

Contoh 4

Since she had not paid the rent,she was told to vacate the flat. (Karena dia belum membayar sewa, dia diberitahu untuk mengosongkan ruangan.)

Cause : she had not paid the rent.

Effect : she was told to vacate the flat.

Conjunction : since

Contoh 5

When the ocean is extremely polluted, coral reefs die..

Cause : the ocean is extremely polluted.

Effect : coral reefs die.

Conjunction : when

Hal yang perlu diperhatikan adalah jika kata hubung terletak di awal kalimat, kita perlu menggunakan tanda baca koma. Akan tetapi, jika kata hubungnya berada di tengah, kita tidak perlu menggunakan koma.

As it was getting late, We decided to book into a hotel.

(Karena sudah larut, kami memutuskan untuk memesan hotel.)

You can go first as you're the oldest.

(Anda boleh duluan karena Anda yang tertua.)

19. tolong buat kan minimal 5 kalimat cause and effect menggunakan because of dan due to dengan teman fenomena alam


Cause and Effect

Cause (sebab) merupakan penyebab mengapa sesuatu dapat terjadi.

Effect (akibat) adalah hasil dari apa yang telah terjadi.

Cause and Effect sangat berkaitan satu sama lain. "If there is an effect, there must be a cause. If there is a cause, that could lead to effects."

Dalam membuat kalimat cause and effect, diperlukan conjunction (kata hubung), seperti: because, since, as, so, for, therefore, because of, due to, thus.

Contoh:

He was angry because we were late.It is raining heavily outside, so I can't come to your party.Since you can speak English, you have many friends from other countries.Tasya can't go to school today for she is unwell.The boss was happy as John did the work very well.The school is under construction, therefore I don't go to school today.Due to the heavy rain, there were many car accidents last night.We couldn't come on time because of traffic jam.

PembahasanBuat minimal 5 kalimat cause and effect menggunakan because of dan due to dengan tema fenomena alam!Because of bad weather, I got a cold.Because of sunlight, the plants can make food.My skin gets tanned because of sunbeam.Many people lost their homes because of landslide.Many people have respiratory problems because of forest fires.There were many car accidents due to the heavy rain last night.The falling leaves is due to the dry season.A shortage of water was due to the drought.The crops failure was due to the bad weather.Rainbow is due to reflection, refraction, and dispersion of light.

Perhatikan bahwa kata 'due to' dapat digantikan dengan 'caused by'. Sedangkan 'because of' dapat digantikan dengan 'on account of.
