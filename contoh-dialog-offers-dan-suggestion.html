14. Berikan contoh expressing offers and suggestion


A. Suggestion

a. Definition

Suggestion (saran) adalah mengajukan suatu

rencana untuk dipertimbangkan atau memberikan/menyumbangkan suatu ide. Hal-hal yang disarankan dapat diterima atau ditolak. Suggestion bersifat abstrak, dan hal-hal yang bisa disarankan seperti solution (solusi), advice (nasehat), plan (rencana), dan idea (ide).

b. Expressions Suggestion :

- Let's ...

- Why don't we ...

- We could ...

- What about ...

- I suggest that ...

- You might want to change ...

- I think ...

- I don't think ...

c. Responding Suggestion :

Accepting Suggestion

- Yes, let's go

- Ok, I will

- It's a good idea

- Okay, if you say so

- Let's

Declining Suggestion

- No, thank you. I don't feel like going

- Sorry, I think ...

- No, let's just do something else

- No, let's just go somewhere else

- Sorry, I can't. I have another appointment

- No, sorry.

d. Example :

- Dialogue 1 :

A : Let's go to the beach this weekend!

B : It's a good idea. Let's go!

- Dialogue 2 :

A : What don't you study for the test instead of playing outside?

B : Okay, if you say so

- Dialogue 3 :

A : I think you should go apologize to her

B : Sorry, I can't. It's her fault

- Dialogue 4 :

A : You might want to change your mind

B : No, sorry, I've planned this for months

B. Offers

a. Definition

Offers (tawaran) adalah memberikan sesuatu kepada seseorang sebagai hadiah (gifts), pertukaran (trade) atau bantuan (help). Pada umumnya, offers bersifat nyata (konkrit), dan hal-hal yang dapat diberikan kepada seseorang sebagai offers seperti food (makanan), money (uang), solution (solusi), friendship (persahabatan), atau bargain (penawaran).

b. Expression Offers :

- May I ...

- Can I ...

- Shall I ...

- Would you ...

- How about I ...

c. Responding Offers :

Accepting Offers

- Yes, please. I really appreciate it

- Thank you, it's very kind of you

- Yes, please, that be lovely

- Yes, please, that would be very kind of you

- Thank you, I appreciate your help

Declining Offers

- It's okay, I can do it myself

- No, thank you

- No, thanks

- Don't worry, I will do it myself

- That's alright, I will manage it on my own

d. Example :

- Dialogue 1 :

A : Can I help you?

B : Yes, please, I really appreciate your help

- Dialogue 2 :

A : Could I offer you something?

B : Yes, please, I want a cup of tea

- Dialogue 3 :

A : Would you care for another cup of tea?

B : No, thank you.

- Dialogue 4 :

A : Can I clean the car for you?

B : It's okay, I can do it myself

21. Contoh dialog suggestion dan offering dalam satu dialog


Pembahasan:

Sebelum membuat dialog mengenai suggestion dan offering, kita sebaiknya mengetahui kalimat yang digunakan untuk mengekspresikan suggestion dan offering terlebih dahulu. Berikut contohnya.

1. Expression of giving suggestion

- You should ...

- You ought to ...

- It's better for you to ...

- I suggest you ...

- I advise you to ...

- Why don't we/you ... ?

- How about ...?

2. expression of offering

Expression ini terbagi 2 yaitu, offering something dan offering help

Expression of offering help:

- May I help you?

- Can I help you?

- What can I do for you?

- Would you like me to ...?

- Can I lend you a hand with that?

Expression of offering things:

- Would you like some ...?

- Would you like something to ...?

- Should I get you ...?

- What can I get for you?

Berikut contoh dialognya.

A: B, what are you doing here?

B: I'm practicing for the next competition.

A: Don't you feel tired?

B: No, i don't.

A: But, you look so pale.

B: Do I?

A: Yeah, you should take a rest unless you will faint.

B: Yeah, i think so.

A: Here, let me help you.

B: Thanks

A: Don't push yourself.

B: Well, the competition is coming soon. i have to be well-prepared.

A: But, it doesn't mean you have to push yourself. You have to get enough rest, too.

B: You're right.

A: Should i get you a drink?

B: Yes, please.

A: Alright. I'll be back in a moment.

B: Thanks.
