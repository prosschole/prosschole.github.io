You, We, They utk tobe Have
Had digunakan utk Verb3
Has dan Have : Verb1 dan 2


Contoh :
He has a pen
You have a new car
The class had 40 students
Has => digunakan untuk subject " SHE, HE, IT "
Have => digunakan untuk subject " THEY, WE, I, YOU "
Had => digunakan untuk semua subject

Bedanya has, have, sama had :
Has / Have = bentuk PRESENT
Had = bentuk PAST

Jawaban:

Has dan Have keduanya memiliki arti MEMILIKI/MEMPUNYAI. Letak perbedaannya adalah Berdasarkan Penggunaan subject dalam kalimat.

Penjelasan:

Contoh Penggunaan dalam Present Tense.

Present Tense adalah tenses yg digunakan utk menunjukkan suatu Kegiatan atau Peristiwa yg saat ini atau berupa kebenaran umum / FAKTA dan sebuah kebiasaan/rutininitas.

Penggunaan has dan have dalam Present Tense yaitu Berdasarkan subject:

Plural = I, you, we, they + Have

Singular = He, She, it + has

Contoh pada kalimat Present Tense:

(+) S + VERB 1 (-s/es) + O

1. He/She + HAS + a new bag

= Dia MEMILIKI sebuah tas baru

2. I/you/we/they + HAVE + a new phone

= Saya/kamu/kita/mereka MEMILIKI sebuah hp baru

Catatan:

It adalah Subjek utk kata ganti yg mewakili HEWAN dan BENDA. maka contoh kalimatnya

1. Hewan

There is a giraffe. It + has + a long neck

= Ada seekor jerapah. [jerapah] itu MEMILIKI leher yg panjang.

2. Benda

There is a car. It + has + white color

= Ada sebuah mobil. [mobil] itu MEMILIKI warna putih

16. Gunakan tenses yang paling tepat Present perfect atau present perfect continuous tense. 1. (Have you, Have you been) working in the garden all day? You look exhausted. 2. She (wrote, has been writing) the book since she was in her twenties and at last it is finished. 3. You (have eaten, have been eating) chocolate. haven't you? There is some on your shirt 4. Who (has been messing, has messed) around with my papers? They are all over the place. 5. He (has played, has been playing) for the national team in 65 matches so far.


Have you been working in the garden all day? You look exhausted.She has been writing the book since she was in her twenties, and at last it is finished.You have eaten chocolate, haven't you? There is some on your shirt.Who has messed around with my papers? They are all over the place.He has played for the national team in 65 matches so far.

Pembahasan

Simple present perfect dan simple present perfect continuous memiliki persamaan dari segi waktu suatu aksi dimulai, yakni suatu aksi telah dimulai di masa yang telah lampau namun masih berkaitan dengan masa sekarang. Meskipun begitu, keduanya juga memiliki perbedaan, yakni present perfect digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu aksi yang telah selesai di waktu tertentu sedangkan present perfect continuous menyatakan suatu aksi yang sedang dilakukan di masa lampau namun belum diketahui dengan pasti apakah aksi tersebut masih berlangsung hingga kini atau tidak.

•ᴥ• Pattern:

Simple Present Perfect

(+) S + has/have + V3 + O/C.(–) S + has/have + not + V3 + O/C.(?) Has/Have + S + V3 + O/C?

Simple Present Perfect Continuous

(+) S + has/have + been + V-ing + O/C.(–) S + has/have + not + been + V-ing + O/C.(?) Has/Have + S + been + V-ing + O/C?

•ᴥ• Keterangan:

Has digunakan untuk subjek: he, she, it, dan nama tunggal. Have digunakan untuk subjek: I, you, we, they, dan nama banyak.

Penjelasan Soal

(1) Have you been working in the garden all day? You look exhausted.

Menggunakan bentuk simple present perfect continuous, karena pada kalimat di atas menanyakan perihal kegiatan yang telah berlangsung namun mungkin belum selesai hingga pertanyaan tersebut dilontarkan.

(2) She has been writing the book since she was in her twenties, and at last it is finished.

Menggunakan bentuk simple present perfect continuous.

(3) You have eaten chocolate, haven't you? There is some on your shirt.

Menggunakan bentuk simple present perfect, karena kalimat di atas menanyakan kegiatan yang telah dilakukan sebelum pertanyaan tersebut dilontarkan.

(4) Who has messed around with my papers? They are all over the place.

Menggunakan bentuk simple present perfect, karena kalimat di atas menanyakan kegiatan yang telah dilakukan sebelum pertanyaan tersebut dilontarkan.

(5) He has played for the national team in 65 matches so far.

Menggunakan bentuk simple present perfect, karena kalimat di atas menyatakan kegiatan yang telah dilakukan sebelum pernyataan tersebut dilontarkan.

19. penggunaan more fewer and less dalam kalimat ihave 10 books.my teacher has 20 books. my theacher has more than me. i have fewer books than my teacher


People often don’t know when to use less and when to use fewer in a sentence. Here’s how to get it right.Use fewer if you’re referring to people or things in theplural (e.g. houses, newspapers, dogs, students, children). For example:People these days are buying fewer newspapers.Fewer students are opting to study science-related subjects.Fewer than thirty children each year develop the disease. Use less when you’re referring to something that can’t be counted or doesn’t have a plural (e.g. money, air, time,music, rain). For example:It’s a better job but they pay you less money.People want to spend less time in traffic jams.Ironically, when I’m on tour, I listen to less music.
