8. membuat kalimat dengan kata dibawah ini yang berfungsi sebagai noun dan verb. masing-masing kata memiliki 2 kalimat 1. insert 2. insult 3. permit 4. overflow 5. progress 6. protest 7. rebel 8. record 9. survey 10. suspect


Membuat kalimat dengan kata-kata yang berfungsi sebagai noun dan verb. Masing-masing kata dibuat menjadi 2 kalimatJawabanPendahuluan

Dalam bahasa Inggris, banyak kata-kata yang memiliki banyak makna, dan banyak pula dari kata-kata tersebut yang bertulisan sama tetapi digunakan sebagai jenis kata yang berbeda.

Noun (kata benda) adalah kata yang berfungsi menamai sesuatu, dan digunakan untuk menempati posisi subjek atau objek dari kalimat.

Verb (kata kerja) adalah kata yang berfungsi menerangkan suatu kegiatan atau tindakan, dan digunakan untuk menempati posisi predikat kalimat.


Pembahasan

Berikut adalah contoh kalimat dari 10 kata pada soal sesuai jenis katanya

insert - sisipan (noun), menyisipkan (verb)

You may find inserts (n) in certain issues of newspapers or magazines.Please insert (v) your ATM card and enter your PIN number.

insult - penghinaan (noun), menghina (verb)

During childhood, he endured all the insults (n) the neighbors threw at him.Now that his life has changed and he has become successful, no one dares to insult (v) him.

permit - izin (noun), mengizinkan (verb)

You must have a permit (n) to reconstruct your house.You are permitted (v) to take these books home.

overflow - luapan, banjir (noun), meluap (verb)

There was an overflow (n) of grain harvest last summer. All the farmers cheered.Due to the heavy rain, the river overflowed (v) the banks and flooded nearby areas.

progress - kemajuan (noun), maju (verb)

Congratulation, Andy! You've made a tremendous progress (n) this semester.Overall, the construction is progressing (v) smoothly.

protest - protes, bantahan (noun), membantah, memprotes (verb)

There was a protest (n) in the labour union because workers were inadequately paid.The workers protested (v) as they didn't get enough pay.

rebel - pemberontak (noun), memberontak (verb)

The spoilt boy turned into a rebel (n) when his parents stopped spoiling him.The spoilt boy rebelled (v) when his parents stopped spoiling him.

record - rekaman, rekor (noun), merekam, mencatat (verb)

That singer made a gold record (n) in 1972.He recorded (v) his music in a church.

survey - survei, hasil penelitian (noun), meneliti, memsurvei (verb)

The results of the survey (n) show that more people are now inseparable from their smartphone.A group of scientists surveyed (v) some 1000 people in the village.

suspect - tersangka (noun), mencurigai (verb)

Last night, the police arrested a suspect (n) to the serial murder case.He was suspected (v) of murdering the family.
