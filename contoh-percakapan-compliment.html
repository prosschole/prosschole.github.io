12. Percakapan 2 orng tentang compliment minimal 15 percakapan


Formula yang digunakan pada umumnya ketika membuat ungkapan compliment adalah sebagai berikut:

1. How + Adjective + Noun + is/are.

Contoh:

How beautiful flower is.How big ocean isHow careful jobs are

2. What + Adjetive + Noun.

Contoh:

What a beautiful flower.What a big houseWhat a wonderful job

3. Noun Phrase + is/look + (really) + Adjective.

Contoh:

Your hair is really beautifulThe skirt is niceHis performance is outstanding

4. Noun/Pronoun + (really) + like/love + Noun Phrase.

Contoh:

We all really love your performanceAll teachers really love your commitmentsHis father really like his test result

5. Pronoun + is + (really) + Adjective + Noun Phrase.

Contoh:

That is a really beautiful t-shirtThis is a really wonderful paintingThat’s a great looking car.

Sebelum membuat percakapan 2 orang tentang compliment, ada beberapa kalimat yang dapat digunakan untuk menyampaikan compliment. Compliment adalah ungkapan yang berupa pujian untuk seseorang atau sekelompok orang. Berikut akan dibahas beberapa kalimat yang bisa digunakan untuk compliment.

Pembahasan

Beberapa kalimat yang dapat digunakan untuk menyampaikan compliment.

1. Ungkapan yang dapat digunakan untuk memuji penampilan atau kepribadian:

You look beautiful/handsome.I love your jacket/shoes/dress.I like your new hairstyle.You look great in yellow/red/pink.Your son is so great/diligent/kind.Your boyfriend is really funny/serious.

2. Ungkapan yang dapat digunakan untuk memuji kemampuan/bakat/kerja keras:

Great job!Good job!Nice work!Well done!You're so creative.You're really talented.You're really smart.

3. Ungkapan yang dapat digunakan untuk memuji suatu produk/benda:

These cookies are delicious.Your house is so big.Your new car is so cool.Your new website is so awesome.This nasi goreng is very tasty.The new game is very fun.

Selain memberikan pujian, kita juga perlu memahami beberapa ungkapan untuk merespon pujian. Beberapa ungkapan ini dapat digunakan untuk merespon compliment:

Thank you!I'm really happy with it.That's sweet of you to notice.I appreciate you saying that.Thanks. It was so hard.Thanks. We did our best.

CONGRATULATION  In daily, it’s familiar to congratulate someone’s success and the particular events.  Congratulation is an utterance used to express the appreciation toward someone’s achievement or praise someone’s good fortune and also used to congratulate on the other specific moments for good wishes/respecting each other.

There are variety of ways in expressing congratulation , such as :

Please , accept my warmest congratulationsI’m very proud of your success. CongratsLet me congratulate on your successWarmest congratulations on the birth of your sweet baby...Congratulations. You've got it made.Happy anniversaryHappy Birthday
