Tanda waktu Past TenseYesterdayLast nightTwo days agoa few minutes ago

Rumus Past Tense

1. Verbal Sentence: Kalimat Verba karena ada kata kerja (verb) di dalam kalimatnya

(+) S + Verb 2 + O/C

(-) S + did not + Verb 1 + O/C

(?) Did + S + Verb 1 + O/C?

Kesimpulan

(?) Did + S + Verb 1 + O/C?
