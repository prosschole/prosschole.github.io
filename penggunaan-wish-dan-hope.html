1. Cara penggunaan Hope dan Wish


Jawaban:

PERBEDAAN WISH DAN HOPE

Wish dan Hope dipercaya merupakan dua kata yang mempunyai arti sama. Padahal keduanya berbeda pada konteks.

HOPE

Berbeda dengan wish, hope merupakan harapan yang bisa diwujudkan dan sangat mungkin terjadi.

Contohnya:

I hope we can still be friends.

WISH

Adalah harapan yang sulit diwujudkan bahkan bisa dibilang mustahil.

Contohnya:

I wish I can fly.

18. Hope vs wish (perbedaan hope wish bahasa Inggris)bantu jawab dong:)​


We all hope you a speedy recovery.I hope she likes the flowers.My mother hope that you will visit us.I wish I was taller.I hope you can understand me.Don't forget, wish me luck.I wish it doesn't rain tomorrow.I hope you had finished your homework.

Perbedaan:Hope itu adalah kata yang menyatakan harapan atau keinginan yang mungkin saja dapat terjadi di dunia ini.Wish itu adalah kata yang menunjukkan harapan atau keinginan namun, harapan-harapan tersebut hampir mustahil untuk terwujud dan hanya ada sedikit kemungkinan untuk terjadi.

28. contoh percakapan menggunakan wish and hope


Nina : It's almost holiday! I really can't wait! Do you already have a plan for your holiday, Reyhan?

Reyhan : No, not yet. I think my family and I will just spend the holiday at home. My father does not really like travelling. He is such a boring person. I really wish I could go to the mountain. I really need fresh air, literally.

Nina : How could someone do not like travelling? You father is so unique. Or, your father must be a very hard worker. He only thinks about his job. But, I hope he changes his mind this time.

Reyhan : I hope he will change his mind forever so we can travel all around the world every holiday.

Nina : Haha you're right!

Reyhan : So what about you? Do you already have a plan?

Nina : Yes, off course, that's why I really can't wait. My family and I are going to go to Japan.

Reyhan : Really? Ah, I wish I were your brother so I could go to Mountain Fuji.

Nina : You still can join us. Just buy the ticket and then you fly with us.

Reyhan : I need to rob a bank for the ticket. You should help me.

Nina : No way!

Semoga membantu :)

30. pengertian hope and wish beserta contoh dan penggunaannya


artinya sama2 harapan/berharap, namun dalam penggunaannya wish biasa digunakan dalam percakapan sehari-hari yg biasa saja misal; wish u all the best. kalo hope itu benar2 mengharap/ harapannya lebih dalam misal; i hope i can finished this work 
hope dan wish berarti semoga,berharap

contoh :
1. Present Wishes 
S+Wish+S2+V2

Example :
I Wish,I know what I to do

2. Future wishes

S+Wish+S2(subject ke-2)+could/would(kata lampau dari can/will)
Example :
They Wish the could visit paris

3. Past Wishes 
S+Wish+S2+Had+V3

Example :
I Wish my teacher had given mean A+
