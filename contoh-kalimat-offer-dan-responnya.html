10. contoh kalimat congratulation dan responnya


We congratulate people when we hear good news about someone's achievement, promotion, graduation, etc. The social purpose of congratulating people is to express our happiness about someone's achievement.

Pembahasan

Contoh kalimat congratulation dan responsnya yaitu

1. “Congratulations on your wedding. I wish you a happy life ahead.”

Response: “It’s so nice of you to say so.”

2. “Please accept my warmest congratulations on your success.”

Response: “Thank you so much.”

3. “I’d like to congratulate you on the opening of your new store.”

Response: “Thank you, I really appreciate it.”

4. “Congratulations on your new business.”

Response: “Thank you.”

5. “Congratulations on your new position as the manager in your company.”

Response: “Thank you for your kind guidance.”

6. “Good job on your promotion!”

Response: "Thanks!"

7. “Congrats on your wedding.”

Response: “Thanks a lot.”

8. “I saw your performance on the dance show tonight. That’s so fantastic!”

Response: “Thanks! I’m so happy to hear that.”

9. “Congrats on your graduation! You did a good job.”

Response: “Thank you.”

10. “Good job!”

Response: “Thank you.”

17. Buatlah 5 contoh kalimat accepting an offer and refusing an offer


The ability to offer help or something is very important because we often meet a situation in daily life where we need to do that. In English, there is an interesting expression that we can use to offer help. We can ask "Do you need a hand?" when others seem to need assistance. 'A hand' in this sentence means help.

Offering help or something to others is important. But, knowing how to response is important as well. There are two types of responses: to accept and to refuse.

Answers:

Accepting offer

Dialogue 1

A: Is there anything I can do to help?

B: Great! Could you please open the window? It's too hot here.

A: Sure!

B: Thanks!

Dialogue 2

A: Would you like some tea?

B: Thanks! You're the best.

Refusing

Dialogue 1

A: Do you need help?

B: It's okay. I'm almost done with it. Thank you for asking.

Dialogue 2

A: Would you like to join us for lunch today?

B: I'm sorry, I'm a bit busy today. I have to finish my final report today.

29. 1.Apa pengertian Offer?2.Buat contoh kalimat Offer?3.Apa pengertian Suggestion?4.Buat contoh kalimat Suggestion?Tolong dibantu nanti mau dikumpul​


Jawaban:

1. Offer adalah untuk memberitahu seseorang bahwa kamu akan memberi mereka sesuatu jika mereka mengingingkannya. Suggesting biasanya diungkapkan ketika kita memberi saran kepada orang lain, sedangkan offering ialah ketika kita menawarkan bantuan kepada orang lain.

2. Contoh kalimat offering

May I help you? / Bisa saya bantu?

Can I bring this luggage for you? / Bisakah saya membawa koper ini untuk Anda?

Shall I make a cup of tea for you? / Haruskah aku membuat secangkir teh untukmu?

Would you like me to find another product? / Apakah Anda ingin saya mencarikan produk lain?

3. Sesuai dengan namanya, kalimat suggestion secara harfiah adalah kalimat yang bertujuan untuk memberikan sugesti atau saran. Lebih luasnya, kalimat suggestion juga dipakai untuk memberikan nasihat, masukan, atau ide kepada orang lain.

4.I suggest that you meet her now. / Aku menyarankanmu untuk menemuinya sekarang. You might want see it now. / Kamu mungkin mau melihatnya sekarang. I think you need to see a doctor. / Aku pikir kamu perlu ke dokter. I don't think you should buy the bag. / Aku tidak berpikir kamu harus membeli tasnya.

Penjelasan:

Semoga Membantu

Sobat Pintar kita Offer dalam bahasa Indonesia berarti tawaran. Tawaran adalah bukan seperti menawar harga ya Sobat Pintar. Tetapi offer berupa menawarkan jasa, barang, ataupun hal lainnya.

2.Buat contoh kalimat Offer?

a. Would you like a cup of tea?

a. (Apakah kamu mau minum teh)

B. Can I help you?

B. (Bolehkah saya membantu mu)

3.Apa pengertian Suggestion?

Suggestions merupakan Saran. Yang akan disampaikan /diberikan kepada seseorang yang mengalami kesusahan dalam memilih sesuatu hal.

4.Contoh kalimat Suggestions?
