4. Dialog bahasa inggris tawar menawar tentang jilbab


(Aminah and Mar'ee are in a hijab store)

Aminah: Wow, I love this color... It's very bright, don't you think?
Mar'ee: It looks good on you, Aminah. How much is it?

(Aminah checked the label)

Aminah: Fifty thousand rupiahs for this? I think no...

(Store Owner walked towards them)

Store Owner: That is a very exclusive model, Lady! Do you care to see the silver-colored one?
Aminah: No... Actually I like this one, Madam. But I think it's too expensive.
Mar'ee: I agree. How about thirty thousand for this?
Store Owner: Dear, oh, dear! I'm very sorry to say that thirty thousand is too cheap!
Aminah: Thirty five?
Store Owner: No, no, Ladies. I'm sorry. But I do have other models with such cheap price...
Mar'ee: Forty?
Store Owner: But...
Aminah: Forty thousand, but we buy three of them. How about that?
Store Owner: Oh, well. Forty thousand rupiahs it is. Which color, may I ask?
Aminah: This yellow one, and perhaps that turquoise.
Mar'ee: And this black one, please.

(Store Owner nodded and packed their stuffs, while Mar'ee rummaged her bag for money)

Mar'ee: Here you go, a hundred and twenty rupiahs.
Store Owner: Thank you, here is your receipt. Please come again!

Jawaban:
Officer: Can I help you, Sir? (Ada yang bisa saya bantu, Pak?)

﻿Passenger 1: I think we have a problem with our flight, Sir (Saya rasa kami punya masalah terkait penerbangan kami,Pak)

Officer: What kind of problem, Sir? Maybe I can help you (Masalah seperti apa, Pak? Mungkin saya bisa membantu)

Passenger 1: we bought a plane ticket for flight next week, but our schedule changed and we ust take a flight two days after today. We don’t want our ticket wasted, but we really must to go at the new scheduled time (kami sudah beli tiket pesawat untuk penerbangan pekan depan , tapi jadwal kami berubah dan kami harus ikut penerbangan dua hari dari sekarang. Officer: I see. I can help you to change the time of your flight, Sir. Do you mind it? (Oh, begitu. Saya bisa membantu untuk mengganti waktu penerbangan Anda, Pak. Apakah Anda keberatan?)

Passenger 2 : So we don’t need to buy a new ticket? (Jadi kami tidak perlu beli tiket baru?)

Officer: It’s no needed, Maam. You can just give me your booking code and your identition card, then I will help to proceed it (Itu tidak perlu, Bu. Anda hanya perlu memberikan kode pemesanan dan kartu identitas, maka saya akan membantu memprosesnya)

Passenger 2 : Thank you so much, Sir! We are glad that our ticket won’t go waste (Terima kasih banyak, Pak! Kami senang tiket kami tidak akan terbuang sia-sia)
