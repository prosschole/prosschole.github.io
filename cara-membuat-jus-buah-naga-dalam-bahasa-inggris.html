HOW TO MAKE DRAGON FRUIT JUICE

Red dragon fruit juice is one of the tasty and nutritious drinks that are suitable for regular consumption. This drink is made from red dragon fruit which is rich in vitamins B and C

In this opportunity we are going to learn how to make sweet Dragon Fruit Juice. Just stay still and pay attention to this method.

Pembahasan

How to make red dragon fruit juice that is delicious, fresh, and does not smell unpleasant

Here I show you the ingredients and how to make red dragon fruit juices that are tasty, simple, and doesn't feel unpleasant. Only three ingredients are needed to make it.

Ingredients (for 2 large glasses):

1 large red dragon fruit (about 500 grams of fruit flesh)3 small yakult bottles (around 200 ml)3 ice cubes

How to make:

1. Scrape dragon fruit meat. Do not scrape it near the inner skin. Put it in a blender.

2. Pour three bottles of yakult into a blender. Add three ice cubes. Then process it with a blender until it becomes a soft juice.

3. Pour the juice into a glass and serve it cold. No need to filter.

Variation of yakult substitutes. Besides yakults, you can also use various other ingredients, for example:

1. sweetened condensed milk

2. Liquid milk with strawberry flavor / vanilla flavor / yellow melon

3. Fresh full cream milk.

4. Watery yogurt  

5. Buttermilk (can be made by mixing cow's milk and lime juice)

6. Vanilla / strawberry ice cream

Recipe variations

From the basic recipe for the juice, you can replace some ingredients or add processing steps to make the food completely different. Here are some ideas that you can try.

1. Red dragon fruit popsicle

Pour red dragon fruit juice into popsicle molds. Give the ice cream stick, then freeze it in the freezer.

2. Dragon fruit jelly

Filter the juice, then mix the water with gelatin or konyaku jelly. Add water and sugar if it is too thick. Then boil until foamy. Pour into the pudding mold and let it stand until it freezes.

3. Red dragon fruit shorbet

Remove yakult from the recipe. Replace with sugar and lime juice. Blender, then freeze in the freezer until it is quite solid but not ice. Serve in dragon fruit skin to make it more tempting.

4. Dragon fruit ice cream

Take 150 grams of red dragon fruit flesh. Replace yakult with 200 ml of heavy cream and 5 sachets of sweetened condensed milk, add 1 teaspoon of steamed SP, then shake with mixer until smooth. Freeze in the freezer, then serve in the skin of the dragon fruit.

5. Smoothie bowl.

Blenders of red dragon fruit and pineapple that have been frozen with the same comparison. Serve in a bowl with a sprinkling of almonds, pepitas, raisins, sliced bananas, berries, dried coconut, walnuts, or granola.

Additional ingredients for juices and smoothies

You can also combine several ingredients at once to make fruit + vegetable juices or smoothie bowls. Here are some possible ingredients that you can try:

1. Banana

2. Sliced anna apple / green Malang apple (choose one with sweet and sour taste)

3. Sliced watermelon, remove its seeds

4. Fresh strawberries

5. Sliced sweet pineapple

6. Canned peach pieces

7. Canned lychee fruit

8. Mango pieces

9. Slices of star fruit, remove its seed

10. Honey

11. Lime / lemon juice

12. Sunkist orange / orange juice

13. Red boiled spinach

14. Boiled sweet carrots that have been cut

7. buatlah deskripsi buah naga dalam bahasa inggris


Dragon Fruits grow on vining cacti with stems that reach up to six meters long, climbing over trees and structures with aerial roots. Depending on variety, Dragon fruits can vary in shape, size and color. They are typically oval to oblong and average 10 centimeters in length. Their pink to magenta exterior has scale-like succulent leaves that are tipped in green, giving them the appearance of an exotic oriental lantern. The inner flesh ranges from white to deep pink or purple and is dotted with tiny black seeds. The spongy pulp has a juicy yet firm texture like the cross between a melon and a pear. The flavor is subtly sweet with notes of star fruit, kiwi, melon and just a hint of salinity and a thirst quenching finish.

HOW TO MAKE A GLASS OF DRAGONFRUIT JUICE

Materials:
1 Dragonfruit
Sugar
Ice cube
Water
White liquid milk

Tools:
Teaspoon
Blender
Two glasses for cold water

Steps:

3) After, add some iced cubes, blend it until the ingredients are mixed.
