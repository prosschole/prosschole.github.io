25. contoh dan gambar suggest and offer?


"Offer" means "do you want this?" or "how about this?". You are offering the other person a choice to do/have something or not to do/have it.

• Do you want __?
• Would you like a ___?
• Want any of ___?

"A offers tea to B= A says to B "do you want tea?".

"A offers to drive B home = A says to B "Do you want me to drive you home?".

"Suggest" means "I think you should do this". You are offering the other person advice.

• I think it's better for you to ___
• You better ___
• You should do___

"A suggests B drink tea" = A says to B "I think you should drink tea". A is not saying "I will give you tea", but "You should drink tea". B can take this advice or not.

"A suggests B consult a lawyer" - A says to B "you should consult a lawyer". A is not offering to be a lawyer, only telling B that B should consult a lawyer. B can take this advice or leave it.Contoh:

I suggest you to buy that book.
I think you should get a haircut.
Would you like a banana?
Do you mind if I gave you my sweatshirt?

Suggest itu seperti opini yang membujuk kita melakukan, atau mendapat sesuatu, walaupun kita tidak mau.
Offer itu lebih ke pertanyaan apa kamu ingin ini atau tidak.
