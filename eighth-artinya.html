PEMBAHASAN

Calendar system atau sistem penanggalan merupakan jadwal waktu dalam kehidupan yang meliputi Day (hari), Month (bulan), dan Year (tahun).

Dalam satu pekan, terdapat tujuh hari:

Sunday = Minggu / Ahad.Monday = Senin.Tuesday = Selasa.Wednesday = Rabu.Thursday = Kamis.Friday = Jum'at.Saturday = Sabtu.

Dalam satu tahun, terdapat 12 bulan:

January = Januari (the one Month).February = Februari (the two Month).March = Maret (the three Month).April = April (the for Month).May = Mei (the five Month).June = Juni (the six Month).July = Juli (the seven Month).August = Agustus (the eight Month).September = September (the nine Month).October = Oktober (the ten Month).November = November (the eleven Month).December = Desember (the twelve Month).
