7. Apa bahasa inggrisnya, pohon kelapa lebih tinggi dari pohon pisang


Coconut trees are taller than banana trees

Koreksi Jika Ada Kesalahan Penulisan

Semoga Membantu

Original : Pohon kelapa leboh tinggi dari pohon pisang.

English version : Coconut trees are higher than banana trees

Uraian

Jenis : Comparative Degree

Formula :

1) S + to be (am/is/are) + adj/adv + (-er) + than + O >> digunakan untuk one syllable adj/adv (kata sifat / kata keterangan yang dibaca satu suku kata : high, short, dll)

2) S + to be (am/is/are) + more + adj/adv + than + O >> digunakan untuk two or more syllables (kata sifat / kata keterangan yang dibaca dua atau lebih suku kata : expensive, beautiful, dll)

How to Make Es Kelapa Muda

Ingredients:

- 1,000 grams young coconut, dredged widely

-1,000 ml of coconut water

- 200 grams of sugar

- 3 grains of cloves

- 4 cm of cinnamon

- 1,000 grams of black grass jelly

- 700 grams of ice cubes

Steps :

1. First, boil the coconut milk, sugar, cloves, and cinnamon over low heat until boiling and fragrant. Chill.
2. Second, Enter the young coconut and black grass jelly. Stir well.
3. Finally, Serve with ice cubes. And let's drink it.

14. cara buat es kelapa muda murni dengan bahasa inggris


How To Make "Es Kelapa Muda Murni"

Ingredients :
– 300 grams of shredded coconut pulp
– 1/4 teaspoon of vanila powder
– 2000 grams of salt
– 4 pieces of pandan leaves
– 400 gram white sugar
– 1 teaspon of salt
– 4000 grams of ice shavings

Step by step :
1 . Boil the coconut milk, salt, white sugar, pandan leaves and vanilla powder untul boiled correctly, while you boil, it stir manually and slowly.

2 . And then, put all of shredded coconut pulp to the boiled coconut milk (according to step one) , mix it correctly.

3 . Prepare ice shavings with salt, put on a bowl.

4 . Then put mixed coconut milk with shredded coconut pulp into the bowl.

5 . Serve for 10 people.
