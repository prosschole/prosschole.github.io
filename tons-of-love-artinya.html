25. Bantu kak buru buru"A car move 1 tons, has a kinetic energy 800 KJ. Calculate the velocity of the car​"


The Velocity of the car when moving at around 40 m/s.

_____________.

Kinetic Energy

Data:

m = 1 TonsKinetic Energy (Ke) = 800 kJ

• Convers Tons to kg

m = 1 tons

m = 1 × 1.000

m = 1.000 kg

• Convers kJ to J

Ke = 800 kJ

Ke = 800 × 1.000

Ke = 800.000 J

• Find The Velocity

Ke = ½m.v²

v² = Ke/(½m)

v² = 800.000/(½ × 1.000)

v² = 800.000/500

v² = 1.600

v = √1.600

v = √40²

v = 40 m/s

So, The Car move with Velocity around at 40 m/s

___________.

Class: 8

Subject: Physics

Code: 8.6.2

Keyword: Kinetic energy, Velocity.

Kinetik Energy

Data:

mass = 1 ton = 1.000 kg Ek = kinetic energy = 800 kJ = 800.000 J

Asked:

v = velocity ?

Answer

Ek = ½ × m × v²

800.000 = ½ × 1.000 × v²

800.000 = 500 × v²

v² = 800.000/500

v² = 1.600

v = 40 m/s ✔
