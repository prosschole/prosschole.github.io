10. I told the librarian ... (pembahasannya kenapa jawabannya A)


Indirect Sentence

Kalimat Tidak langsung

I told the librarian ....

Opsi A.

To return the book

Menggunakan { told, said }pada saat berbicara artinya kalimat tersebut menjelaskan bahwa saya berkata kepada pustakawan tersebut untuk mengembalikan buku itu.

Ciri kalimat disini dijelaskan kepada pihak ke tiga yaitu si pembaca (orang ke 3) bahwa sipenulis ( orang ke1) sedang berkata kepada pustakawan ( orang ke dua ) untuk mengembalikan buku itu.

Demikian jelaslah bahwa text tersebut adalah kalimat tidak langsung.

21. Sebutkan lima tugas an engineer, a librarian , a meteorologist, a market researcher, an animator a designer, a manager ! (Dalam bahasa inggris)


An engineer is a practitioner of engineering, concerned with applying scientific knowledge, mathematics, and ingenuity to develop solutions for technical, societal and commercial problems.

A librarian is a person who works professionally in a library, providing access to information and sometimes social or technical programming to users.

A meteorologist is a scientist who studies weather; and may, like a weather presenter (who often are not scientists) engage in weather forecasting, or may solely conduct research.

A marketing researcher, or marketing research analyst, is responsible for analyzing information from marketing reports and making recommendations based on their analysis.

An animator is an artist who creates multiple images, known as frames, which give an illusion of movement called animation when displayed in rapid sequence.

A designer is a person who makes designs for objects.

A manager is a person responsible for controlling or administering all or part of a company or similar organization.

Building Planning Civil Engineer.

4. Making work drawings, work plans, formulating implementation requirements and identifying bill of quantity


5.. Forecast the initial costs and calculate the Budget Plan


Librarian duties


1. Ensuring that library services meet the needs of particular groups of users (eg children, adult learners and schools)

2.Making improvements to accessibility of library resources

3.Maintaining statistical and financial records

4. Using library systems and specialist computer applications.

5. Selecting, cataloguing and classifying library resources


A market researche duties


1. Investigating market activity

2. Analysing published data and statistics

3. Evaluating past performance of a product or service's sales

4. Assessing future trends

5. Commissioning surveys


Animator duties


1. Working creatively to produce

original and aesthetically pleasing designs and solutions.


2. using specialist computer generation software such as Maya,

Flash and After Effects


3.. presenting designs to customers for evaluation.


4. promoting and running the business.


5. liaising and working effectively with other design and production staff..


A designer duties


1. liaising with clients to determine their requirements and budget


2. managing client proposals from typesetting through to design, print and production


3. working with clients, briefing and advising them with regard to design style, format, print production and timescales


4. developing concepts, graphics and layouts for product illustrations, company logos, and websites


5. determining size and arrangement of illustrative material and copy, and font style and size.


Manager Job Duties:

1. Maintains staff by recruiting, selecting, orienting, and training employees; maintaining a safe, secure, and legal work environment; developing personal growth opportunities.


2. Accomplishes staff results by communicating job expectations; planning, monitoring, and appraising job results; coaching, counseling, and disciplining employees; developing, coordinating, and enforcing systems, policies, procedures, and productivity standards.


3. Establishes strategic goals by gathering pertinent business, financial, service, and operations information; identifying and evaluating trends and options; choosing a course of action; defining objectives; evaluating outcomes.


4. Accomplishes financial objectives by forecasting requirements; preparing an annual budget; scheduling expenditures; analyzing variances; initiating corrective actions.


5. Maintains quality service by enforcing quality and customer service standards; analyzing and resolving quality and customer service problems; identifying trends; recommending system improvements.
