Jawaban:

B: Yes, me too. Especially since the restrictions imposed by the government.

A: In the past, my mother and father used to take me to the mall to buy ice cream or just go for a walk on the weekends. What do you usually buy or see at the mall?

B: When I go to the mall, my family usually goes to the supermarket in the mall, then goes around the mall to look at some items, such as clothes, shoes, or food.
