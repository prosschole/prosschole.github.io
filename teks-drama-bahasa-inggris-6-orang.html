Pembahasan

In the afternoon when the break arrived, the school bell rang. Budi, Cindy, Dion, Ellie, and Farhan ordered food in the canteen while chatting together.

Budi: Dika hasn't been in school for three days, what's going on? Is he sick?

Cindy: Yesterday evening I saw Dika on the porch of his house sitting pensively, I was going to buy something at a shop near his house. I had time to ask what caused him to reflect and not go to school. He only replied that it was okay and reasoned that lately he was always late.

Dion: Hmm, is it possible that Dika is constantly oversleeping ?, Since he is a diligent child, he always gets up early even before the dawn call to prayer reverberates. Maybe there's a problem Dika is hiding.

Ellie: Oh yeah, I remember, three days ago after school, my mother told me that she saw Dika at the market helping her mother to sell. Isn't that day where the first day Dika didn't go to school?

Farhan: we should visit him after school to find out what happened to Dika.

Budi, Cindy, Dion, Ellie, and Farhan agreed with the idea. And in the afternoon they gathered then went together to Dika's house. There they found Dika tidying up her mother's merchandise.

Dika: What's up, friends? How come you suddenly come here? Sit down first.

Budi: We just want to make sure the reason you did not go to school for three days in a row, because we all know that you are our most diligent friend and we suspect that you are having a problem.

Cindy: You better tell us about your real problem, Dik.

Dika: Sorry for that friends, I don't want to tell because I'm embarrassed and don't want to bother you. I worked to help my mother to reduce school fees because I was threatened that I could not pay tuition fees and I was sorry to see my mother working alone.

Dion: Well, tomorrow afternoon we will help you go around for selling some of your wares. What do you think friends?

Ellie: I agree!

Farhan: My mother often buys your wares because the fish and vegetables are always fresh. The snacks are also delicious. I'll ask Mom later to offer your mother's snacks to her social gathering friends.

Dika: Thank you, my best friend, you are all my true friends

-------------------

Di siang hari saat jam istirahat tiba, lonceng sekolah pun berbunyi. Budi, Cindy, Dion, Ellie, dan Farhan memesan makanan di kantin sembari berbincang - bincang bersama.

Budi : Dika sudah tidak masuk sekolah selama tiga hari, kira - kira ada apa ya? Apa dia sakit?

Cindy : Kemarin sore aku lihat Dika di teras rumahnya sedang duduk termenung, saat itu aku mau beli sesuatu di warung dekat rumahnya. Saya sempat bertanya apa yang menyebabkan dia merenung dan tidak masuk sekolah. Dia hanya menjawab tidak apa - apa dan beralasan jika akhir - akhir ini dia selalu kesiangan.

Dion : Hmm, apakah mungkin Dika terus - menerus kesiangan?, Dika kan anak yang rajin, selalu bangun pagi bahkan sebelum azan subuh berkumandang. Jangan-jangan ada masalah yang sedang disembunyikan Dika.

Ellie : Oh iya aku ingat, tiga hari yang lalu sepulang sekolah, ibuku bercerita kalau dia melihat Dika di pasar sedang membantu ibunya berjualan. Bukankah hari itu dimana hari pertama Dika tidak masuk sekolah?

Farhan : Sebaiknya, sepulang sekolah nanti kita mengunjunginya untuk mengetahui apa yang terjadi pada Dika.

Budi, Cindy, Dion, Ellie, dan Farhan setuju dengan usulan itu. Dan pada sore harinya mereka berkumpul kemudian berangkat bersama menuju rumah Dika. Di sana mereka menemukan Dika sedang merapikan barang dagangan ibunya.

Dika : Ada apa teman - teman? Kok mendadak sekali kalian kemari? Duduk dulu yuk.

Budi : Kami cuma mau memastikan alasan kamu tidak masuk sekolah selama tiga hari berturut - turut, karena kami semua tahu bahwa kamu sahabat kami yang paling rajin dan kami curiga kalau kamu sedang ada masalah.

Cindy : Lebih baik kamu cerita kepada kami tentang masalahmu sebenarnya, Dik.

Dika : Maaf sebelumnya teman - teman, aku ga ingin cerita karena malu dan ga ingin menyusahkan kalian. Aku kerja bantu Ibu untuk meringankan biaya sekolah karena saya terancam tidak bisa membayar uang SPP dan saya kasihan melihat Ibu saya bekerja sendiri.

Dion : Kalau gitu besok sore kami mau bantu keliling, jualin setenah daganganmu. Gimana teman - teman?

Ellie : Saya setuju!

Farhan : Ibuku sering membeli daganganmu karena ikan dan sayurnya selalu segar. Jajanan pasarnya juga nikmat. Nanti saya tawarkan ke Ibu untuk menawarkan jajanan Ibumu ke teman - teman arisannya.

Dika : Makasih ya sahabatku, kalian emang sahabat sejatiku.
