Cara Menyapa dan Berpamitan dalam bahasa inggris

Holly : Hi Sam, nice to see you again.

Sam : Hi Holly. It’s nice to meet you too.

Holly : by the way, where are you going?

Sam : I am going to library. I need some books to finish my task.

Holly : Oh great, I am going to library too, but I am still waiting for Sarah.

Sam : well, see you there soon.

Holly : See you there.

Kalimat sapaan dan pamitan yang sering digunakan

1. Hi/Hello

2. Good morning/ Good afternoon/ Good evening (Selamat Pagi/ Selamat Sore/ Selamat Malam)

3. How are you? (Bagaimana kabarmu?)

4. Nice to meet you (Senang bertemu denganmu.)

5. It’s good to see you again. (Senang bertemu kamu lagi.)

6. This is my colleague, Andrea. (Kenalkan ini kolega saya, Andrea.)

7. Where do you work? (Di mana kamu bekerja?)

8. What do you do? (Apa pekerjaanmu?)

Untuk mengakhiri percakapan Anda bisa mengucapkan

1. Bye

2. Goodbye

3. Take care (hati-hati)

4. Until next time (sampai bertemu lagi)

5. See you (sampai nanti)

6. See you soon (sampai bertemu lagi)

7. keep in touch(tetap berhubungan ya)

8. Talk to you later (nanti ngobrol lagi ya)

Penjelasan:

See you at another time
