3. Pengertian autobiografi dalam bahasa sunda


Autobiografi nyaeta tulisan anu ngajelaskeun atawa nyaritakeun kahirupan hiji tokoh atawa jelema. Autobiografi ditulis sangkan bisa dijadikeun referensi, catatan, jeung palajaran keur anu macana atawa pihak anu merlukeun eta riwayat kahirupan tokoh anu ditulis dina eta autobiografi.

Autobiografi ditulis atawa dikarang ku subjekna atawa babarengan jeung penulis sejen jeung biasana disebutkeun saperti "sakumaha dicaritakeun" atawa "jeung". Lamun eta karangan ditulisna lain ku tokoh atawa jelema nu dicaritakeun dina eta karangan, ngaranna lain autobiografi tapi ngaranna biografi.

Autobiografi lain ngan saukur ngeunaan daftar tanggal lahir atawa riwayat pagawean hiji tokoh, tapi autobiografi oge nyaritakeun ngeunaan parasaan nu terlibat dina kajadian-kajadian nu kaalaman ku tokoh nu dimaksud. Dina autobiografi oge dijelaskeun sacara lengkap kahirupan tokoh nu dimaksud, mimiti ti leutik tepi ka kolot.

Dina autobiografi dijelaskeun oge sagala karya, tanda jasa, jeung sagala hal anu dilakukeun atawa dihasilkeun ku tokoh nu dimasksud. Biografi biasana dikarang atawa ditulis dumasar kana sababaraha sudut pandang jeung rupa-rupa dokumen, sedengkeun  otobiografi bisa wae sakabehna dumasar kana ingetan jeung pikiran nu nulis atawa anu ngarangna.

Autobiografi sacara alami relatif leuwih subjektif. Dina autobiografi, kadang-kadang penulisna henteu mampu atawa teu aya niat pikeun sacara tepat nuliskeun memorina nu ngakibatkeun hasil tulisannana jadi menyimpang atawa henteu bener. Bahkan aya sababaraha pendapat anu nganyatakeun yen autobiografi bisa mere kasempatan ka penulisna pikeun ngarubah sejarah.

4. naon harti biografi jeung autobiografi dina basa sunda


Biografi nyaeta tulisan anu eusina nyaritakeun atawa ngajelaskeun kahirupan hiji jalma atawa tokoh sacara lengkep. Biografi ditulisna teh ku jalma sejen, atawa biografi henteu ditulis ku jalma atawa tokoh nu aya dina hiji biografi. Sedengkeun autobiografi nyaeta biografi anu ditulisna ku jalma atawa tokoh anu dicaritakeun dina eta biografi. Biografi atawa autobiografi ditulis boh dina basa Sunda boh dina basa lianna.  

Pembahasan

Eusi biografi atawa autobiografi henteu ngan saukur manggrupa data ngeunaan tanggal lahir, tanggal maot, pendidikan atawa pagawean hiji jalma atawa tokoh wungkul, tapi biografi atawa autobiografi oge eusina nyaritakeun unggal kajadian penting anu dialaman ku eta jalma atawa tokoh, sarta rarasaan anu kaalaman ku eta tokoh dina unggal kajadian penting nu aya.

Biografi jeung autobiografi, miboga kagunaan anu sarua. Manfaat biografi atawa autobiografi antara lain nyaeta mangrupakeun dokumen, catetan penting atawa referensi pikeun tokoh anu aya dina eta biografi atawa autobiografi. Sedengkeun pikeun anu maca atawa jalma sejen, biografi atawa autobiografi bisa dijadikeun referensi umpama aya kaperluan pikeun ngawanohan eta jalma atawa tokoh, saperti pikeun dijadikeun conto atawa teladan, jeung sajabana.

Hiji biografi atawa autobiografi miboga struktur nyaeta orientasi, kajadian atawa masalah, sarta reorientasi. Sedengkeun unsur unsur anu biasana aya dina hiji biografi atawa autobiografi nyaeta Ngaran atawa lalandiannana (tokoh), Kalahiran jeung asal-usul kulawargana, Bentuk fisik jeung pasipatanana, Riwayat pendidikan jeung pagaweanana, Pikiran atawa gagasanana, Karya atawa hasil gawena, sarta Prestasi jeung pangajen (penghargaan) anu kungsi katarima.

11. autobiografi soekarno dalam bahasa inggris


soekarno is a heroes of the Indonesian revolution .Sukarno was born on June 6, 1901, in Surabaya, and was given the name Kusno Sosrodihardjo. His parents renamed him Sukarno later, after he survived a serious illness. Sukarno’s father was Raden Soekemi Sosrodihardjo, a Muslim aristocrat and school teacher from Java. His mother, Ida Ayu Nyoman Rai, was a Hindu of the Brahmin caste from Bali.

Young Sukarno went to a local elementary school until 1912. He then attended a Dutch middle school in Mojokerto, followed in 1916 by a Dutch high school in Surabaya. The young man was gifted with a photographic memory and a talent for languages, including Javanese, Balinese, Sundanese, Dutch, English, French, Arabic, Bahasa Indonesia, German, and Japanese.

Sukarno began to think about independence for the country while he was in high school. During college, he read deeply on different political philosophies, including communism, capitalist democracy, and Islamism, developing his own syncretic ideology of Indonesian socialist self-sufficiency. He also established the Algameene Studieclub for like-minded Indonesian students.

In 1927, Sukarno and the other members of the Algameene Studieclub reorganized themselves as the Partai Nasional Indonesia (PNI), an anti-imperialist, anti-capitalist independence party. Sukarno became the first leader of the PNI.

in late December of 1929, The Dutch colonial secret policearrested Sukarno and the other members. He was sentenced to four years in prison and went to the Sukamiskin Prison in Bandung to begin serving his sentence. However, Sukarno was released from prison after just one year.

In February 1942, the Imperial Japanese Army invaded the Dutch East Indies, included, area of Indonesia. After a long struggle, on August 17, 1945, Ir Sukarno and Drs. Mohammad Hatta proclaimed Indonesia’s independence. In the trial PPKI, August 18, 1945 Ir.Soekarno unanimously elected as the first President of the Republic of Indonesia.

The first President of the Republic of Indonesia, Soekarno, commonly called Bung Karno, was born in Blitar, East Java, June 6, 1901 and died in Jakarta, June 21, 1970. His father was Raden Soekemi Sosrodihardjo and his mother Ida Ayu Nyoman Rai. During his lifetime, he had three wives and was blessed with eight children. From Fatmawati's wife has children Guntur, Megawati, Rachmawati, Sukmawati and Guruh. From Hartini's wife has Taufan and Bayu, while from Ratna Sari Dewi's wife, a Japanese derived woman by the name of Naoko Nemoto has Kartika's child. Soekarno's childhood lived only a few years with his parents in Blitar. From elementary school to graduate, he lives in Surabaya, homestay at Haji Oemar Said Tokroaminoto's house, a veteran politician of the founder of Islamic Sharia. Then continue your studies at HBS (Hoogere Burger School). While studying at HBS, Sukarno has galvanized his nationalism spirit. After graduating HBS in 1920, moved to Bandung and continued to THS (Technische Hoogeschool or High School of Engineering which is now ITB). He won the title of "Ir" on May 25, 1926. Then, he formulated the teachings of Marhaenism and established the PNI (Partai Nasional Indonesia) on 4 July 1927, with the goal of Indonesia Merdeka. As a result, the Netherlands, put it into Sukamiskin prison, Bandung on December 29, 1929. Eight months later just trial. In his defense entitled Indonesia Menggugat, he showed the Dutch apostasy, the nation who claimed more advanced it. His defense made the Dutch even more angry. So in July 1930, the PNI was dissolved. After his release in 1931, Soekarno joined Partindo and also led it. As a result, he was again arrested by the Dutch and exiled to Ende, Flores, in 1933. Four years later moved to Bengkulu.
