11. I want you all members of class to make at least 5 sentences of Present Perfect Tense. Please be beyond expectation person. as you want to be paid more than your expectation, you should do more than anyone expectation. Be an Extra Ordinary Person


Present perfect tense

Kita menggunakan Present Perfect Tense untuk menunjukkan suatu kejadian yang telah terjadi dalam waktu yang tidak spesifik. Jadi, keterangan waktu seperti yesterday, one year ago, last week, dan lainnya, tidak dibutuhkan dalam tenses ini.




1. Kalimat Positif : S + have / has + Past participle

Contoh : -She has worked in the hospital for six years                 

- I have fixed my computer

2. Kalimat Negatif : S + have / has + not + Past participle

Contoh : -She hasn’t worked in the hospital.                  

-I have not fixed my computer.

3. Kalimat Tanya :  Have / has  + S + Past participle

Contoh :  -Has she worked in the hospital for six years?                  

-Have I fixed my computer?
