7. II. Change these following sentences into a positive sentence (+), a negative sent (-), or a yes/no question (?). (=Ubah kalimat-kalimat berikut ke dalam kalimat p (+), atau kalimat negatif (-) atau kalimat tanya yes/no (?)) Contoh soal : 1. (+) (-) (?) Did you go there?​


(+) You went there

(-) You didn't go there

(?) Did you go there? (Yes, You did/No, you didn't)

Penjelasan:

Simple Past Tense adalah sebuah tense yang menjelaskan kejadian yang dilakukan pada masa lampau. Formulanya adalah sebagai berikut :

(+) = S + V2 (-) = S + didn't + V1(?) = Did + S + V1 + ? (Y/N)

Contoh kalimat di atas adalah "Did you go there?", kalimat ini kita ubah ke bentuk Simple Past Tense karena ada to be "Did".

Rangkuman

We use the present continuous (am/is/are + -ing) to talk about temporary things which

have begun but haven't finished that often happening now, at this moment.

We use the present continuous for future

arrangements with other people.

For negatives you add not after the verb to be.

For questions you just change the subject and the verb to be.

If a verb ends in e, you take off the e and add –ing.

If the stress is on the last syllable, you do double the final consonant.

There is a group of verbs which are called state verbs which we use

for states (not actions) and we don't usually use these in the continuous form.
