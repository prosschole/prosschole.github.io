10. membuat argumen tentang dilarang memakai sandal disekolah dalam bahasa inggris


Membuat argumen tentang dilarang memakai sandal di sekolah memerlukan kemampuan untuk menulis paragraf persuasif (persuasive paragraph). Persuasive paragraph adalah tulisan yang berisi argumen terhadap sebuah isu. Contoh argumen tentang dilarang memakai sandal di sekolah dalam Bahasa Inggris adalah:

Students are not allowed to wear sandals at school. To begin with, sandals are not considered formal. Moreover, wearing sandals at school can also hurt students. For example, when students are playing sports during break time, they can hurt their feet if they wear sandals to play badminton, soccer, and so on. In the same way, students may take off their sandals during lessons. It is not good because, can you imagine if students walk barefeet during lessons when their teachers ask them to come up to in front the class? Obviously, it would be very hillarious and students would not be able to concentrate.


Pembahasan

Dalam menyampaikan argumen untuk mendukung maupun menentang sebuah pernyataan, kita perlu menyusun argumen dengan baik. Contoh argumen yang kakak berikan di atas adalah contoh argumen yang mendukung pernyataan. Yang perlu kamu perhatikan adalah struktur penyusunan argumen.

To begin with: digunakan untuk membuka argumen atau menyampaikan argumen pertama.Moreover dan in the same way: digunakan untuk menambahkam argumen.For example: digunakan untuk memberikan contoh yang menguatkan argumen.Beberapa kata lain yang dapat kamu gunakan untuk menguatkan argumen adalah as you know, obviously, of course, without a doubt.Detil Jawaban

17. contoh pidato bahasa inggris tentang larangan membawa hp ke sekolah


The Good and Bad Impact of Bringing Handphone to School

Mobile phones are very popular means nowadays. This modern technology has changed from its main function as communicative means to media of information and entertainment.

How is the impact to students? There must be good and bad impacts of bringing mobile phone especially for students.

Some people agree that mobile phone has many benefits. Beside as a mean of communication, mobile phones can be used when people get bored. They can watch video or listen to music, and also be active in social media.

The good impact for students in school is as a mean of searching information for lesson reference. If teachers ask student to find materials in the internet, mobile phones may help to find the materials.

Some other people think that mobile phone has bad impact especially for students.

Students are easily losing their concentration everytime the phone rings. For example, BBM notification, SMS notification, and also a phone call during learning process make studens lost of focus.

Students are not sensitive to social life because they often check their mobile phone. Students prefer chat on social media to chat to his friends directly.

By those two points of view, the good effect of bringing mobile phone to school may be the reasons to bring it. Meanwhile the bad effect should be reduced by rules of using mobile phone in school.

18. sebutka 10 peraturan dan larangan di sekolah menggunkan bahasa inggris


Peraturan : 1. Siswa harus tepat pada waktunya. (Students must come on time).
2. Siswa harus berpakaian seragam sesuai ketentuan sekolah. (Students must wear uniform according to the school's rules).
3. Jagalah ketenangan. (Keep silent in the class).
4. Siswa harus sopan dan saling menghargai. (Students must be polite and respect each other).
5. Jangan mengejek atau menghina teman. (Don't mock your friends).
6. Jagalah kebersihan kelas. (Keep the class clean).
7. Jagalah barang-barang di kelas dengan baik. (Take care of all things in the class carefully).
8. Dilarang membawa dan memakai handphone di dalam kelas. (Don't bring or use cellphone in the class).
9. Dilarang membawa benda tajam kecuali dibutuhkan. (Don't bring any sharp object unless it is needed).
10. Buanglah sampah pada tempat sampah. (Throw trash in trash can).
