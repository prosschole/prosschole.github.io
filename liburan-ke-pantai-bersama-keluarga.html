8. contoh cerita liburan ke pantai bersama keluarga yang sering di lakukan. bantu yaaaa


kelas : X
pelajaran : Bahasa Inggris
kategori : Recount text
kata kunci : holiday, simple past tense

pembahasan:
Untuk menulis karangan mengenai liburan yang telah dialami dalam bentuk recount text. Sehingga menggunakan simple past tense (v2) dengan struktur : orientation - events - reorientation

Sepanjang Beach

orientation
During the holiday break, I went to Sepanjang beach, unspoiled beach south of Gunungkidul regency. I went there with my family just after New Year. The drive took about an hour, and it was very interesting because most of it was countryside.

Event 1
When we arrived, the first thing we all did were to get changed into our clothes and go for a swim down at the beach. The sun was very bright and the water freezing cold, but it was very interesting. We swam for a little while and then we returned to the car to get some food.

Event 2
My mother and I walked down to the souvenir stalls, where we had shopped for about two hours. After that, we played beach volleyball and we swam until the sun sets in the west.

reorientation
I will always remember this holiday because it was the first time in years that the whole family was able to holiday together. The weather was fantastic the whole time. We took plenty of pictures. They are treasured memories of a great holiday experience.

18. Bikinin narasi tentang liburan sekolah dipantai bareng keluarga menggunakan verb2


Last holiday, my family and I went to Parangtritis beach in Yogyakarta. We had arrived in Yogyakarta several days before. We had gone around the city and at the last day we finally came to the beach and we rent a cottage near it. We arrived at 10 o’clock in the morning. It was a perfect time to enjoy the beach. The scenery was so wonderful and I felt that the sun kissed me though its bright light. I felt blessed because I could enjoy one of God’s masterpieces. My parents sat together under their umbrella. My younger brothers played sand. My older sister took her self picture and I enjoyed the wind blowing.

My mother had prepared a bucket of foods and some drinks for us. She had arranged to have a picnic time at the beach. She brought many bread toast with beef and cheese on it, some fruit salads, and orange juice. Those were so fresh and perfect companion for the perfect moment we had. After having picnic, my father challenged us to fly with paragliding. That was an amazing offer for me. But, my sister seemed afraid of it. Then, we accepted the challenge. I was so excited, and so were my brothers. My mother and my sister just watched us.

After that, my mother and sister took their turn, but it was not paragliding. They were interested to ride a horse. While they rode the horses and my father kept his eyes on them, my brothers and I were walking around and looking for some beautiful snails. I got some and my brothers got starfishes. They looked cute. Therefore, we let them go then and didn’t take it. It was almost 4 o’clock in the afternoon and we went to the cottage. I felt so happy to have great experience at Parangtritis beach. I wished someday I could come back.

Jonjongan pun mesak, unyin dikakhikon di hadapan khang mejong sikam. Sikam mulai nganik palas ngeliak lawok si setemonni mawat pikha helau, ulih wakhna way lawokni cokelat.
