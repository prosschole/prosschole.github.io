17. jawaban bahasa inggris buku bright kelas 8 hal. 31 dan 32act. 7


Mother: What are you doing?

Bob: I am cooking fried rice.

Mother: Do you  knoe hoe to do it?

Bob: Sure, I have the recipe with me.

Mother: Do you need any help?

Bob: Thanks, but I can do it myself.

Mother: Be careful with the stove.

Bob: Don't worry, I can manage

A few minutes later...

Bob: Ouch.

Mother: What's the matter?

Bob: I burnt my fingers. I guess I need your help after all.

Mother: OK. No Problem. We can do it together. You have to hold the frying pan like this..

Pembahasan

Offering help adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk menawarkan bantuan kepada orang lain. Ungkapan offering help antara lain:

Can I help you?Do you need a help?Do you need a hand?Would you mind if I help you?Need a help? Need a hand?A help?

Untuk meresponnya kita dapat menggunakan Accepting Help (menerima bantuan) dan Rejecting help (menolak bantuan).

Ungkapan accepting help antara lain:

Thanks so muchI'd love tooThank you very muchThanks for your helpSure

Ungkapan Rejecting Help antrara lain:

Thabnks, but I can do it my self.I can manage it myselfDon't worry. I can handle it.No, thanks.Pelajari Lebih LanjutContoh offering dan rejecting help - https://brainly.co.id/tugas/3258939Detil Jawaban

18. Buku Brightkelas 8Unit 1 , Bright 2 , activity 5.Maaf,tolong carikan maksud kata yang dicetak tebal.Terima Kasih


ARE YOU WITH ME

- On the dialogue, it is a comprehension where Ethan was making sure whether Lucas is understand or paying attention on his statement/concept . This sentence is somehow more equivalent than "Do you understand". But between "are you with me" and "do you undertsand" are quite different.

- Didalam percakapan, kalimat tersebut merupakan sebuah koprehensi dimana ethan mencoba untuk meyakinkan dirinya apakah Lucas memahami atau memperhatikan penjelasan darinya. Kalimat ini terkadang memiliki artian yang sama dengan kalimat "do you understand" namu kedua nya tetap frasa yang berbeda



I SEE

- Lucas was convenient Ethan that he's noticing or understanding the explanation on which given by him

- Lucas meyakinkan etha, bahwa ia menangkap/memahami penjelasan yang diberikan olehnya



ARE YOU WITH ME SO FAR?

- Ethan was making sure of Lucas if he's following all his concepts he explains to him before he went further

- Ethan meyakinkan Lucas jika ia mengikuti semua konsep/penjelasan yang ia berikan sebelum menerangkan lebih lanjut


I'M NOT SURE I GET YOUR POINT

- On this equivalent, Lucas didn't seem to understand completely through whole Ethan's explanations

- Dalam hal ini, Lucas tidak memahami betul akan semua penjelasan ethan



I KNOW WHAT YOU MEAN

- Lucas is 100% understood on Ethan's explanation

- Lucas memahami keseluruhan penjelasan ethan

21. jawaban dari buku bright


Subject : English
Category : Tenses (Present Simple Interrogative)

Present Simple digunakan untuk menyatakan kegiatan yg dilakukan berulang-ulang atau merupakan kebiasaan di masa sekarang.

Pola :
(+) S + infinitive
jika subjek orang ketiga tunggal (kata ganti HE/SHE/IT) infinitive diberi akhiran -s/es

(?) Do/Does + S + infinitive
jika subjek orang ketiga tunggal (kata ganti HE/SHE/IT) gunakan kata bantu DOES.
