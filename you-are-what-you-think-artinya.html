5. Apa arti 1) ''Everybody, may I have your attention, please?''2) ''Everybody, are you ready to learn English now?''3) ''What about you, Edo? what do you think if weuse english in our english class?''4) ''What do you think if we use English in our English class. We willuse english in our class. Do you understand me? Siti, What did I say?


Translation (English to Bahasa Indonesia)Pembahasan

1. ''Everybody, may I have your attention, please?''

Semuanya, bisakah saya minta perhatiannya?

2. ''Everybody, are you ready to learn English now?''

Semuanya, apakah kalian siap untuk belajar bahasa Inggris sekarang?

3. “What about you, Edo? What do you think if we use english in our english class?''

Bagaimana denganmu, Edo? Apa pendapatmu jika kita menggunakan bahasa Inggris di kelas kita?

4. ''What do you think if we use English in our English class. We will use english in our class. Do you understand me? Siti, What did I say?

Apa pendapatmu jika kita menggunakan bahasa Inggris di kelas kita. Kita akan menggunakan bahasa Inggris. Apa kamu mengerti? Siti, apa yang saya katakan?
