Ungkapan Dan Dialog Memberi Ucapan Selamat/ congratulation Dalam Bahasa Inggris

memberi ucapan selamat - congratulation

Congratulation adalah salah satu ungkapan yang sering diucapkan kepada keluarga, sahabat, rekan kerja atau orang terdekat kita pada saat moment membahagiakan seperti pernikahan, kelulusan, kenaikan pangkat/ jabatan dan sebagainya.

Terkadang pelajar bahasa inggris bingung bagaimana mengucapkannya. Ucapan selamat dapat dilakukan melalui lisan dan postcard. Di bawah ini adalah beberapa ungkapan yang bisa ada gunakan untuk memberi ucapan selamat.

8. Percakapan bahasa inggris congratulation


Congratulating

JAWABAN:  

After a long struggle and hard work, Ammar is finally elected as the leader of students’ association (OSIS) at his school. Many of his friends who support him congratulate him.

Firdaus : That’s wonderful Ammar… (shaking Ammar’s hand).

Ammar : All praise is due to Allah. Thanks.

Ameera : Congratulation! You deserved it guy. Nice work, Ammar.  

Ammar : Thanks a lot. I still need your support.

Ameera   : Insha Allah… we will.

Yusuf : Well done…We must help you (while shaking Ammar’s hand).

Ammar : Thank you.

Farhan  :That’s great. Your parents must be very proud of your achievement (then he shakes Ammar’s hand).

Ammar :Thanks. Nothing will happen without Allah’s help. I’m glad you think so. But I still have to learn a lot.

PEMBAHASAN:

Terjemahannya:

Setelah melalui perjuangan dan kerja keras yang panjang, akhirnya Ammar terpilih menjadi ketua perkumpulan siswa (OSIS) di sekolahnya. Banyak dari teman-temannya yang mendukungnya memberikan selamat kepadanya.

Firdaus: Ammar yang luar biasa… (menjabat tangan Ammar).

Ammar: Segala puji bagi Allah. Terima kasih.

Ameera: Selamat! Kamu pantas mendapatkannya. Kerja bagus, Ammar.

Ammar: Terima kasih banyak. Saya masih membutuhkan dukungan kalian.

Ameera: Insha Allah… kami akan.    

Yusuf: Bagus sekali… Kami pasti membantu mu (sambil menjabat tangan Ammar).

Ammar: Terima kasih.

Farhan: Bagus sekali. Orang tuamu pasti sangat bangga dengan pencapaianmu (kemudian dia menjabat tangan Ammar).

Ammar: Terima kasih. Tidak ada yang akan terjadi tanpa pertolongan Allah. Saya senang kalian berpikir demikian. Tapi saya masih harus belajar banyak.

║EXPRESSION OF COMPLIMENT & CONGRATULATION║

Answer

( Your friend gets the highest score for an English test )

Dee   : Wait....Sara!

Sara   : Yes, what’s the matter?

Dee   : I would like to say something!

Sara  : What is it about, Dee ?

Dee   : Warmest congratulations on getting the highest score for an English test this morning, Sara!  You’re really great.

Sara  : Thank you, Dee! I can’t believe that I got the highest score. I have never thought about it.

Dee   : It is caused by your effort to study hard, Sara. You're such a smart girl. Keep it up, Sara!

Sara  : Do you really think so! Thank you very much, Dee!

Dee   : You deserve it.

Explanation

CONGRATULATION  In daily, it’s familiar to congratulate someone’s success and the particular events.  Congratulation is an utterance used to express the appreciation toward someone’s achievement or praise someone’s good fortune and also used to congratulate on the other specific moments for good wishes/respecting each other.

There are variety of ways in expressing congratulation , such as :

Please , accept my warmest congratulationsI’m very proud of your success. CongratsLet me congratulate on your successWarmest congratulations on the birth of your sweet baby...Congratulations. You've got it made.Happy anniversaryHappy Birthday

Here are the responses of congratulation :

Thank you very muchThank you  so much. It’s very kind of you to  say soThank you for saying so.Thanks, I really needed itDo you really think so!How nice of you to say soNot really, thanks!Thanks.  I am glad you think so

COMPLIMENTCompliment/Pujian merupakan sebuah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk menunjukkan pujian/kekaguman atas penampilan/suatu hal lain dari orang yang baru ditemuinya pertama kali, atas sesuatu yang telah orang lakukan dan atas sesuatu yang orang miliki ditujukan untuk membangun semangat seseorang untuk lebih baik lagi ke depannya. Ini seperti rayuan tetapi sebenarnya memiliki tujuan positif.

Expression of Compliment

What a beautiful paintingThat's incredibleThat's greatYou look charmingPerfect . SuperbHow clever you areYou are so creative

Responding to compliment

Thank you very muchI'm glad you like itThanks. But it's really nothing great.

Pada umumnya, kedua ungkapan "Congratulation dan Complement" digunakan saling bersamaan melengkapi dalam mengutarakan ungkapan selamat dengan tujuan agar adanya semangat untuk bisa mempertahankan prestasi/pencapaian yang digapai.

1. Intention

Velma: What are you doing Fio?

Fio: Nothing. I am just playing a game.

Velma: Have you heard that Amy house was on fire?

Fio: Yes, I have.

Velma: Don’t you want to help him? I thought you two quite close.

Fio: I intended to help him, but he said that he is fine and he doesn’t need my help.

Velma: He really said that?

Fio: Yes, he did.

Velma: Maybe he got emotional and said that to you. Just let him alone for now.

Fio: Yes I know.

Mimin: ”Who won the football match yesterday?”

Udin : ”Our team did. We won two to one.”

Dayu: ”Congratulation. I hope you guys will be better next year.”

Dion: Did you see my father in the airport yesterday?

Dion: I bought it for him. He looks great with that.

Rina: Yes I agree Dion.
