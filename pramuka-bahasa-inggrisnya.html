10. karangan pramuka dalam bahasa inggris


“Scout is Always Ahead”




Good Morning / afternoon / evening. (liat situasi)
The honorable judges, and my beloved friends.


First of all, lets pray and thanks to our God ALLAH SWT the creator of everything in this universe for giving us a chance to gather in this place. Secondly, may peace and solutation always be given to our beloved prophet Muhammad SAW who has guided us from the darkness to the brightness, from jahiliyah era to the Islamiyah era namely Islamic religion that we love.


Thanks for the opportunity that you given to me. In this good occasion, I would like to give a short speech about ‘Scout is AlwaysAhead’.


Let us interpret the deeper that scouts should be at the forefront of every life as a pioneer and role model. Do not even run away and hide if problems come off.
We know, today's younger generation is more likely to run away from the problem and avoid the challenges of life, the result when life is no longer friends with them, then the man will be very easy to fall, stress, upset and so on.


Life is not a mathematic equation that has a definitive results, life has many mysteries and problems that could sink us. Therefore, we must have the courage to live a life full of challenges. One way is to train yourself to always be at the forefront and feel for yourself how to make contact with the problem. Putting yourself into a scout is certainly not just a mere front, but also must ready physically and mentally to accept the risk and fight for these risks.
Success or not we are is not important,because the important thing is whether we have struggled with the full power and exemplary effort or not.


Indeed, not all thing can be done by scout,but every scout can certainly be leading the field of his work. We need scouts, and need to be a scouts because we need to keep upgrading our life. So, be a scout who isalways ahead in allthings, becauseitindirectly can raise the pride of the young generation to be the scout.


Finally, I hope this speech can be useful for all of us.

Nasruminallah Wa Fathun Qareeb.

history : Indonesian Scout Movement is the name of non-formal education organization providing education scouting implemented in Indonesia. The word "Scout" is an abbreviation of Praja Muda Karana, meaning Young People who Love Work.

Scouting is a term for members of the Scout Movement, which includes; Cub Scout (7-10 years), Scout (11-15 years), Rover Scout (16-20 years) and Pandega Scouts (21-25 years). Other group members are Scoutmaster, Mainstay Scout, Scout Coach Corps, the Civil Saka Scouts, staff Kwartir and Assembly Supervisor.

Scouting is the process of education outside the school and outside the family in the form of exciting activities, fun, healthy, orderly, purposeful, practical conducted in the open with the Basic Principles and Methods of Scouting Scouting, which ultimately target the formation of character, morals and manners sublime. Scouting is scouting education system tailored to the circumstances, the interests and the development of society and the nation of Indonesia.

21. Ucapan selamat hari pramuka ke dalam bahasa inggris


Bahasa Inggris dari Selamat Hari Pramuka adalah "Happy Scout Day!"

Pembahasan

Pramuka atau Praja Muda Karana adalah organisasi yang berisi generasi muda yang ingin belajar berkarya. Pramuka sendiri merupakan organisasi pendidikan yang tergolong non-formal dan dapat diikuti dari umur 7-25 tahun. Kegiatan Pramuka mulai diadakan sejak 1961 tepatnya 9 Agustus. Namun belum resmi, sehingga akhirnya pemerintah RI meresmikan Pramuka ini pada 14 Agustus 1961. Maka, ditetapkanlah 14 Agustus sebagai Hari Pramuka Indonesia.

Pramuka juga diadakan secara global. Pertama kali pramuka dibuat oleh Robert Baden-Powell (beliau berjabat sebagai letnan dan penulis) sejak 1900-an. Tahun 1921 kegiatan ini mulai mendunia sejak pertama kali diadakan di London, Inggris. Robert Baden sendiri meninggal dunia di Nyeri, Kenya pada 1941.

Tingkat Kepramukaan berdasarkan Umur

Pramuka memiliki empat tingkat yang berdasarkan umur seseorang (dapat diikuti sejak kelas 1 SD hingga kuliah bahkan kerja), diantaranya :

Siaga (7-10 tahun)Penggalang (11-15 tahun)Penegak (16-20 tahun)Pandega (21-25 tahun)
