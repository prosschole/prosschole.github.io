How to play:

Draw a small circle on the ground. All the children name the marbles in the circle

Then all the children stand about a meter from the circle, behind a line. Alternately, toss another marble into the circle. The child whose marble is furthest from the circle may play first.

He must use the marbles that are outside the circle as "Striker" to hit the marbles in the outer circle. If it works, then I can keep every marble that gets larvae.

How to flick the marbles: join the thumb with the middle finger. Flick both fingers right at the mound.

The "Attacking" marble must remain in the circle. Otherwise, the child who has it will lose the marble.

The winner is the child who collects the most marbles or marbles.

Benefit:

Train motor skills, train thinking skills (cognitive), numeracy skills, hone social skills and train children to control emotions.
