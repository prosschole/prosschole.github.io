10. Buatlah kalimat pembuka dan penutup lomba pidato dlam bahasa inggris (tanpa unsur agama)


Answer:

In opening and closing a speech, one doesn't need quite long as in Indonesia as speech in English context is more like a brief formality, not the essential part of it. That's why there has never been a speech contest in Western countries.
The opening is basically about the introduction, while the closing is for closure obviously. Here's the example of them:

Opening:
'Welcome to the contestants of the World Cup 2018 Final Drawing, and also the guests. We would like to express our gratitude for being able to present at this earliest crucial stage of the World Cup Final. Let us begin this draw and hope you all enjoy this exciting event.'

Closing:
'That is the end of the Final Drawing today. We hope the result would satisfy everyone, especially the contestant nations. We shall see you in the final round of the World Cup Matches on 14th June 2018. Poka.'

Kelas: SMA
Mapel: English
Kategori: speech
