4. Buatlah contoh dialog opinion Buat dengan bahasa InggrisBeserta artinya ​


Jawaban:

Conversations:

Jack: Hi Jill, what are you watching there?

Jill: Oh, hi Jack, I'm watching Aladdin. Have you watched it?

Jack: Yes, I have watched it. I like Princess Jasmine, she is pretty.

Jill: That's right, I like her too and she has a tiger as her pet.

Jack: Yeah and that makes her look strong. What do you think, Jill?

Jill: I think I couldn't agree more with your opinion. By the way, What is your opinion about Aladdin?

Jack: In my opinion, Aladdin is a smart young man and he is lucky to be the master of the genie. Don't you think so, Jill?

Jill: I think so and I'd like to add that Aladdin is good looking and his monkey is so cute. Do you like the ending of the movie?

Jack: Yes, I like the ending of Aladdin movie because Aladdin is such a wise young man to set the good old genie free!

Jill: Do you think there will be another Aladdin movie?

Jack: Yes, I think there will be, but I just don't know when.

Jill: I was thinking the exact same thing. Thanks for your opinions about the movie, Jack.

Jack: You're welcome, Jill.

Penjelasan:

Jawaban merupakan materi pembelajaran Conversations (percakapan) tentang Asking & Giving Opinions (meminta dan memberikan pendapat) tentang film Aladdin dari Disney dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Percakapan:

Jack: Hai Jill, apa yang kamu tonton di sana?

Jill: Oh, hai Jack, aku sedang menonton Aladdin. Sudahkah kamu menontonnya?

Jack: Ya, aku sudah menontonnya. Aku suka Putri Jasmine, dia cantik.

Jill: Itu benar, aku juga menyukainya dan dia memiliki harimau sebagai hewan peliharaannya.

Jack: Ya dan itu membuatnya terlihat kuat. Bagaimana menurutmu, Jill?

Jill: Aku sangat setuju dengan pendapatmu. Omong-omong, Apa pendapatmu tentang Aladdin?

Jack: Menurutku, Aladdin adalah pemuda yang cerdas dan dia beruntung menjadi tuan bagi si jin. Tidakkah menurutmu begitu, Jill?

Jill: Menurutku begitu dan aku ingin menambahkan bahwa Aladdin tampan dan monyetnya sangat lucu. Kamu suka ending (akhir) filmnya?

Jack: Ya, aku suka akhir film Aladdin karena Aladdin adalah seorang pemuda yang bijaksana untuk membebaskan jin tua yang baik!

Jill: Apakah menurutmu akan ada film Aladdin lain?

Jack: Ya, menurutku akan ada, tapi aku tidak tahu kapan.

Jill: Aku memikirkan hal yang sama persis. Terima kasih atas pendapatmu tentang film ini, Jack.

Jack: Sama-sama, Jill.

Asking for Opinions:

What do you think?What is your opinion about Aladdin?Don't you think so?Do you like the ending of the movie?Do you think there will be another Aladdin movie?

Giving Opinions:

I think I couldn't agree more with your opinion.In my opinion, Aladdin is a smart young man and he is lucky to be the master of the genie.I think so and I'd like to add that Aladdin is good looking and his monkey is so cute.Yes, I like the ending of Aladdin movie because Aladdin is such a wise young man to set the good old genie free! Yes, I think there will be, but I just don't know when.I was thinking the exact same thing.

DIALOG BAHASA INGGRIS ASKING AND GIVING OPINION TENTANG PERTEMANAN

A:  Some people say friends are always honest with each other. What do you think?

B:  In my opinion friends need to be completely honest.One little lie and the friendship just disappears.

A:  Ah, that’s pretty strong language. Do you really think that?

B:  Yeah. Once, one of my good friends lied to me. He was a really good friend, and I just felt completely betrayed. It destroyed the friendship.

B: Menurut saya teman  harus benar-benar jujur. Sedikit kebohongan saja, maka persahabatan tersebut bisa hilang begitu saja.
