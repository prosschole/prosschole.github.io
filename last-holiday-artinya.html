23. cerita last holiday di bali​


First day, we visited Sanur Beach in the morning. We saw the beautiful sunrise together. It was a great scenery. Then, we checked in to the hotel. After prepared our selves, we went to Tanah Lot. We met so many other tourists there. They were not only domestic but also foreign tourists.

Second day, we enjoyed the day on Tanjung Benoa beach. We played so many water sports such as banana boat, jetsky, speedboat etc. We also went to Penyu island to see many unique animals. They were turtles, snakes, and sea birds. We were very happy. In the afternoon, we went to Kuta Beach to see the amazing sunset and enjoyed the beautiful wave.

The last day, we spent We could enjoy the green and shady forest. There were so many monkies. They were so tame but sometimes they could be naughty. We could make a close interaction with them. After that, we went to Sukowati market for shopping. That was my lovely time. I bought some Bali T-Shirt and souvenirs.

Last Holiday in Bali

On December 28 yesterday, I was on holiday in Bali. On that day I was very happy. I went to Bali using transportation in the form of an airplane. When I arrived in Bali, I was very amazed by the scenery in Bali. I saw many statues located there, many temples there, and other beauties.

At that time, I visited one of the most popular beaches in Bali. Namely Kuta beach, Bali. The scenery is very beautiful, many tourists are on this beach. However, I was very disappointed. There is a lot of garbage in the sea of ​​Kuta Beach. Certainly because we don't want to take care of the beach. And finally I took some trash and threw it in the trash. Even so, my joy did not fade. In fact, I am very happy because I have cleaned the beach even though it is only reduced. I hope, I can come back there again
