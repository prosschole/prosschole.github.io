29. karto said to Karno " you are driving me crazy" jawabannya apa ya​


Jawabannya adalah..
=> Dilihat dari kalimat direct nya menggunakan PRESENT CONTINUOUS :

S + To be (am/are/is) + V-ing + O
=> Karto Said to Karno "You ARE DRIVING me crazy".

Maka indirect nya menggunakan PAST CONTINUOUS
S + To be (was/were) + V-ing + O
=> Karto said to Karno that He WAS DRIVING him crazy.
