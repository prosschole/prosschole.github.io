20. Skuy jawab............​


panjang (p) = 2x + 4

lebar (l) = x - 1

keliling = 78 m

a. nilai x

keliling = 2p + 2l

78 = 2(2x + 4) + 2(x - 1)

78 = 4x + 8 + 2x - 2

78 = 6x + 6

6x = 78 - 6

6x = 72

x = 12

b. panjang dan lebar lahan

panjang (p) = 2x + 4 = 2(12) + 4 = 28 m

lebar (l) = x - 1 = 12 - 1 = 11 m

c. harga jual lahan

hj = luas lahan × harga per meter persegi

hj = (p × l) × 250.000

hj = (28 × 11) × 250.000

hj = 77.000.000
