26. contoh dan gambar suggest and offer?


"Offer" means "do you want this?" or "how about this?". You are offering the other person a choice to do/have something or not to do/have it.

• Do you want __?
• Would you like a ___?
• Want any of ___?

"A offers tea to B= A says to B "do you want tea?".

"A offers to drive B home = A says to B "Do you want me to drive you home?".

"Suggest" means "I think you should do this". You are offering the other person advice.

• I think it's better for you to ___
• You better ___
• You should do___

"A suggests B drink tea" = A says to B "I think you should drink tea". A is not saying "I will give you tea", but "You should drink tea". B can take this advice or not.

"A suggests B consult a lawyer" - A says to B "you should consult a lawyer". A is not offering to be a lawyer, only telling B that B should consult a lawyer. B can take this advice or leave it.Contoh:

I suggest you to buy that book.
I think you should get a haircut.
Would you like a banana?
Do you mind if I gave you my sweatshirt?

Suggest itu seperti opini yang membujuk kita melakukan, atau mendapat sesuatu, walaupun kita tidak mau.
Offer itu lebih ke pertanyaan apa kamu ingin ini atau tidak.

28. contoh percakapan untuk 2 orang suggest dan offer


Pembahasan:


Suggestion

Suggestion adalah suatu ungkapan yang digunakan untuk mengekpresikan saran atau nasihat. contoh ungkapan yang dapat digunakan untuk mengungkapkan saran adalah sebagi berikut.

- You should ...

- You ought to ...

- It's better for you to ...

- I suggest you ...

- I advise you to ...

- Why don't we/you ... ?

- How about ...?

- You had better ...

- Let's ...


Offer

Offer merupakan suatu ungkapan yang digunakan untuk menawarkan suatu bantuan atau barang. berikut contoh kalimat yang digunakan untuk mengekspresikan offer:

a. Untuk menawarkan bantuan kita dapat menggunakan ungkapan berikut:

- May I help you?

- Can I help you?

- What can I do for you?

- Would you like me to ...?

- Can I lend you a hand with that?


b. Untuk menawarkan barang kita dapat menggunakan ungkapan berikut:

- Would you like some ...?

- Would you like something to ...?

- Should I get you ...?

- What can I get for you?


Berikut contoh dialognya.

A: Hey, B!

B: Hey. What's up?

A: B, i need your help.

B: Oh? what can i do for you?

A: I want to buy a gift for my sister, but i don't know what i should buy.

B: Hmm, How old is your sister?

A: She will be turning 8 next week.

B: How about give her a doll? she must have a doll that she really want to have, right?

A: I'm not sure, I know that she likes disney princess

B: Let's go to the toy shop. we might be able to find something for your sister there.

A: You're right. Let's go.
