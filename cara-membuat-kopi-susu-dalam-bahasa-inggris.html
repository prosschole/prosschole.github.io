•How to make simple sweetened condensed milk:

- Stir the milk and sugar in the pan. Heat it over low heat, then stir until the sugar dissolves.

- After the sugar dissolves and the milk starts to warm, use medium heat and let it heat up.

- Cook the milk for 40 minutes or until the milk thickens and turns darker.

- After getting the desired thickness, lift the milk from the stove. After that lift the foam that collects on the surface with a spoon.

- Wait until the sweetened condensed milk is really cold, then put it in an airtight container.

Sweetened condensed milk like this can be stored for weeks, provided the packaging is tightly closed.

8. cara membuat kopi instan dalam bahasa inggris​


Make Instant Iced Coffee

1. Mix 2 teaspoons of instant coffee powder with 120 ml of hot water.

2. Add sugar or spices to the coffee solution if desired.

3. Add 1⁄2 cup cold water or milk to the hot coffee solution.

4. Pour the cold coffee solution over the ice.

5. Immediately serve instant iced coffee.

10. Cara membuat kopi susu dalam bahasa inggris beserta artinya.


► Kopi susu mungkin menjadi minuman yang tidak di sukai beberapa orang dan menjadi minuman yang paling disukai banyak orang umum. Kopi susu biasanya sering dinikmati ketika sedang bersantai di sore hari, bersantai bersama teman, atau sambil berangkat kerja di pagi hari kita bisa memesan milk coffee sebagai minuman yang menemani macet.

⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯

► Ingredients (bahan – bahan):2 tablespoons coffee-flavored syrup (dua sendok makan sirup rasa kopi). 1 cup cold milk (satu gelas susu dingin).

⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯

► To make your own coffee milk syrup (cara membuat sirup kopi sendiri): Brew 1/2 cup finely ground coffee with 2 cups cold water in a coffeemaker (masak kopi yang sudah di haluskan dengan 2 gelas air dinign menggunakan mesin pembuat kopi). Combine brewed coffee and 1 cup sugar in a saucepan; simmer until reduced by half, about 30 minutes (campur kopi yang di masak dengan segelas gula di dalm panci). Refrigerate until cold or up to 2 weeks (dinginkan hingga bekuk atau selama 2 minggu).

⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯

► How to make (cara membuat):How to make (cara membuat);In a large glass, add coffee syrup and milk; stir well until combined (masukkan susu dan sirup kopi kedalam sebuah cangkir berukuran besar dan kemudian di aduk). How to make (cara membuat);In a large glass, add coffee syrup and milk; stir well until combined (masukkan susu dan sirup kopi kedalam sebuah cangkir berukuran besar dan kemudian di aduk).Taste and add more coffee syrup to taste (untuk rasa yang lebih kuat kamu bisa menambahkan sirup kopi lagi). Makes 1 serving (disajikan untuk 1 gelas porsi).

16. tolong buatkan dialog procedure text tentang membuat susu atau kopi dalam bahasa inggris & terjemahannya indonesia


Chocolate coffee

Ingredients:

1.    1 tablespoon instant coffee
2.    1 tablespoon chocolate powder
3.    450 ml hot milk
4.    2 tablespoons sugar
5.    50 grams ice cubes

How to make:

1.    Prepare a glass or a cup. Put sugar, instant coffee, and chocolate powder into the glass/cup. Stir it.
2.    Add hot milk, and stir it again until mixed well.
3.    After it is cold, add ice cubes.
4.    Finally, chocolate coffee is ready to serve.

Chocolate coffee

1. 1 sendok makan kopi instan
2. 1 sendok makan cokelat bubuk
3. 450 ml susu panas
4. 2 sendok makan gula
5. 50 gram es batu

Cara membuat:

1. Siapkan gelas atau cangkir. Masukan gula, kopi instan, dan  cokelat bubuk ke dalam gelas / cangkir. Aduk itu.
2. Tambahkan susu panas, dan aduk lagi sampai tercampur dengan baik.
3. Setelah dingin, tambahkan es batu.
4. Akhirnya, kopi coklat siap disajikan.


17. Cara membuat es kopi susu ala tasya farasya dalam bahasa inggrisTolong ini soal nya buat bsok ​


Jawaban:

The Materials:

1)Black coffee powder [Kopi bubuk hitam]

2)Milk powder [Susu bubuk]

3)Sugar (may u need it) [Gula [mungkin anda membutuhkannya.]

4)Hot water [Air panas]

5)Spoon [Sendok]

6)3 Glasses [3 Gelas/cangkir]

The steps:

1)First, heat two cups of water [Pertama, panaskan dua cangkir air.]

2)Second, after the water is already hot and boiling, put a spoon of coffee powder into the first glass [Kedua, setalah airnya cukup panas, dan mendidih, masukkan satu sendok kopi bubuk kedalam gelas pertama.]

3)Then, stir until the coffee and the water is mixed [Lalu, aduk kopi dan airnya hingga bercampur.]

4)After that, add two tablespoons of milk powder into the second glass then stir it until mixed [Setelah itu, tambah dua sendok susu bubuk kedalam gelas kedua lalu aduk hingga merata.]

5)Pour the coffee and milk into the third glass [Tuangkan kopi dan susu kedalam gelas ke tiga.]

6)Stir again until the coffee and milk are completely blended [Aduk kembali hingga kopi dan susu benar-benar tercampur.]

Last, you can add a few tablespoon of sugar if you feel less sweet of coffee [Terakhir, anda dapat menambahkan sedikit gula jika anda rasa kopinya kurang manis.]

7)Your special coffee milk is ready to be served and don’t forget the fried banana ^_^ Good luck! [Kopi susu special anda sudah siap untuk disajikan dan jangan lupa pisang gorengnya ya ^_^ Semoga berhasil!]

Ingredients:
• Black coffee powder
• Sugar

How to make:

3. Strain the coffee with a soft cloth several times.
4.  Prepare a cup and pour the boiling coffee into it.

6. Stir it gently and a cup of coffee is ready to be served.
