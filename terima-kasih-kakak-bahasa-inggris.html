11. Tolong kakak abg yang baik dalam bahasa inggris sama arti nya terima kasih


Bellow are conversations about asking and giving opinion:

Buying a new car

A: I think we should buy a new car.

B: Why? Our old car is fine and functional.

A: I know, but I am bored with our car.

B: I think it's not the reason for us to buy a new car. We still need more money for our daily life. What do you think if we change our car color?

A: I think it's a good idea.

B: Alright, I will bring our car at the modification workshop.

Banning of cigarettes

A: I believe that smoking should be banned.

B: I don't think I agree with you.

A: Why?

B: Because smoking is people right.

A: That's right, but smoking is not only dangerous for people who smoked, but also for people around.

B: You're right. The solution is smoked people should smoking in the provided area.

Songs

A: What kind of music do you like?

B: I like rock and roll.

A: How can you like rock and roll? It's so loud. What do you think about pop music?

B: I like rock and roll because it makes me energic. I think pop also good, but it doesn't make me energic. How about you? What kind of music do you like?

A: I see. I like classis music because it makes me feel relax.

B: Wow, I really don't like classic music.

Pembahasan

Pada soal, kita diminta untuk melengkapi dialog menggunakan kalimat-kalimat yang berisi asking and giving opinion atau bertanya dan memberi pendapat. Pada dialog pertama merupakan percakapan tentang membeli mobil baru. Pada dialog kedua merupakan percakapan tentang merokok. Sedangkan pada dialog ketiga merupakan percakapan tentang musik kesukaan.
