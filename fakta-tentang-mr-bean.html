19. _____ mr bean like drawing mountain? ( do / does )​


Does Mr. Bean like drawing mountain?

Subjek I, you, we, they digunakan untuk kata Do

Contoh: I do my homework, You do the dishes, We do the laundry, They do the housework.

Subjek he, she, it digunakan untuk kata Does

Contoh: He does like fries, She does the housework, It does snow every winter, Lisa (she) does her nails.
