25. Yourcar/amazing/what/an/!


What an amazing your car!

Answer:

To reoder an unjumbled sentences, you need to find out the beginning of a sentence first as well as the pattern of it. The exercise above seems to be an expression of compliment. Here's the pattern:

- what + adjective + noun ( + S + V). Example: What a lovely day (we have)

- how + adjective ( S + auxiliary verb). Example: How lovely the day is!

The one in the exercise seems to be similar to the first one. So, the right answer should be.

- What an amazing car you have!   OR How amazing your car is!   but there's no proper answer if you rely on those words. 'What an amazing your car.' is not correct at all.

Kelas: SMP

Mapel: English

Kategori: expression of compliment
